Encrusted Bar

[statblock Encrusted_Bar]

Encrusted Bars are pre-Hardmode bars crafted using Encrusted Ore at a Furnace. They are used to craft mid-tier pre-hardmode equipment and weapons. Encrusted bars are considered an evil bar.
