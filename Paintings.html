[statblock Paintings]

Paintings are decorative [link furniture] items that can be placed on background [link walls]. Each one is a framed image that depicts a particular scene, usually Terraria-related. Their tooltips generally contain a person's name, apparently intended to credit each painting's artist; although the names appear to be fictional, most of them correspond to the dedicated spriter for Origins, [link chee | | Developers.html]. Paintings are highlighted by the [link Spelunker | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Spelunker_Potion] effect.
