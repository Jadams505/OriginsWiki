Toilets

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla toilets, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Toilets].

[statblock Toilets]

Toilets are decorative [link furniture] items that fulfill a [link house | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/House]'s [link comfort item | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/House#Comfort_items] requirement. In combination with a [link Work Bench | | Work_Benches.html] or a [link Table | | Tables.html], they also form minor crafting stations. Players can sit in toilets by using the Open/Activate button. [link NPCs] can sit on toilets too.
