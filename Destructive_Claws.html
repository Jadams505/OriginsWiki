Destructive Claws

[statblock Destructive_Claws]

Destructive Claws are a Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] crafted using Feral Claws and the [link Bomb Handling Device | $default | Bomb_Yeeter.html]. It increases the velocity of thrown [link explosives | $default | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] by 25% and their attack speed by 10%. Additionally, it enables auto-use for all explosive weapons.
