Plasma Phial

[statblock Plasma_Phial]

Plasma Phial is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that has a 1/2 (50%) chance of being dropped by a Purple Slime. While equipped it reduces debuff durations by half, increases life regeneration by 1, and increases the effectiveness of [link Bleeding | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bleeding] applied to the player.
