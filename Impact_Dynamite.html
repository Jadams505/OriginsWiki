Impact Dynamite

[statblock Impact_Dynamite]

The Impact Dynamite is an [link explosive | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] that can be crafted using [link Dynamite | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Dynamite] and [link Peat Moss] or purchased from the [link Demolitionist | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Demolitionist] [link NPC | | NPCs.html] after selling 150 Peat Moss to him once [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] is reached. The Impact Dynamite has an increased [link velocity | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Velocity] from regular dynamite and detonates after colliding with a tile or enemy, or after 5 seconds.
