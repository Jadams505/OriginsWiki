Nova Fragment

The Nova Fragments are a Hardmode, post-Lunatic Cultist material that is dropped from the Nova Pillar during the Lunar Events in stacks of 12-60 (24-100 in Expert Mode). Nova Fragments are used to craft Nova armor, a late-game armor set that benefits all types of explosives, as well as Nova-themed tools, weapons, wings, etc.
