Defiled Asphyxiator

The Defiled Asphyxiator is a Hardmode enemy that occasionally spawns in an Ocean overtaken by the Defiled Wastelands. The Defiled Asphyxiator will fire projectiles similar to the Defiled Amalgamation's "projectile spray" above the player, spawning falling defiled stones towards the player. If the defiled stones land on the player and crush them, they will cause the player to lose all of their breath and receive Rasterized for 3 seconds. The Asphyxiator will also attempt to intercept the player as well.

Tips

- The greatest way to counter the Defiled Asphyxiator's main attack is by placing tiles directly above to block the stones. The player should be wary that the Asphyxiator will also try to attack the player through contact as well and can float through tiles.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Defiled Asphyxiator: "The Asphyxiators are one of the Defiled's nastiest machinations, capable of wiping out anyone or anything not meant to be in its waters."
