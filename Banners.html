Banners

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla banners, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Banners_(enemy)].

[statblock Banners]

Banners are functional [link furniture] items that can be placed on the underside of [link blocks]. Most [link enemies] drop a banner based around them that can apply a [link buff | | Buffs.html] to any players within a 170x125-tile area. The buff grants players +50% (+100% in [link Expert Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Expert_Mode]) damage to the corresponding enemy and negate damage received by said enemy by 25% (50% in Expert Mode). Multiple of the same banner will not grant a stacked bonuses, but multiple different banners in the same vicinity will all take effect and consolidate into a single buff icon.
