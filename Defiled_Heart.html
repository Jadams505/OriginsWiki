The Defiled Heart is a floating collection of Defiled matter around a central, Defiled spirit. The Heart can be found deep in the [link Defiled Caverns] at the center of the all the veins it connects to. It is protected by 2 thick layers of [link Defiled Stone] which revolve around it in a spherical manner. Detroying the Heart is neccessary to stop the Defiled from spreading entirely, but cannot be destroyed until after defeating the mechanical bosses.

Consequences of Destruction

- Once all Defiled Hearts in the world are destroyed, the Defiled will not spread at all unless when spread artificially.
- Defiled enemies will no longer regenerate health.

These messages will appear if the player doesn't hit the Defiled Heart a 6th time:

- 5 seconds after: "T&ank you, [player name]. I was ex9ecting to die."

At this point, the Defiled Heart will drop the [link Mimic Armor] set for the player to use. Addtionally, '[link Defiled Protector]' will be permanently applied to the player
making Defiled enemies friendly and providing various boosts when in the [link Defiled Wastelands] until the last Defiled Heart has been destroyed. This opens up an
entirely new path for progression in the game. The [link Defiled Envoy] will now be allowed to move in with the player as well.

Lore

The Defiled Heart and Fissures

The Defiled Heart serves as the central working center for the Defiled, communicating information, generating chunks, and using oxygen for energy production.
The Defiled Heart uses up oxygen and gathered dark spirits to keep the Defiled going and growing eventually expelling out a pollutant that absorbs light in a way
to make the world appear desaturated of color. Defiled Fissures contribute by communicating with the Heart about any activity reported by nearby Defiled creatures
by sending the information with a nerve impulse signal, which travels along Defiled matter as dark magic.

Defiled Fissures also release and send Defiled spirits to paste to existing and future Defiled machinations for construction and repair.
