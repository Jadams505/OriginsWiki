Internal Combustionfish

The Internal Combustionfish is an explosive that can be fished for in the Ashen Factory. It is currently unobtainable through conventional means, however, and can only be obtained through Shimmer. When thrown, it will home to nearby enemies and stick to them and only explode after 5 seconds or when another enemy is nearby the stuck enemy. It will drop after detonation and can be infinitely reused.
