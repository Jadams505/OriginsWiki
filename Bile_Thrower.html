Bile Thrower

[statblock Bile_Thrower]

The Bile Thrower is an [link enemy | | Enemies.html] derived from a [link Penguin | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Penguin] that has been corrupted, either by the player using [link Dreadful Powder] or by a [link Blood Moon | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Blood_Moon] event on a [link Defiled | | Defiled_Wastelands.html] world. The Bile Thrower behaves similarly to its vanilla counterparts: the [link Corrupt Penguin | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Corrupt_Penguin] and [link Vicious Penguin | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Vicious_Penguin], except that it can now spit bile-like projectiles at the player to inflict [link Slow | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Slow] for 2 seconds.

Notes

- Although they spawn during the Blood Moon event, they cannot drop [link Bloody Tears | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bloody_Tear].

Trivia

- The [link Bestiary | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bestiary] entry for the Bile Thrower: "During a Blood Moon, the Defiled grows rampantly and is able to deviate from its pure forms warping more unique entities, like penguins, to its will."
