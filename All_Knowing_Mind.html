The All-Knowing Mind is a craftable [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html]. It is crafted using a [link Cybernetic Brain], some [link Eyndum Bars | $default | Eyndum_Bar.html] and an Ankh Charm. It provides an immunity to nearly all debuffs and greatly increases mana regeneration. Below is a list of the debuffs it does not provide the player immunity to:

  • 'Oozed'
  • 'The Tongue'
  • 'Cursed Inferno'
  • 'Moon Bite'
