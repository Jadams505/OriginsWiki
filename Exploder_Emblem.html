Exploder Emblem

[statblock Exploder_Emblem]

The Exploder Emblem is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] dropped by the [link Wall of Flesh | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Wall_of_Flesh] like the other emblems. When equipped, the player recieves a 5% increase in [link explosive | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] damage. For balance reasons, the damage boost provided by the Exploder Emblem is lower compared to the emblems of other classes.
