Exploder Emblem

[statblock Exploder_Emblem]

The Exploder Emblem is a Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] dropped by the Wall of Flesh like the other emblems. When equipped, the player recieves a 5% increase in [link explosive | $default | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] damage. For balance reasons, the damage boost provided by the Exploder Emblem is lower compared to the emblems of other classes.
