Mounts

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla mounts, click here.

Mounts are creatures or objects that act as vehicles that a player can use for transportation or dealing damage. When a mount-summoning item is used, it applies an unlimited buff to the player, spawns the mount and places the player on or in it. Mount movement can be controlled using the player's usual movement keys. Only one mount can be in effect at a time and will unequip when the player dies.
