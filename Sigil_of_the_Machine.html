Sigil of the Machine

Sigil of the Machine is a Hardmode accessory crafted using mech components dropped by the mechanical bosses. Equipping it transforms the player into a mech that is able to use two weapons at once with the Use item and Interact buttons respectively. Alternate use items are not able to have their secondary abilities because of this. Furthermore, the player's defense is increased by 50% and mana capacity doubled, are unaffected by fall damage but have their flight capabilities halved, and are capable of dashing by double-tapping the horizontal movement keys. Like most transformations, the player will adopt a unique appearance while this is equipped.
