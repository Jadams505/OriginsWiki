Return To Sender

[statblock Return_To_Sender]

Return To Sender is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that has a 1/2 (50%) chance of being dropped by the [link Defiled Amalgamation]. When equipped, all damaged recieved by the player through contact or a projectile is reflected in their assailant.

Notes

- Return To Sender will still deal damage to enemies upon the player's death making it possible to defeat a boss post-mortem.
- The effects of Return To Sender are stackable with the [link Thorns | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Thorns_Potion] effect.
- All of Return To Sender's upgrades have progressively decreased retaliation damage depending on how many accessories they've been combined with.
