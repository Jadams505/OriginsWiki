Return To Sender

[statblock Return_To_Sender]

Return To Sender is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that has a 1/3 (33%) chance of being dropped by the [link Defiled Amalgamation]. When equipped, all damaged recieved by the player is reflected in their assailant.

Notes

- Return To Sender will still deal damage to enemies upon the player's death making it possible to defeat a boss post-mortem.
- The effects of Return To Sender are stackable with the Thorns effect.
- All of Return To Sender's upgrades have progressively decreased retaliation damage depending on how many accessories they've been combined with.
