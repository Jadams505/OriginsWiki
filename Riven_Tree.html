Riven Tree

[statblock Riven_Tree]

Riven Trees are a variant of [link trees | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Trees] grown on [link Riven Grass] or [link Riven Jungle Grass] [link blocks] found around the [link Riven Hive]. Unlike the vanilla equivalents, Riven Trees do not have a dedicated wood item and drop [link Spug Flesh] upon destruction despite having to use an [link axe | | Axes.html] or [link chainsaw | | Chainsaws.html] to destroy it despite not having a "leafy" top. Additionally, Riven Trees have a 143/2000 (7.015%) chance to drop a [link Pawpaw] or [link Periven] in normal conditions. During the [link Halloween | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Halloween] event, these chances are slightly reduced to a 34073/500000 (6.8146%).
