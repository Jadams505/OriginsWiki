Krunch Mix is a rare (1/100, 1%) food item dropped by the [link Defiled Phantom]. Upon consumption, it gives the player '[link Well Fed | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Well_Fed]' and '[link Regeneration | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Regeneration_Potion]' for 8 minutes.

Tips

- When farming for Krunch Mix, be up high in the [link Defiled Wastelands] and make sure visible ground is limited to prompt more [link Defiled Phantoms | $default | Defiled_Phantom.html] to spawn.
