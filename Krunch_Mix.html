Krunch Mix

[statblock Krunch_Mix]

The Krunch Mix is a [link food] item that has a 1/17 (5.88%) chance of being dropped by a [link Defiled Phantom]. When consumed, it provides the [link Well Fed | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Well_Fed] and [link Regeneration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Regeneration_Potion] [link buffs | | Buffs.html], which lasts for 6 minutes.

Tips

- When farming for Krunch Mix, be up high in the [link Defiled Wastelands] and make sure visible ground is limited to prompt more [link Defiled Phantoms | | Defiled_Phantom.html] to spawn.

Trivia

- An inside joke of Krunch Mix, "A candy fair to the average white male child", was under consideration as a tooltip for some time but was changed. The
context is that Krunch Mix resembles the Defiled in appearance and was referred as "Skittles for colorblind people" and the most colorblind group of
people statisically goes to white male children, hence the phrase.
