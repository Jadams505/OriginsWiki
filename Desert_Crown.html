Desert Crown

[statblock Desert_Crown]

The Desert Crown is a pre-hardmode summon weapon that can be found in the chest of a Pyramid. It summons a sand elemental minion which attacks by approaching enemies and casting a cloud of dust at them. The Desert Crown might be preferred over its equivalents for the high knockback the sand elemental's attack applies.

Desert Crown's best modifiers are Mythical, Furious, or Godly. The Sand Elemental deals moderate damage that can benefit from damage bonuses, but its best attribute is its knockback potential which is promoted by the featured modifiers.

Tips

- The sand elemental works best in open spaces so it can fully utilize its knockback effectiveness.
- The Desert Crown is most effective when using another ranged weapon as it can keep enemies away from the player.

Trivia

- The Desert Crown is comprised of sandstone with emeralds cast inside of the structure. It was likely used as a status symbol by some powerful entity within the pyramid's civilization.
