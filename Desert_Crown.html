Desert Crown

[statblock Desert_Crown]

The Desert Crown is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link summon weapon | | Summon_Weapons.html] that can be found in the chest of a [link Pyramid | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pyramid]. It summons a sand elemental [link minion | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Summon_weapons#Minions] which attacks by approaching enemies and casting a cloud of sand at them. The Desert Crown might be preferred over its equivalents for the high [link knockback | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Knockback] the sand elemental's attack applies.

Desert Crown's best [link modifier | | Modifiers.html] is Mythical. The Sand Elemental deals moderate damage that can benefit from damage bonuses, but its best attribute is its knockback potential.

Trivia

- The Desert Crown is comprised of hardened sand with emeralds cast inside of the structure. It was likely used as a status symbol by some powerful entity within the pyramid's civilization.
