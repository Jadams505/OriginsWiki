Last Descendant

[statblock Last_Descendant]

The Last Descendant is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining the Star Veil and [link Locket Necklace]. When equipped, the player will have their damage increased by 20%, movement speed increased by 50%, length of invincibility doubled after taking damage. Furthermore, falling star projectiles will fall from the sky onto enemies after taking damage as well.
