Defiled Pylon

The Defiled Pylon is a functional furniture item that appears as a large crystal hovering above Defiled chunks. It is sold by the Defiled Effigy after obtaining enough happy in the Defiled Wastelands. It allows a player to teleport to any other pylon by pressing the Open/Activate button on them, which will open the fullscreen map, and selecting the other pylon there.
