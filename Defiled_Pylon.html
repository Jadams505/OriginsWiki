Defiled Pylon

[statblock Defiled_Pylon]

The Defiled Pylon is a functional [link furniture] item that appears as a large crystal hovering above Defiled chunks. It is sold by the [link Defiled Effigy] after obtaining enough [link happy | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/NPCs#Happiness] in the [link Defiled Wastelands]. It allows a player to teleport to any other pylon by pressing the Open/Activate button on them, which will open the fullscreen [link map | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Minimap], and selecting the other pylon there. The Defiled Pylon has certain restrictions that can prevent it from properly functioning, which are mentioned in the main article [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pylons].
