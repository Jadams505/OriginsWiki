Torn

[statblock Torn]

Torn is a [link debuff | | Debuffs.html] that is applied by most items and enemies found in the [link Riven Hive]. When applied to an NPC, they will receive more damage from the player depending on the severity of the Torn being applied. When applied to a player, they will instead have their max [link life | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Health] according to the severity applied. When the effects of Torn have elapsed, the player will have to regain all of the health lost from Torn through their own means.
