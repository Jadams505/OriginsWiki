Felnum Shock Grenade

[statblock Felnum_Shock_Grenade]

The Felnum Shock Grenade is an [link explosive | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] that can be crafted using [link Grenades | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Grenade] and [link Felnum Ore]. When the grenade explodes, any nearby enemies will be shocked by electricity from the projectile and can share the damage to other nearby enemies for the same damage.
