Felnum Shock Grenade

The Felnum Shock Grenade is an explosive that can be crafted using Grenades and Felnum Ore. When the grenade explodes, any nearby enemies will be shocked by electricity from the projectile and can share the damage to other nearby enemies for the same damage.

Tips
