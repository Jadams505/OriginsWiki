Gun Glove

The Gun Glove is a pre-Hardmode accessory sold by the Goblin Tinkerer NPC after completing his 'Lend a Hand?' quest. When equipped, it causes the player to randomly fire any bullets in their inventory while swinging their arm.

Tips

- The Gun Glove benefits from attack speed boosts.
- The most effective bullet types to use for the Gun Glove are Chlorophyte Bullets in open areas and Crystal Bullets in enclosed spaces.
