Gun Glove

[statblock Gun_Glove]

The Gun Glove is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] sold by the [link Goblin Tinkerer | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Goblin_Tinkerer] [link NPC | | NPCs.html] after completing his 'Lend a Hand?' [link quest | | Quests.html]. When equipped, it causes the player to randomly fire any [link bullets] in their inventory while swinging their arm.
