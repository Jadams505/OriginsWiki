Pincushion

[statblock Pincushion]

The Pincushion is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link tool | | Tools.html] sold by the [link Clothier | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Clothier] [link NPC | | NPCs.html] for [price 10|0|0]. It prevents tile destruction from [link explosives | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html]. While in the inventory, the Pincushion will prevent the destruction of all tiles from explosives. Pressing Open/Activate on the Pincushion in the player's inventory will toggle it between the Pincushion and Pinless Cushion, which also re-enables tile destruction from explosives. The player can press Open/Activate again to toggle the Pinless Cushion back into the Pincushion thereby toggling the effect.
