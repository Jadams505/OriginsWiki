Weapons

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla weapons, click here.

Weapons are what the player uses to progress throughout the game and are very necessary to finish it in nearly all circumstances. Origins adds a list of new weapons that can be obtained in a variety of unique ways, so players are advised to research how they can get their hands on them.
