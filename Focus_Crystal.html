Focus Crystal

[statblock Focus_Crystal]

The Focus Crystal is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] post-[link Golem | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Golem] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] crafted using a Shiny Stone and the [link Ruby Reticle]. While equipped, the player's life regeneration is boosted depending on damage the player deals, their [link critical strike | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Critical_hit] chance is increased by 15% of the current weapon's base damage, and weapon damage is increased while the player stands still.
