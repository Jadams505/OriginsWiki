Focus Crystal

[statblock Focus_Crystal]

The Focus Crystal is a Hardmode post-Golem [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] crafted using a Shiny Stone and the [link Ruby Reticle]. While equipped, the player's life regeneration is boosted depending on damage the player deals, their critical strike chance is increased by 15% of the current weapon's base damage, and weapon damage is increased while the player stands still.
