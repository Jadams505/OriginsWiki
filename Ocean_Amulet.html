Ocean Amulet

[statblock Ocean_Amulet]

The Ocean Amulet is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link summon weapon | | Summon_Weapons.html] which can be found in water chests at the bottom of [link Ocean | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Ocean]. Using the amulet summons an ocean elemental [link minion | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Summon_weapons#Minions] that attacks by either dealing contact damage or casting water at them. All attacks from the elemental inflict the [link Wet | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Wet] debuff and deal 50% more damage if attacking an enemy that already has this applied.

Ocean Amulet's best [link modifier $default | Modifiers.html] is Mythical. The Ocean Elemental's attacks are relatively weak compared to alternatives and thus only benefits from the Wet debuff applied.

Tips

- The ocean elemental moves faster when submerged in water.

Trivia

- The Ocean Amulet is unique for being the only weapon to inflict and benefit from the Wet debuff.
- The ocean elemental's sprite changes when submerged in water.
