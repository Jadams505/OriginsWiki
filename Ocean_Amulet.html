Ocean Amulet

[statblock Ocean_Amulet]

The Ocean Amulet is a pre-hardmode summon weapon which can be found in water chests at the bottom of oceans. Using the amulet summons an ocean elemental minion that attacks by either dealing contact damage or casting water at them. All attacks from the elemental inflict the Wet debuff and deal 50% more damage if attacking an enemy that already has this applied.

Forest Staff's best modifier is Mythical. The Ocean Elemental's attacks are relatively weak compared to alternatives and thus only benefits from the Wet debuff applied.

Tips

- The ocean elemental moves faster when submerged in water.

Trivia

- The Ocean Amulet is unique for being the only weapon to inflict and benefit from the Wet debuff.
- The ocean elemental's sprite changes when submerged in water.
