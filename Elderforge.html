Elderforge

The Elderforge is a giant post-Moon Lord crafting station that can be found at the center of the Fractured Isles in the Cosmos. It is solely used to obtain the endgame Eyndum items. It can only function once after the player completes the Test of the Ancients where it will output a given item depending on the ingredients put into it. Most of the ingredients used to craft its respective items are obtained from the enemies that spawn during the Test of the Ancients. A new item is guaranteed for every type of blueprint used as an ingredient, however, the cycle of items that can be output will reset once all unique items have been output.

Tips

- The player has some control over the result that can be output based on the specific amount of materials they put into the Elderforge. Do note that it will still be randomized if the larger amounts are put in, however, both the Alien Conduit and Endyean Ceramic can be limiting factors to each other.
