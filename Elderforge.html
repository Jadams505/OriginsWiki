Elderforge

[statblock Elderforge]

The Elderforge is a giant post-[link Moon Lord | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Moon_Lord] [link crafting station | | Crafting_Stations.html] that can be found at the center of the [link Fractured Isles] in [link the Cosmos]. It is solely used to obtain the endgame Eyndum items. It can only function once after the player completes the [link Test of the Ancients] where it will output a given item depending on the ingredients put into it. Most of the ingredients used to craft its respective items are obtained from the enemies that spawn during the Test of the Ancients. A new item is guaranteed for every type of blueprint used as an ingredient, however, the cycle of items that can be output will reset once all unique items have been output.
