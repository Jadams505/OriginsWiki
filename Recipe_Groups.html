Recipe groups

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla recipe groups, click here.

Recipe groups are a feature added by Origins for specific crafting recipes. Sometimes items featured in a recipe group can be Shimmer cycled into one another. See below for the details of each recipe group.

Any Evil Boss material

The 'Any Evil Boss material' recipe group was made solely for Abysswalker's Armor and the Void Lock. It includes the Shadow Scale, Tissue Sample, Undead Chunk, Riven Carapace and NE-8. Items in this recipe group can be Shimmer cycled.

Any Evil Herb

Any Evil Substance

The 'Any Evil Substance' recipe group was made for items that could flexibly use the evil substances like the Ruby Reticle and Necromancer Armor. It includes Cursed Flame, Ichor, Black Bile, Alkahest and Respyrite. Items in this recipe group can be Shimmer cycled.

Any Evil Tissue

The 'Any Evil Tissue' recipe group was made for items that could flexibly use any of the evil tissue equivalents in its recipe like the Mechanical Worm. It includes the Rotten Chunk, Vertebra, Strange String, Bud Barnacle and Biocomponent 10. Items in this recipe group can be Shimmer cycled.

Any Gem Staff

The 'Any Gem Staff' recipe group was solely made for the crafting recipe for the Bled Out Staff, Crimson Rod and Vilethorn. It includes the Amber Staff, Amethyst Staff, Diamond Staff, Emerald Staff, Ruby Staff, Sapphire Staff and Topaz Staff.

{ stations:[ 'Any Anvil' ], items:[ { result:'Crimson Rod', ingredients:[ 'Any Gem Staff', 'Crimtane Bar(10)', 'Tissue Sample(6)' ] }, { result:'Vilethorn', ingredients:[ 'Any Gem Staff', 'Demonite Bar(10)', 'Shadow Scale(6)' ] } ] }, { stations:[ 'Any Hardmode Anvil' ], items:[ { result:'Bled Out Staff', ingredients:[ 'Any Gem Staff', 'Bleeding Obsidian(6)' ] } ] }
Notes

- Defiled Sand and Silica are included in the 'Any Sand' recipe group already established by the base game.
- Endowood and Marrowick are included in the 'Any Wood' recipe group already established by the base game.
- Bileberries, Pawpaw, Periven Petrified Prickly Pearsand Prickly Pears are included in the 'Any Fruit' recipe group already established by the base game.
