Bouncy Harpoon

[statblock Bouncy_Harpoon]

The Bouncy Harpoon is a type of [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link harpoon | | Harpoon_Variants.html] used as [link ammunition] for [link harpoon guns]. The bouncy harpoon bounces on surfaces and enemies up to 17 times before being reeled in or breaking off from chain.
