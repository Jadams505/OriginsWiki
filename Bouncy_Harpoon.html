Bouncy Harpoon

[statblock Bouncy_Harpoon]

The Bouncy Harpoon is a type of pre-Hardmode harpoon used as ammunition for harpoon guns. The bouncy harpoon bounces on surfaces and enemies up to 17 times before being reeled in or breaking off from chain.
