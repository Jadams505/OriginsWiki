Bouncy Harpoon

[statblock Bouncy_Harpoon]

The Bouncy Harpoon is a type of Pre-Hardmode harpoon used as [link ammunition] for [link harpoon guns]. The bouncy harpoon bounces on surfaces and enemies up to 17 times before being reeled in or breaking off from chain.
