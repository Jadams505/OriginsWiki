Torches

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla torches, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Torches].

[statblock Torches]

Torches are [link furniture] items that produce light when [link placed | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Placement], held or dropped, and are crucial at all stages of gameplay as primary [link light sources | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Light_sources]. Torches provide light permanently, and are never extinguished. Torches can be placed on the sides or tops of most [link blocks], on [link walls], and on the sides of [link trees | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Trees] and [link Wooden Beams | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Beams]. They can also be placed on any type of [link platform | | Platforms.html]. A placed torch can be retrieved by pressing the Open/Activate key on it or by freeing it with a [link pickaxe | | Pickaxes.html] or [link drill | | Drills.html]. When the block a torch is attached to is broken, the torch will attach itself to any other block or wall directly adjacent to it. If all adjacent blocks surrounding a torch are removed (left, right, and bottom), and no wall exists behind it, it drops like any other item.

The Shadow Torch uniquely reduces the visibility around it unlike most torches.
