Blast-Resistant Plate

[statblock Blast_Resistant_Plate]

Blast-Resistant Plate is a [link Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] craftable using Adamantite Bars or Titanium Bars and Obsidian. It protects the player by reducing explosive self-damage by 10% and providing 3 Defense.
