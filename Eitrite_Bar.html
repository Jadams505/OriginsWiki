Eitrite Bar

[statblock Eitrite_Bar]

Eitrite Bars are [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link bars] crafted using [link Eitrite Ore] at a [link Adamantite/Titanium Forge | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode_Forges]. They are used to craft early hardmode equipment and weapons. Eitrite bars are an alternative to [link Adamantite | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Adamantite_Bar] and [link Titanium Bars | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Titanium_Bar].
