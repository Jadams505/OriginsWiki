Defiled Stone

[statblock Defiled_Stone]

Defiled Stone is a [link block | | Blocks.html] type found in the [link Defiled Wastelands]. It is the Defiled-equivalent to [link Stone | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Stone_Block], and can be converted to other variants with [link any thrown powder | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Thrown_Powder]. Before [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode], it is most prominent on the [link surface | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Layers#Surface] being the main material of the massive spike-like protrusions. It is also the primary material for the veins underground. Defiled Stone requires at least 65% [link pickaxe power | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pickaxe_power] or [link explosives | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] to mine.

Notes

- Defiled Stone can be purified, but will quickly reclaim lost ground. This is a part of the [link Defiled Resurgence] mechanic where the Defiled cannot be cleansed until all [link Defiled Hearts | | Defiled_Heart.html] are removed from the world.

Trivia

- For immersion reasons, the sound Defiled Stone makes when being mined and destroyed is the same sound used by Defiled creatures when hurt.
