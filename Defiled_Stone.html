Defiled Stone

[statblock Defiled_Stone]

Defiled Stone is a [link block | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Blocks] type found in Before Hardmode, it is most prominent in the lining of Defiled Chasms. It requires at least 65% [link Pickaxe power | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pickaxe_power] or [link explosives | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Explosives_(class)] to mine. Defiled Stone is the Defiled version of Stone. As with its vanilla counterparts, Defiled Stone can be converted into ordinary Stone via Purification Powder. In [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode], the spread of the Defiled Wastelands converts ordinary Stone blocks into Defiled Stone. Defiled Stone itself also spreads Defiled Wastelands to susceptible blocks nearby.
