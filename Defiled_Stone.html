Defiled Stone

{statblock { header:Defiled Stone, items:[ {image: Images/Defiled_Stone.png} ] }, { header:Statistics, items:[ { label:Pickaxe Power Required, value:65% }, { label:Placeable, value:Yes (1x1) }, { label:Use Time, value:Very fast }, { label:Max Stack, value:999 }, { label:Rarity, value:[link | Images/RareWhite.png | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Rarity] }, { label:Research, value:100 required } ] } statblock}

Defiled Stone is the primary contents of [link Defiled Wastelands] generation. It is the equivalent of Ebonstone and Crimstone, and, as such, is used to form most Defiled structures. It can be destroyed by the [link Dismantler] and its equivalents or explosives similar to its counterparts. [link Defiled Trees | $default | Defiled_Tree.html] can grow on it and through it. As the name implies, Defiled Stone is the Defiled variant of Stone.
