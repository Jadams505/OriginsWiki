Solar Panel

[statblock Solar_Panel]

The Solar Panel is a [link Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] created by putting a Band of Starpower, Sun Stone and some [link Silicon] together. The Solar Panel increases mana regeneration depending on how much sunlight the player is exposed to.
