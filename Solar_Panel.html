Solar Panel

[statblock Solar_Panel]

The Solar Panel is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] created by putting a [link Band of Starpower | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Band_of_Starpower], [link Sun Stone | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Sun_Stone] and some [link Silicon] together. The Solar Panel increases [link mana regeneration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mana_regeneration] depending on how much sunlight the player is exposed to.
