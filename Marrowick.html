Marrowick

Marrowick is a type of wood block and material dropped by Exoskeletal Trees found in the Riven Hive. Marrowick behaves the same as regular wood, but adopts a light yellow coloration with a smoother consistency. Exoskeletal Trees can be planted anew by planting Acorns on Riven Grass.
