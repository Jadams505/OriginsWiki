Laser Tag Vest

[statblock Laser_Tag_Vest]

The Laser Tag Vest is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] crafted from a [link Power Core] and some [link Rubber]. It allows players who equip it to play [link laser tag | | Laser_Tag_Event.html] and recieve no damage or [link knockback | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Knockback] from a [link Laser Tag Gun], however players with this equipped will be unable to do anything if shot by Laser Tag Gun until interacting with a [link Laser Tag Console]. Players with this equipped can assign themselves to a [link team | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Multiplayer#PvP] and interact with other players according to the rules set up at a [link Laser Tag Console].
