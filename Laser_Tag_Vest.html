Laser Tag Vest

The Laser Tag Vest is a Hardmode accessory crafted from a Power Core and some Rubber. It allows players who equip it to play laser tag and recieve no damage or knockback from a Laser Tag Gun, however players with this equipped will be unable to do anything if shot by Laser Tag Gun until interacting with a Laser Tag Console. Players with this equipped can assign themselves to a team and interact with other players according to the rules set up at a Laser Tag Console.
