Cellarkeep

[statblock Cellarkeep]

The Cellarkeep is an [link enemy | | Enemies.html] found in the [link Dungeon | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Dungeon]. They behave and look similar to their [link Angry Bones | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Angry_Bones] counterparts, except that they will throw rolling explosive barrels whenever a player is within range. The explosive barrels they throw explode when making contact with the player or a high-enough wall, dealing splash damage and destroying tiles for a considerable area.

Tips

- Unlike the strategies used to avoid Angry Bones, Cellarkeeps are capable of throwing their barrels upwards to hit the player. Because of this, it is advisable to keep your distance.

Trivia

- The [link Bestiary | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bestiary] entry for the Cellarkeep: "Unlike most of the cursed dungeon dwellers, the cellarkeep is having a good time with all the beer and rum to himself. It's gotten to the point it all goes right through him."
