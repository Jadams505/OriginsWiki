Spider Ravel

[statblock Spider_Ravel]

Spider Ravel is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is crafted using [link Cobwebs | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Cobweb], [link Spider Fangs | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Spider_Fang] and [link Ravel]. Spider Ravel turns the player into a small sphere that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Spider Ravel allows the player to latch onto walls and ceilings by pressing the corresponding key whereas Move Left is used to remain attached to a wall directly left of the player or Up is used to remain attached to a ceiling the player can touch.
