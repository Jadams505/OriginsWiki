Eccentric Stone

[statblock Eccentric_Stone]

The Eccentric Stone is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] given to the player by the Zoologist after completing her "Under the Starlight" [link quest | $default | Quests.html]. Equipping it does nothing, however, it will react and turn into a [link Spirit Shard] when the player is nearby a Dark Lamia.

The Eccentric Stone is a [link lore item | $default | Lore_Item.html] indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."
