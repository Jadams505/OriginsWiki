Equipping it does nothing, however, it will react and turn into a [link Spirit Shard] when the player is nearby a [link Dark Lamia | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Lamia].

The Eccentric Stone is a [link lore item | | Lore_Item.html] indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."
