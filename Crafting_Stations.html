Crafting stations

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla crafting stations, click here.

Crafting stations allow the player to craft various items. Entering the [link inventory | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Inventory] screen displays the "crafting window" in the lower-left, and when the player is nearby one or more crafting stations and possesses the right crafting ingredients, the bar is expanded with additional item choices. To use a crafting station, the player must be standing within 3 tiles horizontally, 3 tiles vertically upwards and 2 tiles vertically downwards of any tile the crafting station occupies.
