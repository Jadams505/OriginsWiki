Mine Flayer

[statblock Mine_Flayer]

The Mine Flayer is a Hardmode [link mine launcher | $default | Mine_Launchers.html] crafted using mech components dropped by the mechanical bosses. When this weapon is swung, it will rapidly release 9 [link mines] in a large arc at a great velocity.

Its best [link modifier | $default | Modifiers.html] is Guileless.

the released mines from being too spread out.
