Grandfather Clocks

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla grandfather clocks, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Grandfather_Clocks].

[statblock Grandfather_Clocks]

Grandfather Clocks are functional [link furniture] items which provide information about the in-game [link time | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Day_and_night_cycle]. If a player is nearby a Grandfather Clock (about 80 tiles horizontally and 50 tiles vertically), the time is displayed on the right side of the screen, underneath the [link minimap | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Minimap].
