EAC Thunder

[statblock EAC_Thunder]

The EAC Thunder is a Hardmode [link harpoon gun | $default | Harpoon_Guns.html] crafted using mech components dropped by the mechanical bosses and some [link Valkyrum Bars | $default | Valkyrum_Bar.html]. It uses [link Power Cores | $default | Power_Core.html] for ammo and fires a giant stream of electric bolts that inflict Electrified to any enemies struck by it.

Its best [link Modifier | $default | Modifiers.html] is Godly or Demonic.

Trivia

- The EAC Thunder is inspired by the Zeus heavy weapon from War Robots.
- EAC stands for "Electric Assault Cannon".
