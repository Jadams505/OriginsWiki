EAC Thunder

The EAC Thunder is a Hardmode gun crafted using mech components dropped by the mechanical bosses and some Valkyrum Bars. It uses Canisters for ammo and fires a giant stream of electric bolts that inflict Electrified to any enemies struck by it.

Its best modifier is Unreal.
