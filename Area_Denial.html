Area Denial

[statblock Area_Denial]

The Area Denial is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link magic weapon | | Magic_Weapons.html] that is obtained through the [link Broken Record]. It launches a probe to the targetted location the player selected and begins to explode in 1-second intervals, dealing considerable damage and [link knockback | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Knockback] in a large blast radius. Only one probe can be active at a time and it does not pass through terrain, and can be blocked from its destination by tiles. Once placed, the probe will remain for five minutes, similarly to the [link Crimson Rod | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Crimson_Rod].

Its best [link modifier | | Modifiers.html] is Demonic or Deadly for damage output, and Loaded for knockback and blast radius.
