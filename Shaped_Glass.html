Shaped Glass

[statblock Shaped_Glass]

The Shaped Glass is a boss-summoning consumable item that is crafted from Fiberglass Blocks. Upon use, it will summon the Fiberglass Weaver. If not used in the Fiberglass Caverns, it will do nothing and will not be consumed.
