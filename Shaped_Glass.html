Shaped Glass

[statblock Shaped_Glass]

The Shaped Glass is a boss-summoning [link consumable | | Consumables.html] item that is has a 1/35 chance of being dropped by any enemies in the [link Fiberglass Caves]. Upon use, it will summon the [link Fiberglass Weaver]. If not used in the [link Defiled Wastelands], it will do nothing and will not be consumed.
