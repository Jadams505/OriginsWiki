Crimson Hymn

[statblock Crimson_Hymn]

The Crimson Hymn is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that has a 1/2 (50%) chance of being dropped by the [link Treasure Bag | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Treasure_Bag] dropped by the [link Brain of Cthulhu | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Brain_of_Cthulhu] in [link Expert Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Expert_Mode]. When equipped, enemies struck by the player recieve more damage and are inflicted with [link Bleeding | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bleeding] for a duration that depends on how close they are to the player. Crimson Hymn's effect has a range of 50 tiles and can boost damage up to 15% and inflict Bleeding up to 10 seconds. This item shares its loot pool with the [link Brain of Confusion | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Brain_of_Confusion].
