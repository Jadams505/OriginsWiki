Crimson Hymn

[statblock Crimson_Hymn]

The Crimson Hymn is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that has a 1/2 (50%) chance of being dropped by the Treasure Bag dropped by the Brain of Cthulhu in [link Expert Mode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Expert_Mode]. When equipped, enemies permanently recieve more damage and are inflicted with Bleeding of a duration that depends on how close they are to the player. This item shares its loot pool with the Brain of Confusion.
