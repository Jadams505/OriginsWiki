Blast Armor

[statblock Blast_Armor]

Blast armor is a [link Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode], post-[link Mech | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mechanical_bosses] [link armor] set consisting of the Blast Helmet, Blast Plate and Blast Guards. In order to craft the full set the player will need 54 [link Chlorophyte Bars | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Chlorophyte_Bar], 3 [link Blast-Resistant Plates | $default | Blast_Resistant_Plate.html], 6 [link Busted Servos | $default | Busted_Servo.html], 4 [link Rotors | $default | Rotor.html] and 4 [link Power Cores | $default | Power_Core.html].

A full set of Blast armor provides the following benefits:

- +39 [link defense | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Defense]
- +10% [link generic | $default | Generic_Damage.html] [link critical strike | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Critical_hit] chance
- -60% [link self-damage | $default | Self_Damage.html]
- Immunity to [link knockback | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Knockback]
- +25% movement speed
- +2% acceleration/deceleration
- 20% jump speed
Set bonus
- -30% self-damage
- Allows the player to berserk by pressing the ability key
- Incoming damage and self-damage increases the berserk duration
- All [link explosive | $default | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] combat stats are increased by 50% for berserk duration
- The helmet changes its appearance while berserked
