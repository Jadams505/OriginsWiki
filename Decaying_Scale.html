Decaying Scale

The Decaying Scale is a Hardmode accessory that has a 1/8 (12.5%) of being dropped by a Carpalfish. When equipped, all attacks from the player inflict Toxic Shock, which has a stronger effect while Acrid Armor is also equipped.
