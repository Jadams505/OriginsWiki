The Decaying Scale is an [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] dropped by [link Scalren | $default | Scalron.html]. It provides afflicts enemies with '[link Toxic Shock]' and '[link Solvent]' with a 40% chance for either and a 16% chance for both at once. The effects of these debuffs are also boosted while [link Acrid Armor] is equipped with that being Toxic Shock's [link stun] time lasts 50% longer and Solvent's increased critical damage is raised to 60% more damage.
