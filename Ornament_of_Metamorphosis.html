Ornament of Metamorphosis

The Ornament of Metamorphosis is a Hardmode accessory combining the Moon Charm, Neptune's Shell and a Strange Feather. Equipping it will turn the player into a Merfolk when entering water or honey (at any time), Werewolf at night or a Half-Harpy when in Space. The Merfolk form replaces the Werewolf form if entering water at night and the Half-Harpy form replaces either regardless. The Ornament takes the place of the Moon Shell, but the both are still craftable for the the player, however the Celestial Shell's crafting recipe now requires the Ornament instead and is not craftable otherwise.
