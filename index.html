What is Terraria Origins?

Terraria Origins is a (planned) large content mod that seeks out to give Terraria a sensible origin story to the game in tandem with previously given lore availible.
Terraria Origins adds a variety of content throughout the entirety of the playthrough including five new [link Biomes] and three structures, an explosive-based
[link Demolitionist] class, twenty-eight [link songs] for the new bosses and biomes, and fourteen [link changes] as well as various [link recipe changes] to vanilla content, and more!

***If you have questions about the wiki, it would be best to discuss it on the official discord.
