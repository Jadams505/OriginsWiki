What is Terraria Origins?

Terraria Origins is a fairly-sized content mod that aims to follow the vanilla game style as close as possible while also providing a cohesive story to the pre-existing knowledge already provided to the player. Terraria Origins adds content spanning all of Terraria's progression with a small portion after defeating the [link Moon Lord | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Moon_Lord]. The main features of Origins include 72 new [link enemies], 9 new [link biomes] and a structure, a planned dimension; [link the Cosmos], an explosive-based [link Demolitionist | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] class, 10 [link songs | | https://soundcloud.com/user-523569744/sets/terraria-origins-official] for the new bosses and biomes, and more that you can find on this wiki.

Thank you for showing interest in our project and we hope you have had a lot of fun with it!
