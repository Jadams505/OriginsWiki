What is Terraria Origins?

Terraria Origins is a fairly-sized content mod that aims to follow the vanilla game style as close as possible while also providing a cohesive story to the pre-existing
knowledge already provided to the player. Terraria Origins adds content spanning all of Terraria's progression with a small portion after defeating Moon Lord.
The main features of Origins include seven new [link Biomes] and a structure, an explosive-based [link Demolitionist | $default | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] class, twenty-six [link songs | $default | Origins_Music.html] for the new bosses and biomes,
many [link changes] and [link recipe changes] to vanilla content, and more that you can find on this wiki page.

Thank you for showing interest in our project and we hope you have had a lot of fun with it! - Origins Team
