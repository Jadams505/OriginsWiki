What is Terraria Origins?

Terraria Origins is a fairly-sized content mod that aims to follow the vanilla game style as close as possible while also providing a cohesive story to the pre-existing knowledge already provided to the player. Terraria Origins adds content spanning all of Terraria's progression with a small portion after defeating the Moon Lord. The main features of Origins include 82 new enemies, 10 new biomes and two structures, a planned dimension; the Cosmos, an explosive-based Demolitionist class, 14 songs for all of the content, and more that you can find on this wiki.

Thank you for showing interest in our project and we hope you have had a lot of fun with it!
