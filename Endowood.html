Endowood

Endowood is a type of wood block and material dropped by Petrified Trees found in the Defiled Wastelands. Endowood behaves the same as regular wood, but adopts a black-purple coloration with white stripes. Petrified Trees can be planted anew by planting Acorns on Defiled Grass.
