Thermite Launcher

[statblock Thermite_Launcher]

The Thermite Launcher is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link launcher | | Launchers.html] dropped by the [link Wall of Flesh | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Wall_of_Flesh]. All [link ammunition] fired by this weapon are treated like [link Thermite Canisters | | Thermite_Canister.html] on top of their base stats. Using Thermite Canisters as ammunition will dramatically increase the amount of napalm released and the damage dealt from them.

Its best [link modifier | | Modifiers.html] is Guileless.
