Retaliatory Tendril

[statblock Retaliatory_Tendril]

The Retaliatory Tendril is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is obtained from the [link Treasure Bag | | Treasure_Bags#Lost_Diver] dropped by the [link Lost Diver] in [link Expert Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Expert_Mode]. While this is equipped, the all of the player's combat stats will be increased for a maximum of +50% depending on the amount of [link breath | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Breath_meter] lost relative to their max. The [link cursed | | Curses.html] version, will additionally make the player lose all of their breath when affected by high amounts of knockback.
