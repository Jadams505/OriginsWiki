Retaliatory Tendril

The Retaliatory Tendril is a Hardmode accessory that is obtained from the Treasure Bag dropped by the Lost Diver in Expert Mode. While this is equipped, the all of the player's combat stats will be increased for a maximum of +50% depending on the amount of breath lost relative to their max. The cursed version, will additionally make the player lose all of their breath when affected by high amounts of knockback.
