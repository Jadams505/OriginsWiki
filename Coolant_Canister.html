Coolant Canister

The Coolant Canister is a type of Hardmode canister used as ammunition for launchers and the Proximity Mine Launcher. Upon landing on a tile or enemy, the Coolant Canister will explode for a blast radius of 13 tiles and inflict Frostbite to any enemies within.
