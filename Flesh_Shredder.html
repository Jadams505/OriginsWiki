Flesh Shredder

The Flesh Shredder is a Hardmode crafting station used to craft furniture based on the Riven-themed Residue Block. The Flesh Shredder can be purchased from the Steampunker for [price 10|0|0] but only appears for sale in worlds that are generated with the Riven.
