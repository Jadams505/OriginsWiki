Protozoa Food

[statblock Protozoa_Food]

Protozoa Food is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that is obtained from the Treasure Bag dropped by the [link World Cracker] in [link Master Mode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Master_Mode]. While equipped, the player will generate amoeba pellets to fight for them based on how many minion slots they have without actually taking up any slots. The pellets will kamikaze into enemies and inflict Slow, and will replenish over time. Additionally, the player will gain one extra minion slot.
