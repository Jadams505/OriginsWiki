Protozoa Food

[statblock Protozoa_Food]

Protozoa Food is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is obtained from the [link Treasure Bag | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Treasure_Bag] dropped by the [link World Cracker] in [link Master Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Master_Mode]. While equipped, the player will generate amoeba pellets to fight for them based on how many [link minion slots | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Summon_weapons#Minions] they have without actually taking up any slots. The pellets will kamikaze into enemies and inflict [link Slow | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Slow], and will replenish over time. Additionally, the player will gain one extra minion slot.
