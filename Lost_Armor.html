Lost Armor

[statblock Lost_Armor]

Lost armor is an [link armor] set consisting of the Lost Monocle, Lost Cloak and Lost Pants. In order to craft the full set the player will need 42 [link Lost Ore] and 66 [link Strange String].

A full set of Lost armor provides the following benefits:

- +10 [link defense | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Defense]
- +2 [link mana | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mana] regeneration
- +15% [link magic | $default | Magic_Weapons.html] damage
- +5% movement speed
Set bonus
- +15% [link mana shielding]

Notes

- Mana shielding takes into account the damage the player would have received with 0 defense and 0% [link damage reduction]. This results in the proper amount of
damage deducted from the attack, but an inefficiency in the mana consumed from it.
- Whenever the player benefits from mana shielding, [link Defiled Exhaustion] will be applied preventing any mana regneration for 0.83 seconds.

Tips

- Lost armor can be paired with [link Refactoring Pieces] to increase its mana shielding by 10%, allowing up to 25% of damage the player receives to be redirected to their
mana.
- Lost armor pairs well with [link Magic Cuffs | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Magic_Cuffs] and reduces the amount of mana consumed from mana shielding by 66%.
