Crates

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla crates, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Crates].

[statblock Crates]

Crates are [link grab bag | | Grab_Bags.html]-type items which can be caught while [link fishing | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Fishing], containing random loot such as [link ores], [link bars], [link potions | | Consumables.html], [link accessories] and other items. In addition, [link biome | | Biomes.html]-specific crates can contain biome-specific loot. Their content can be unloaded by pressing the Open/Activate button, either on their icon in the [link inventory | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Inventory] or while holding them. Crates can also be placed.
