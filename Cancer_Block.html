Cancer Block

[statblock Cancer_Block]

The Cancer Block is a [link block | | Blocks.html] type crafted from 2 [link Defiled Stone] at a [link Meat Grinder | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Meat_Grinder]. They are used to craft Cancer [link furniture] and [link walls] at a [link Defiled Waste Organ]. Cancer Blocks will not spread the [link Defiled | | Defiled_Wastelands.html].
