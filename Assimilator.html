The Assimilator is a melee weapon based off of the Riven. It is dropped exclusively by the [link Riven Fighter]. The Assimilator has a similar range to shortswords, but attacks more like a broadsword. It also has a chance inflict 'Confused' and '[link Malice]' like many other Riven weaponry.
