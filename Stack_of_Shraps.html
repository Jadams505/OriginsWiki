Stack of Shraps

[statblock Shrap_Stack]

The Stack of Shraps is a pre-Hardmode accessory that is dropped by the Scrapper in Master Mode. While equipped, all explosive projectiles wil be duplicated.

Stack of Shraps is a lore item indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."
