Stack of Shraps

[statblock Shrap_Stack]

Stack of Shraps is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that is obtained from the Treasure Bag dropped by the [link Scrapper] in [link Master Mode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Master_Mode]. While equipped, all [link explosive | $default | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] projectiles wil be duplicated.

Stack of Shraps is a [link lore item | $default | Lore_Item.html] indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."

Tips

- When paired with [link Bleeding Obsidian Armor] or [link Nova Armor], all explosive projectiles have a chance to be tripled and are guaranteed to be doubled.
