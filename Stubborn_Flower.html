Stubborn Flower

The Stubborn Flower is a pre-Hardmode summon weapon that is crafted from Biocomponent 10, NE-8, and a Survey Sprout. It summons a Mechanical Giant minion that attacks enemies through contact damage. Uniquely, the giant also doubles as a ridable mount where the player can sit on its shoulder away from enemies and have a height advantage. Using the Flower multiple times makes Mechanical Giant stronger and faster, much like Abigail's Flower. The giant's status can be indicated by its glowing internals depending on the color of its flame.

The Stubborn Flower is a lore item indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."

Stubborn Flower's best modifier is Mythical, Ruthless, or Godly. The Mechanical Giant deals great damage to benefit from any damage bonuses.

Notes

- The Mechanical Giant is a grounded minion and does not have a ranged attack, greatly limiting its effectiveness in airborne situations. - The giant is large and cannot fit in most spaces where the player can.

Tips

- The most optimal location for the Mechanical Giant is on the surface where it has plenty of room to navigate. - Due to the Mechanical Giant's damage output, it serves as a really strong alternative to early Hardmode weapons.
