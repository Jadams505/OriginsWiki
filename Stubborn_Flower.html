Stubborn Flower

[statblock Stubborn_Flower]

The Stubborn Flower is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link summon weapon | | Summon_Weapons.html] that is crafted from [link Biocomponent 10], [link NE-8], and a [link Survey Sprout]. It summons a Mechanical Giant [link minion | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Summon_weapons#Minions] that attacks enemies through contact damage. Uniquely, the giant also doubles as a ridable mount where the player can sit on its shoulder away from enemies and have a height advantage. Using the Flower multiple times makes Mechanical Giant stronger and faster, much like [link Abigail's Flower | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Abigail%27s_Flower]. The giant's status can be indicated by its glowing internals depending on the color of its flame.

The Stubborn Flower is a [link lore item | | Lore_Item.html] indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."

Stubborn Flower's best [link modifier | | Modifiers.html] is Mythical, Ruthless, or Godly. The Mechanical Giant deals great damage to benefit from any damage bonuses.

Notes

- The Mechanical Giant is a grounded minion and does not have a ranged attack, greatly limiting its effectiveness in airborne situations. - The giant is large and cannot fit in most spaces where the player can.

Tips

- The most optimal location for the Mechanical Giant is on the surface where it has plenty of room to navigate. - Due to the Mechanical Giant's damage output, it serves as a really strong alternative to early [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] weapons.
