Playtime's Over

[statblock Playtimes_Over]

Playtime's Over is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining a [link Bug Trapper] and [link Fleshy Figurine]. When equipped, the player's [link life regeneration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Health_regeneration], [link defense | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Defense] and inflicted [link Torn] severity will be increased depending on the current severity of Torn being applied to them. On its own, however, it grants the ability for all of the player's attacks to inflict Torn while increasing the severity of inflicted Torn by 13%. The highest amount of severity the player can inflict with only Playtime's Over receiving 99% Torn severity is 25.935%. This effect is stackable with other boosts in Torn severity.
