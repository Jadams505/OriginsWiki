Candles

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla candles, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Candles].

[statblock Candles]

Candles are a type of [link furniture] and [link light source | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Light_sources] that emits light when placed on a flat surface such as [link blocks], [link platforms] and flat furniture items. They are not destroyed by [link water | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Water] and can be toggled on or off by [link mechanisms | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mechanisms].
