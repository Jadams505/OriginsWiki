Advanced Imaging is an [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] sold by the Cyborg after defeating [link Logic Brane]. It boosts the weapon velocity for all classes and an immunity to 'Confused'. It is a crafting ingredient to make the [link Sonar Visor] which is used to see in the dark.
