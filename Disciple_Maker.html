Disciple Maker

The Disciple Maker is a Hardmode post-Golem harpoon gun with a 1/15 (6.67%) chance of being dropped by a Holy Enlightener. The Disciple Maker fires large metal rods that are slightly affected by gravity and have a fast velocity. It has a small chance of recovering its ammunition.

Its best modifier is Godly or Demonic.

Trivia

- The Disciple Maker is a nod to the primary weapon used by JKM9 in Startropic Adventures.
