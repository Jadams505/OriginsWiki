Disciple Maker

[statblock Disciple_Maker]

The Disciple Maker is a Hardmode [link harpoon gun | $default | Harpoon_Guns.html] with a 1/15 (6.67%) chance of being dropped by a [link Holy Enlightener]. The Disciple Maker fires large metal rods that are slightly affected by gravity and have a fast velocity. It has a small chance of recovering its [link ammunition].

Its best [link Modifier | $default | Modifiers.html] is Godly or Demonic.

Trivia

- The Disciple Maker is a nod to the primary weapon used by JKM9 in Startropic Adventures.
