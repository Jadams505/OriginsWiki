Coologne

[statblock Coologne]

Coologne is a Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] combining Frozen Turtle Shell and [link Mysterious Spray]. When equipped, it reduces damage the player recieves by 25% and inversely increases life regeneration as the player's health gets lower. Like all of [link Mysterious Spray]'s variants, Coologne applies a unique visual effect on the player depending on their health.
