Coologne

[statblock Coologne]

Coologne is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode], [link Master Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Master_Mode]-exclusive [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining [link Frozen Turtle Shell | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Frozen_Turtle_Shell] and [link Mysterious Spray]. When equipped, it inversely increases [link life regeneration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Health_regeneration] as the player's health gets lower and applies 25% [link damage reduction | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Damage_reduction] when the player is below 50% of their max [link life | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Health]. Like all of [link Mysterious Spray]'s variants, Coologne applies a unique visual effect on the player depending on their current health.
