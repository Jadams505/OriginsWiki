Cursed Canister

The Cursed Canister is a type of Hardmode canister used as ammunition for launchers and the Proximity Mine Launcher. Upon landing on a tile or enemy, the Cursed Canister will explode into a lingering inferno, similar to Hellfire Grenades and Bombs, that inflicts Cursed Inferno to any enemies that cross it.
