Hazard Charm

The Hazard Charm is a pre-Hardmode accessory crafted with the Lava Charm, Bomb Charm and Trap Charm. It protects the player from most traps underground specifically providing a 15% reduction in self-damage, immunity to Poisoned and half of all damage from traps Underground, and a 7 second immunity to lava.
