Hazard Charm

[statblock Hazard_Charm]

The Hazard Charm is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] crafted with the Lava Charm, [link Bomb Charm] and [link Trap Charm]. It protects the player from most accidents underground specifically providing a 15% reduction in [link self-damage | | Self_Damage.html], 50% reduction and immunity to Poisoned from traps Underground, and a 7 second immunity to lava.
