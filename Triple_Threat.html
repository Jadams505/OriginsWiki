Triple Threat

The Triple Threat is a melee weapon that is crafted with Encrusted Bars. When used, it throws two additional copies of itself outward that last for a brief second before returning back to the player. The original projectile still remains out for a slightly longer period.

Its best modifier is Godly or Demonic.
