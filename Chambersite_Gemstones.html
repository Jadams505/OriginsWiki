Chambersite Gemstones

Chambersite Gemstones are a type of Gemstone block found in the Underground and Cavern layers of the world evil. When any are mined, they will drop a Chambersite gem similarly to the other types. They can be mined by any pickaxe or drill, or destroyed by explosives.
