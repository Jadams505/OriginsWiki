Weakpoint Analyzer

[statblock Weakpoint_Analyzer]

The Weakpoint Analyzer is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that is obtained from the Treasure Bag dropped by the Brain of Cthulhu in [link Master Mode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Master_Mode]. While equipped, all ranged projectiles wil be duplicated 3 times and approach their targets from random directions. Each of the 3 duplicated projectiles will have an equal chance to that of the player's ammo conservation rate to actually damage the enemy. Additionally, the Weakpoint Analyzer boosts the player's ammo conservation rate by 20%.
