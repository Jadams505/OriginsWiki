Sinks

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla sinks, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Sinks].

[statblock Sinks]

Sinks are functional [link furniture] items and [link crafting stations] that mostly serve as decorations, but can be used to craft some items that require [link water | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Water] as a crafting station. The Residue Sink counts as a source of [link Amebic Gel | | Riven_Hive.html] instead of water despite using a [link Water Bucket | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Buckets] in its recipe.
