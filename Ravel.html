Ravel

[statblock Ravel]

Ravel is a Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that has a 1/5 (20%) chance of dropping from a [link Defiled Mimic]. Ravel turns the player into a small sphere that provides slightly more speed than the player. Ravel has a different role as an accessory in that it can be upgraded and combined into different variations providing unique abilities while ravelled.
