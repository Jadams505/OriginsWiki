Ravel

[statblock Ravel]

Ravel is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that has a 1/5 (20%) chance of dropping from a [link Defiled Mimic]. Ravel turns the player into a small sphere by double-tapping the Down button, which provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Ravel has a different role as an accessory in that it can be upgraded and combined into different variations providing unique abilities while ravelled.
