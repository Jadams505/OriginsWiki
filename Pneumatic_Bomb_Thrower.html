The Pneumatic Bomb Thrower is a craftable [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] made from an [link Air Tank] and [link Bomb Handling Device | $default | Bomb_Handling_Device.html]. The PBT increases the throwing velocity of [link Demolitionist Weapons] by 80% making the reach of these weapons reasonable.

Notes

- Like its earlier-counterpart, the "True Bomb Yeeter" is also abbreviated to "PBT" for simple reference.
