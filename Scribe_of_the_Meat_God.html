Scribe of the Meat God

[statblock Scribe_Of_Meat]

Scribe of the Meat God is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is obtained from the [link Treasure Bag | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Treasure_Bag] dropped by the [link Wall of Flesh | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Wall_of_Flesh] in [link Master Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Master_Mode]. It grants the player the ability to dash by double-tapping the horizontal movemenet keys, however, the player will transform into a miniature wall of fleshh during the dash dealing great damage to anything in the way whilst being impervious to damage.
