C.F.H.E.S.

[statblock CFHES]

C.F.H.E.S. is a Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] crafted using an [link ACME Crate] and the [link Danger Barrel]. When equipped, all [link explosives | $default | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] inflict On Fire!, have a 33% reduced [link fuse time | $default | Fuse_Time.html], a 40% increase to their blast radius and can detect enemies within proximity of their blast.

The C.F.H.E.S. is one of the strongest and latest-game Demolitionist accessories, which makes it a valuable boost to the player's combat effectiveness if obtained early.
