Defiled Watcher

The Defiled Watcher is a Hardmode enemy that rarely spawns anywhere in the Defiled Wastelands. The Defiled Watcher will fly towards the player's current position in order to get close enough to attack. Its attack is similar in behavior to the Dismay inflicting Rasterized for 0.2 seconds.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Defiled Watcher: "The Watcher is a more direct way for the Defiled to look out for any foreign bodies that don't belong. Mainly, it's a way for the Defiled to prevent its heart from being destroyed by the Terrarian."
