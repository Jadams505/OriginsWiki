Cursed Crown

[statblock Cursed_Crown]

The Cursed Crown is a pre-Hardmode accessory that is dropped by King Slime in Master Mode. When equipped, the player will dawn a blue, gelatinous appearance, can float in water, are immune to fall damage, and recieve twice as much damage from the On Fire! debuff. Additionally, the player recieves the following alterations to their stats:

- -10% movement speed
- -40% acceleration/deceleration
- +75% jump speed
- +30% damage reduction
