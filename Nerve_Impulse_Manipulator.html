Nerve Impulse Manipulator

The Nerve Impulse Manipulator is a boss-summoning consumable item crafted using Dreadful Powder and Strange String. Upon use, it will summon the Defiled Amalgamation anywhere in the world. If not used in the Defiled Wastelands, it will cause the Defiled Amalgamation to automatically enter its "enraged" state when spawned.
