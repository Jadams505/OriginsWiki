Binding Book

[statblock Binding_Book]

The Binding Book is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is crafted with [link Celestial Cuffs | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Celestial_Cuffs] and [link Refactoring Pieces]. While this is equipped, the player will have 10% increased [link mana shielding], an increased [link pickup range | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Items#Pickup] for [link Stars | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Star] and an increased mana capacity by 20. Additionally, if [link Lost armor] is not equipped, the player will also recieve a 5% increase in [link damage reduction].

Notes

- The Binding Book's effect is stackable with other mana shielding boosts. - Mana shielding takes into account the damage the player would have received with 0 defense and 0% damage reduction. This results in the proper amount of
damage deducted from the attack, but an inefficiency in the mana consumed from it.
- Whenever the player benefits from mana shielding, [link Defiled Exhaustion] will be applied preventing any mana regneration for 0.83 seconds. - The Binding Book does not stack with [link Magic Cuffs | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Magic_Cuffs] or any of its variants for decreased mana consumption.
