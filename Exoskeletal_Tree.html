Exoskeletal Tree

[statblock Exoskeletal_Tree]

Exoskeletal Trees are a variant of [link trees | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Trees] grown on [link Riven Grass] or [link Riven Jungle Grass] [link blocks] found around the [link Riven Hive]. Exoskeletal trees drop [link Marrowick] as a dedicated wood item upon destruction and are chopped down using [link axes] or [link chainsaws]. Additionally, Exoskeletal Trees have a 143/2000 (7.015%) chance to drop a [link Pawpaw] or [link Periven] in normal conditions. During the [link Halloween | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Halloween] event, these chances are slightly reduced to a 34073/500000 (6.8146%).
