Dim Starlight

[statblock Dim_Starlight]

Dim Starlight is a Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] obtained by smashing a [link Defiled Fissure] in the [link Defiled Wastelands]. Equipping it causes stars to fall from enemies when critical hits are scored. It additionally generates a small amount of light.
