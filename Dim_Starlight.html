Dim Starlight

[statblock Dim_Starlight]

Dim Starlight is a pre-Hardmode accessory obtained by smashing a Defiled Fissure in the Defiled Wastelands. Equipping it causes stars to fall from enemies when critical hits are scored. It additionally generates a small amount of light.
