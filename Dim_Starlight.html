Dim Starlight

Dim Starlight is a Defiled-themed [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] obtained from smashing [link Defiled Fissures | $default | Defiled_Fissure.html]. It causes mana stars to fall from enemies when critical hits are scored. It additionally generates some light during the night.

Notes

- Dim Starlight can be considered an Defiled-alternative to the Mana Flower due to its excellent mana restoration capability.
