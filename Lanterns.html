Lanterns

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla lanterns, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Lanterns].

[statblock Lanterns]

Lanterns are a type of [link furniture] and [link light source | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Light_sources] that emits light when hung from the ceiling. Lanterns take up a 1x2-tile space from the ceiling, making them ideal in smaller areas. Most lanterns can be toggled on and off via [link wire | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Wire].
