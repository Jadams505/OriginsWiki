Respyrite

[statblock Respyrite]

Respyrite is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link material | | Materials.html] that has no current obtain method other than through [link Shimmer | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Shimmer]. Respyrite is considered an [link evil substance | | Recipes.html]. Respyrite offers nothing new like the other evil substances due to the [link Ashen Factory] and its content being planned for a later update of Origins.
