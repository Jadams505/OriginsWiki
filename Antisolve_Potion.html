Antisolve Potion

The Antisolve Potion is a buff potion that can be crafted using Bottled Brine, Brineglow and Peat Moss. When consumed, it provides the Unsolvable buff, which provides immunity to the Toxic Shock and Torn debuffs. This effect will last for 8 minutes, but can be canceled anytime by right-clicking the icon.

Tips

- A safe way to obtain the Antisolve Potion without dying is to use a projectile to cut off any Brineglow vines exposed from the drop-off just before the Brine Pool, drop down in the water with a Recall Potion or Magic Mirror to pick up the fallen Brineglow, and then simply craft it afterwards. It is advisable to collect the Bottled Brine using platforms and Peat Moss from some enemies found in the Underground Jungle before returning back home to save time.
