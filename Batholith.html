Batholith

"Patiently awaiting its unearthment..."

[statblock Batholith]

Batholith is a [link block | | Blocks.html] type found encompassing the [link Cubekon Temple] and at deep depths just before [link the Underworld | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/The_Underworld]. Despite being a variant of the [link Stone Block | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Stone_Block], it cannot be converted. Batholith is the hardest-to-destroy tile in the Terraria [link world | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/World] as it can only be mined with a [link pickaxe power | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pickaxe_power] of 235%.

Tips

- Use a [link Spelunker Potion | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Spelunker_Potion] or [link Sonic Radar] when mining at deeper depths as they will identify Batholith with a red glow.

Trivia

- In earlier parts of Terraria Origins' development, the [link Cubekon Temple] was actually intended to be comprised of a block known as "Cubekon Brick" until it was
decided that it be made of a more natural composition.
