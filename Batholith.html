Batholith

"Patiently awaiting its unearthment..."

Batholith is a block type found encompassing the Cubekon Temple and at deep depths just before the Underworld. Despite being a variant of the Stone Block, it cannot be converted. Batholith is the hardest-to-destroy tile in the Terraria world as it can only be mined with a pickaxe power of 235%.

Tips

- Use a Spelunker Potion or Sonic Radar when mining at deeper depths as they will identify Batholith with a red glow.

Trivia

- In earlier parts of Terraria Origins' development, the Cubekon Temple was actually intended to be comprised of a block known as "Cubekon Brick" until it was decided that it be made of a more natural composition.
