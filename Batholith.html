Batholith

"Patiently awaiting its unearthment..."

Batholith is a naturally-spawning tile featured in the deeper parts of the Cavern Layer in the world. It is very hard to remove and often serves as a blocker
to prevent players from digging straight down into the Underworld.

Batholith can be commonly found as the primary material for [link Cubekon Temple]
generation. It can also be found in patches that appear more often the deeper the player goes.

Notes

- Batholith is definitely one of the strongest tiles in the game as it cannot be broken by many Post-Moon Lord pickaxes.

Tips

- When mining towards the Underworld and you happen to encounter a patch of Batholith, it would be best to mine around the patch and continue going deeper.

- Use a Spelunker Potion or [link Sonic Radar] when mining at deeper depths as they will identify Batholith with a red glow.

Trivia

- In earlier parts of Terraria Origin's development, the Cubekon Temple was actually intended to be comprised of a material known as "Cubekon Brick"
until it was decided that it be made of Batholith instead.
