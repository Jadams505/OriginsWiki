Compacted Scrap

Compacted Scrap is a block type found in the Ashen Factory. It is the Ashen-equivalent to Stone, and cannot be converted to other variants with any thrown powder unlike the other five types. Compacted Scrap is additionally not capable of spreading to adjacent tiles. Thus, Compacted Scrap does not behave like a conventional soil despite classifying as one. The only way to remove Compacted Scrap is by mining it with a pickaxe or drill, or by destroying it with an explosive.
