Bars

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla bars, click here.

Bars are [link materials] from mined [link ores] at their respective [link crafting stations]. Crafting most ore-related items requires that raw ores are first crafted into bars. Most bars are crafted, however, some are obtained by other means.
