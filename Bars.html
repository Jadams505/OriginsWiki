Bars

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla bars, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bars].

Bars are [link materials] from mined [link ores] at their respective [link crafting stations | | Crafting_Stations.html]. Crafting most ore-related items requires that raw ores are first crafted into bars. Most bars are crafted, however, some are obtained by other means. They can additionally be placed and used as decorative [link furniture | | Furniture.html].
