Tools

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla tools, click here.

Tools are an item used to obtain items by interacting with the Terraria world or provide some other utility; as opposed to using weapons to slay enemies. Tools include pickaxes, axes and hammers with tile-destructive explosives also counting as tools as well. Other tools can provide other benefits just by being present in the player's inventory such as the Pincushion.
