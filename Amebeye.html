Amebeye

[statblock Amebeye]

The Amebeye is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link enemy | | Enemies.html] that occasionally spawns in the [link Riven Hive]. It will slowly approach the player from the edge of the screen and will have considerable [link defense | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Defense] until it enters its "enraged" state. Once the player draws near, the Amebeye will fire a bubble at the player in an attempt to capture them that instills [link Riven Assimilation | | Assimilation.html] on them until it disappears. The Amebeye will enter its enraged state and lower its defense, charging at the player for a swift kill.

Tips

- The Amebeye moves very slowly towards the player, but can float through walls. The greatest way to eliminate it is by using weapons with high base damage and/or [link armor penetration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Defense#Armor_penetration]. If none of the weapons are strong enough to attack it, simply avoid it by out manuevering it.

Trivia

- The [link Bestiary | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bestiary] entry for the Amebeye: "Amebeyes are the next evolutionary step for Riven Protoforms ever since the Riven's expansion. They safeguard the hives to eliminate any threats to the ecosystem."
