Power Core

[statblock Power_Core]

Power Cores are a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link material | | Materials.html] that is dropped by the [link mechanical bosses | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mechanical_bosses] in amounts of 1-3, or can be crafted using [link Hallowed Bars | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hallowed_Bar] and [link Eitrite Bars | | Eitrite_Bar.html]. It is the rare mech component needed to craft a lot of mechanical-themed items from the [link Fabricator]. Most of the stronger mech-themed items require 2 Power Cores in their recipes while the lesser items can require one or even none. Power Cores are also used as a type of [link ammunition] for items like the [link Nova Starforge] or [link EAC Thunder].
