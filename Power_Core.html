Power Core

Power Cores are a Hardmode material that is dropped by the mechanical bosses in amounts of 1-3, or can be crafted using Hallowed Bars and Eitrite Bars. It is the rare mech component needed to craft a lot of mechanical-themed items from the Fabricator. Most of the stronger mech-themed items require 2 Power Cores in their recipes while the lesser items can require one or even none. Power Cores are also used as a type of ammunition for items like the Nova Starforge or EAC Thunder.
