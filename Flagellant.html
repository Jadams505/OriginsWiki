Flagellant

The Flagellant is an enemy that appears in the Riven Hive. It behaves similarly to normal Jellyfish.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Flagellant: "A gentle swimmer in the amoeba-infested waters. It is flimsy and fragile, so travelers should be weary when approaching it."
- The Flagellant was an enemy introduced in the early rendition of the Riven. Its current sprite is nearly a full recolor of the original sprite.
