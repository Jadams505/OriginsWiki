Fiberglass Dagger

[statblock Fiberglass_Dagger]

The Fiberglass Dagger is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is obtained from the Treasure Bag dropped by the [link Fiberglass Weaver] in [link Expert Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Expert_Mode]. Equipping it increases weapon damage by 8, but reduces the player's defense by 8. Most rapid-firing weapons like the [link Blotopus] will not receive the full flat damage increase for balancing reasons.
