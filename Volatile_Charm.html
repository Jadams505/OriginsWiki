[statblock Volatile_Charm]

The Volatile Charm is a Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] combining a [link Bomb Charm] with a [link Nitro Crate]. It increases [link explosive | $default | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] blast radii by 40% while providing a 20% reduction to any explosive [link self-damage | $default | Self_Damage.html].
