The Air Pack is an [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that is made when combining two [link Air Tanks | $default | Air_Tank.html] at a Tinkerer's Workshop. It is another accessory that provides extra breath and can be paired with other diving equipment to get up to 4 and a half minutes of breath.
