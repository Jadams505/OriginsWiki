Abysswalker's Armor

Abysswalker's armor is a Hardmode armor set consisting of the Abysswalker's Hood, Abysswalker's Cloak and Abysswalker's Greaves. In order to craft the full set the player will need 12 Souls of Night, 48 of any evil herb, 78 of any evil tissue, 90 of any evil boss material and 60 of any evil substance. Wearing all pieces of the set displays a UI element next to the player's armor slots where they can select from 16 unique abilities. These abilities require a certain amount of assimilation from their respective world-evils in order to be unlocked, pushing the player further into their risks.

A full set of Abysswalker's armor provides the following benefits:

- +24 defense
- +20 max life
- +2 HP/s life regeneration
- +15% movement speed
Set bonus
- +50% max assimilation
- Gain different abilities depending on the assimilation amount for each world evil

Abilities

Threshold 1

Rot
Requires 30% Corrupt assimilation
- All attacks have a chance to inflict Cursed Inferno and Shadowflame simultaneously

Bleed
Requires 24% Crimson assimilation
- All attacks have a chance to inflict Bleeding or Ichor. Always applies Ichor if using a weapon or accessory that inflicts Bleeding

DEPRESS
Requires 15% Defiled assimilation
- All attacks inflict Weak and a prompt Rasterized simultaneously. Doubled Rasterized duration if under the effect of a Bile Flask

Dissolve
Requires 10% Riven assimilation
- All attacks have a chance to inflict Torn. Torn severity increased by 7% if using a weapon or accessory that already inflicts Torn

Threshold 2

Natural Hunter
Requires 50% Corrupt assimilation
- A clinger tethered to you pulls enemies toward you, dealing damage

Goreweaver
Requires 42% Crimson assimilation
- Release a gooey residue as you move that increases the damage of friendly projectiles that pass through it by 35%

EVOLVE
Requires 45% Defiled assimilation
- A Defiled Monolith will follow you and consume your mana to ward off enemies or deflect 45% of damage received to mana

Symbiosis
Requires 25% Riven assimilation
- Bud barnacles grow on your back providing 15% increased damage reduction, an increase of your max minions by 2, and summons any Riven-related summon to fight for you at no cost

Threshold 3

Resilience
Requires 70% Corrupt assimilation
- Defense increased by 6 and damage reduction by 10%

Mesmer
Requires 66% Crimson assimilation
- 20% chance to dodge an attack. Stackable with other items

BROADCAST
Requires 65% Defiled assimilation
- All enemies within a 20 tile radius of the player are inflicted with Rasterized. Stackable only once with the Lousy Liver or its variants

Bulwark
Requires 50% Riven assimilation
- Gain a protective shell able to absorb all damage for a max of 200 damage before needing to regrow for 2 minutes
- Afflicted torn severity is reduced by 33% its original value

Threshold 4

Decay
Requires 90% Corrupt assimilation
- All enemies within a 50 tile radius of the player lose 2.1% of their max life each second

Bloodlust
Requires 86% Crimson assimilation
- Replenish 4 health every second for each bleeding enemy within 40 tiles of you

INTERCONNECT
Requires 95% Defiled assimilation
- Defiled spikes will attack any enemies within 8 tiles from you. All attacks and destroyed projectiles prompt an Infusion explosion dealing 1/5 of the weapon's damage and benefit from any explosive boosts

Split Apart
Requires 75% Riven assimilation
- Gain 50% Torn severity for 7 seconds after striking an enemy that goes on cooldown for 9 seconds after

Notes

- The player will still experience all of the negative effects of each assimilation type while this armor set is equipped.
- Only one ability of each threshold can be enabled at a time.
