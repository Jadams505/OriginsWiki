Mojo Injection

[statblock Mojo_Injection]

Mojo Injection is a [link permanent booster | | Consumables.html] that is vital for handling [link Assimilation] inflicted onto the player by the [link world evils | | Evil_Biomes.html]. It is obtained by [link shimmering | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Shimmer] a [link Nature's Gift | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Nature%27s_Gift], and can only be used once per player. When used, the player's assimilation will deplete at a rate of 0.2664% each second, meaning it takes 6 minutes and 12 seconds to fully recover from being 99% assimilated. Like all sources of assimilation management, all types of assimilation are affected by the full amount of healing rather than being divided amongst each other.
