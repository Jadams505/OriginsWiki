Mojo Injection

[statblock Mojo_Injection]

Mojo Injection is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is vital for handling [link Assimilation] inflicted onto the player by the [link world evils | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/World_evil]. It is sold by the [link Dryad | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Dryad] [link NPC | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/NPCs] at all times. When equipped, Mojo Injection reduces all assimilation types at a rate of 0.2664% each second meaning it takes 6 minutes and 12 seconds to fully recover from being 99% assimilated. Like all sources of assimilation management, all types of assimilation are affected by the full amount of healing rather than being divided amongst each other.
