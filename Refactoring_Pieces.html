Refactoring Pieces

[statblock Reshaping_Chunk]

Refactoring Pieces is a Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that is obtained from the Treasure Bag dropped by the [link Defiled Amalgamation] in Expert Mode. When this is equipped with [link Lost Armor], its set bonus will be increased to 25%. However, if Lost Armor is not equipped, the Pieces will instead increase the player's damage reduction by 5%.
