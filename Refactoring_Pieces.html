Refactoring Pieces

[statblock Refactoring_Pieces]

Refactoring Pieces is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is obtained from the [link Treasure Bag | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Treasure_Bag] dropped by the [link Defiled Amalgamation] in [link Expert Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Expert_Mode]. While this is equipped, the player will have 10% of damage they recieve redirected to [link mana | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mana]. Additionally, if [link Lost armor] is not equipped, the player will also recieve a 5% increase in [link damage reduction].

Notes

- Refactoring Pieces' effect is stackable with other mana shielding boosts. - Mana shielding takes into account the damage the player would have received with 0 defense and 0% damage reduction. This results in the proper amount of
damage deducted from the attack, but an inefficiency in the mana consumed from it.
- Whenever the player benefits from mana shielding, [link Defiled Exhaustion] will be applied preventing any mana regneration for 0.83 seconds.

Tips

- Refactoring Pieces can be paired with Lost armor to increase its mana shielding by 10%, allowing up to 25% of damage the player receives to be redirected to their
mana.
