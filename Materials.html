Materials

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla crafting materials, click here.

Materials are [link items | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Items] used to craft one or more other items. This is usually done by combining them with other crafting materials. The [link Guide | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Guide] can help the player identify items that could be used as a material.
