Silicon

[statblock Silicon]

Silicon is a type of ore block found in the Underground and Cavern layers. It is the one of three ores that do not need to be turned into bars in order to utilize as a crafting material. Silicon requires at least 35% pickaxe power or explosives to mine. It is used for some advanced crafting recipes with the introduction of some more technology-based items.
