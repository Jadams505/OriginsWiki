Tearracuda

Tearracuda are a type of fish which are found uncommonly by fishing in the Riven Hive in any layer. It is the Riven-equivalent to the Corruption's Ebonkoi or the Crimson's Hemopiranha. They are used to craft Seafood Dinners and Protean Potions.
