Amoebic Slime

The Amoebic Slime is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Riven Hive. The Amoebic Slime behaves like other slime except with the ability to apply the Torn debuff.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Amoebic Slime: "With its viscosity unchanged, it behaves like most slimes... the only difference is that it tends to easily digest whatever it absorbs."
