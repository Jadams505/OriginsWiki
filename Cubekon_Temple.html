"The antithesis of any god, a society of which possesses god-like qualities through the establishment of its technology."

- The Cubekon Temple is an artificial, temple-like structure added in Terraria Origins. It is a Post-Moon Lord biome that contains a fair amount of end-game
crafting materials, 3 unique enemies, an unlockable Town NPC, and many equipment. The temple is very difficult to navigate as enemies will attempt to prevent the
player from navigating further into it. It can be usually located adjacent to Marble Caves and is comprised of Batholith, a durable and ancient rocky material.
Traversal through the temple will prove to be very difficult with the numerous traps and enemies that are housed within as well as the ambient conditions that are
present without proper equipment.

Notes

- The Cubekon Temple will always generate adjacent to any Marble Cave.

- A player is considered to be in the Cubekon Temple when surrounded by a minimum of 200 Batholith.

- The player will be affected by the 'Ionizing Radiation' debuff as soon as they enter the Cubekon Temple, making it unsafe to travel long into the temple without
protection. Wearing fully-equipped Lead Armor can reduce the effects of 'Ionizing Radiation' making the total time the player has to explore longer.

- The Cubekon Temple's gate will open once the player has defeated 'Moon Lord', which is indicated by the message "The ground rumbles from a giant hiss below...".

- Cuboids can only spawn ontop of Batholith when in the Cubekon Temple.

Tips

- The player should prepare before entering the Cubekon Temple when farming for materials necessary for most end-game recipes.

Trivia

- Despite being ancient stone, Batholith cannot be destroyed by explosives and can only be broken by pickaxes obtained post-Quantux.

Lore

Cuboids and the Cubekon Temple

- The temple can be best described as a tomb that was earthed long ago due to the catastrophic events that occured in Terraria's past. The temple's inhabitants,
known as the 'Cuboids' adopt a rectangular anatomy consistent with their naming conventions. The Cuboids are an entirely mechanical alien race that ultimately seek
out the mass collection of information throughout the universe. They are not known to be hostile unless when a foreign entity has entered their premises, the
Cubekon Temple. In this case, the temple is locked behind a giant gate that requires an entity to possess an incredible amount of energy in order to open. No
entity can break through the temple through any means, but in the event that they could, the temple's inhabitants would surely destroy said intruder. Simply put,
any entity without enough energy (of any kind) in their possession will not be admitted into the temple, but any entity that is not Cubekon in origin will be
destroyed.

- But... why would the Cubekons want to keep out any intruders if their core goal was to learn more about Terraria? It is because the Cuboids are
protecting something...

- The Cuboids seem to be a more advanced civilization beyond the capabilities of the Lihzard and Martians alike. Their final technological goal is their
eventual transformation into a singularity, namely Quantux. Quantux is the final collection of all information the Cuboids' have gathered, however,
Quantux is still in an incomplete state due to the lack of materials and energy necessary to complete it. Due to this, Quantux is in a weakened state
and cannot function properly.
