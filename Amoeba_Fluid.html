Amoeba Fluid

[statblock Amoeba_Fluid]

Amoeba Fluid is a [link block | | Blocks.html] type found in the [link Riven Hive]. It is often used to block passages and arc across from floating structures within the Hive to block the player. Amoeba Fluid is translucent and glows in sync with the rest of the Hive. It allows the player to bounce off of it when jumped on.
