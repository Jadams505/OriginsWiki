Tripod Nip

[statblock Tripod_Nip]

Tripod Nip is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that has a 1/87 (1.15%) chance of being dropped by a [link Defiled Tripod]. While equipped, enemy aggression and spawn rates are decreased. Additionally, Defiled Tripods will be friendly to the player holding this item.

Tripod Nip is a [link lore item | | Lore_Item.html] indicated by "You hear chittering nearby..." unlike similar items which are indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."

Trivia

- The existence of Tripod Nip inquires that Defiled Tripod behavior is inherently cat-like. This is reinforced by its lore entry.
