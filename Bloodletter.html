Bloodletter

[statblock Bloodletter]

The Bloodletter is a Pre-Hardmode [link harpoon gun | $default | Harpoon_Guns.html] crafted with Crimtane Bars and a [link Harpoon Gun]. Enemies struck by harpoons fired by this weapon are inflicted with Bleeding for 5 seconds. Additionally, any harpoons fired by the Bloodletter will home in on bleeding enemies.

Its best [link Modifier | $default | Modifiers.html] is Godly or Demonic.
