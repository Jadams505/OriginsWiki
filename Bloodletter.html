Bloodletter

[statblock Bloodletter]

The Bloodletter is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link harpoon gun | | Harpoon_Guns.html] crafted with [link Crimtane Bars | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Crimtane_Bar] and a [link Harpoon Gun]. Enemies struck by harpoons fired by this weapon are inflicted with [link Bleeding | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bleeding] for 5 seconds. Additionally, any harpoons fired by the Bloodletter will home in on bleeding enemies.

Its best [link modifier | | Modifiers.html] is Godly or Demonic.
