Carburite

Carburite is a type of ore block found in the Underground and Cavern layers. It is one of three ores that do not need to be turned into bars in order to utilize as a crafting material. Carburite requires at least 55% pickaxe power or explosives to mine. It is used as a supplement to many materials, most notably gems. It can additionally be dropped by shaking gem trees.
