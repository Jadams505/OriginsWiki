Brainy Staff

[statblock Brainy_Staff]

The Brainy Staff is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link summon weapon | | Summon_Weapons.html] that has a 1/17 (5.88%) chance of being dropped by [link Crimbrains | | Crimbrain]. It summons a friendly Crimbrain [link minion | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Summon_weapons#Minions] that charges at enemies with a 10% chance of inflicting [link Confused | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Confused] for 1 second.

Brainy Staff's best [link modifer | | Modifiers.html] is Mythical.
