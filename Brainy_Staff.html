Brainy Staff

[statblock Brainy_Staff]

The Brainy Staff is a Pre-Hardmode [link summon weapon | $default | Summon_Weapons.html] that has a 1/5 (20%) chance of being dropped by a [link Crimbrain] found rarely in the Crimson biome. It summons a friendly Crimbrain minion that attacks enemies through contact damage with a 10% chance of inflicting the Confused debuff for 1 second.

Brainy Staff's best [link modifer | $default | Modifiers.html] is Mythical.

Tips

- The best way to use the Brainy Staff is when the player has access to multiple minion slots as each individual crimbrain has a chance to inflict Confused.

Trivia

- Despite appearing with the same consistency as other Crimtane equipment, it is not craftable unlike the others.
