Priority Mail

[statblock Priority_Mail]

Priority Mail is a Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] crafted using a Book, Fallen Stars, Paper Airplanes and an [link Asylum Whistle]. While equipped, enemies recently struck by a teammate recieve 18% more damage from the player. This accessory is only effective in multiplayer worlds.
