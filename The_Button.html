The Button

The Button is a boss-summoning consumable item sold by the Cubekon Tinkerer NPC for [price 2|0|0|0] after defeating I.C.A.R.U.S. once. Upon use anywhere in the world, it will summon I.C.A.R.U.S. and will not be consumed. It will do nothing if there is an I.C.A.R.U.S. unit already active.
