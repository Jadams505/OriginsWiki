Seal of Cinders

[statblock Cinder_Seal]

Seal of Cinders is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that has a 1/5 (20%) chance of being given to the player when using a [link Broken Record]. The Seal releases a cloud of cinders that trail the player, eventually exploding after a short time. The Seal of Cinders is one of the few secret items the player can obtain.
