Seal of Cinders

Seal of Cinders is a pre-Hardmode accessory that has a 1/5 (20%) chance of being given to the player when using a Broken Record. The Seal releases a cloud of cinders that trail the player, eventually exploding after a short time. The Seal of Cinders is one of the few secret items the player can obtain.
