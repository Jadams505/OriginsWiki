Abrasion Blaster

[statblock Abrasion_Blaster]

The Abrasion Blaster is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link launcher | | Launchers.html] that is crafted using [link Biocomponent 10] and [link Scrap]. The Abrasion Blaster fires charged energy blasts with their explosive properties dependent on how long the player holds the charge. Additionally, one [link Scrap] will be removed from the player's [link inventory | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Inventory] for every second of charge for a maximum of 7 seconds. The Abrasion Blaster will inflict [link self-damage | | Self_Damage.html] for the final 5 charges and destroy tiles in the last 2.

The best [link modifier | | Modifiers.html] is Guileless.
