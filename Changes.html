Changes to Vanilla Content

Origins makes some changes to pre-existing content in order to add more options, encourage a natural progression of added Origins content, and improve
sensibility with the lore. These changes may also touch up on the rather neglected areas of Terraria as a whole for further diversity. It is additionally worth noting
that more changes may be implemented in the future as issues are addressed and when necessary for further development.

Obsidian Armor - Obsidian Armor is now craftable using the [link Undead Chunk] or [link Riven Sample] [link crafting materials].

Harpoon - With interest of a new Ranger sub-class idea, the Harpoon Gunner, we wanted to change the fundamental characteristics of the Harpoon weapon.
So we instead turned the Harpoon weapon into a consumable, throwing item that acted as ammunition for [link Harpoon Gun]-type weapons. Here is the new [link Harpoon] entry.

Trees - All trees besides Defiled Trees now drop [link Bark] and [link Tree Sap], which both can be used to craft rubber. Tree Sap can additionally be used to craft some food items.

Entities

Bats - All bats now drop the [link Bat Hide] material, which can be crafted into Leather. For some reason, this is the only pre-hardmode animal that can drop hides.

Wall of Flesh - The Wall of Flesh now has a chance to drop the [link Exploder Emblem] accessory for the introduction of the new [link Demolitionist Weapons].

Eater of Worlds - With the addition of the [link Demolitionist Weapons], it was necessary to reduce the damage reduction given to the Eater of Worlds in Expert-mode to explosives. The Eater of Worlds' damage
reduction was reduced from 80% to 35% in this regard.
