Changes to Vanilla Content

Origins makes some changes to pre-existing content in order to add more options, encourage a natural progression of added Origins content and
to improve sensibility with the lore. These changes may also touch up on the rather neglected areas of Terraria for further diversity. It is additionally
worth noting that more changes may be implemented or removed in the future as issues are addressed and when necessary for further development.

If you are looking for recipe changes to vanilla items click [link here | $default | Recipe_Changes.html].

Equipment

Harpoon - With interest of a new Ranger sub-class idea, we wanted to change the fundamental characteristics of the Harpoon weapon. So, we instead turned the
Harpoon weapon into a consumable item that acted as [link ammunition] for [link harpoon guns]. Here is the new [link Harpoon] entry.

Post-[link Mech | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mechanical_bosses] Equipment - Due to a desired change in progression, most post-mech [link Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] equipment is now crafted from the [link Fabricator] crafting station
instead.

Proximity Mine Launcher - With the introduction of the [link Resizable Mines], we thought it would make more sense if the Proximity Mine Launcher used them as
ammo instead of [link rockets | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Rockets].

Tiles

[link Trees | $default | Plants.html] - All trees now drop [link Bark] and [link Tree Sap], which both can be used to craft [link Rubber]. Tree Sap can additionally be used to craft some [link food | $default | Food.html]
items.

Entities

[link Bats | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bats] - All bats now drop the [link Bat Hide] material, which can be crafted into [link Leather | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Leather].

[link Demolitionist | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Demolitionist] - The Demolitionist now sells [link Explosive Powder | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Explosive_Powder] at anytime rather than solely in [link hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode]. It does not interfere with the obtainability of
[link Exploding Bullets | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Exploding_Bullet] and the change allows for more [link explosive weapons | $default | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] to be crafted.

[link Eater of Worlds | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Eater_of_Worlds] - With the addition of the [link Demolitionist weapons], it was necessary to reduce the damage reduction given to the Eater of Worlds in [link Expert Mode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Expert_Mode]
to explosives. The Eater of Worlds' damage reduction is reduced to 50% in Expert Mode and remains at 80% in [link Master Mode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Master_Mode].

Boss Immunities - The [link stun] mechanics provided by vanilla's [link Heart Arrows | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Heart_Arrow], and Origins' [link Rasterized] and [link Toxic Shock] debuffs, we felt it was proper to give every
[link boss | $default | Bosses.html] immunity to this mechanic entirely for balancing reasons.
