Changes to Vanilla Content

Origins makes some changes to pre-existing content in order to add more options, encourage a natural progression of added Origins content, and
improve sensibility with the lore. These changes may also touch up on the rather neglected areas of Terraria as a whole for further diversity. It is
additionally worth noting that more changes may be implemented in the future as issues are addressed and when necessary for further
development.

If you are looking for recipe changes to vanilla items click [link here | $default | Recipe_Changes.html]

Equipment

Harpoon - With interest of a new Ranger sub-class idea, the Harpoon Gunner, we wanted to change the fundamental characteristics of the Harpoon weapon.
So we instead turned the Harpoon weapon into a consumable, throwing item that acted as ammunition for [link Harpoon Gun]-type weapons. Here is the new [link Harpoon]
entry.

Tiles

Trees - All trees besides Defiled Trees now drop [link Bark] and [link Tree Sap], which both can be used to craft rubber. Tree Sap can additionally be used to craft some food
items.

Entities

Bats - All bats now drop the [link Bat Hide] material, which can be crafted into Leather. For some reason, this is the only pre-hardmode animal that can drop hides.

Wall of Flesh - The Wall of Flesh now has a chance to drop the [link Exploder Emblem] accessory for the introduction of the new [link Demolitionist Weapons].

Eater of Worlds - With the addition of the [link Demolitionist Weapons], it was necessary to reduce the damage reduction given to the Eater of Worlds in Expert-mode
to explosives. The Eater of Worlds' damage reduction is reduced to 50% in Expert and remains at 80% in Master.

Boss Immunities - With the addition of the '[link Rasterize]' and '[link Toxic Shock]' debuffs all vanilla bosses will now be immune to them to prevent the game from
being unfun.
