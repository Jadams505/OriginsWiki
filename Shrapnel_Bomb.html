Shrapnel Bomb

[statblock Shrapnel_Bomb]

The Shrapnel Bomb is an [link explosive | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] crafted using [link Bombs | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bomb] and [link Scrap]. They are also sold by the [link Demolitionist | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Demolitionist] [link NPC | | NPCs.html] for [price 9|0] after selling him 125 [link Peat Moss] once the [link Scrapper] has been defeated. Upon detonation, the bomb will release 4 bouncy shrapnel that will inflict [link Impeding Shrapnel] to struck enemies.

As of early release, it is currently impossible to obtain the Shrapnel Bomb in normal circumstances.
