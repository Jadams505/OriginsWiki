Shrapnel Bomb

The Shrapnel Bomb is an explosive crafted using Bombs and Scrap. They are also sold by the Demolitionist NPC for [price 9|0] after selling him 125 Peat Moss once the Scrapper has been defeated. Upon detonation, the bomb will release 4 frenzied shrapnel that will inflict Impeding Shrapnel to struck enemies for 5 seconds.
