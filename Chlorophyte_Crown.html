Chlorophyte Crown

[statblock Chlorophyte_Crown]

The Chlorophyte Crown is a helmet [link armor] piece to serve as the [link Summoner | $default | Summon_Weapons.html]-equivalent for [link Chlorophyte armor | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Chlorophyte_armor]. Equipping this as part of a full set does not change the set bonus beyond providing an additional 2 [link minion | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Summon_weapons#Minions] slots, otherwise only offering basic combat stats.

The Chlorophyte Crown provides the following benefits:

- +2 [link defense | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Defense]
- +12% summon damage
- +15% [link artifact | $default | Artifacts.html] damage
- +1 minion slot
Set bonus:
- +2 minion slots
