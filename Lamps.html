Lamps

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla lamps, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Lamps].

[statblock Lamps]

Lamps are a type of [link furniture] and [link light source | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Light_sources] that emits light when placed. They also count as valid [link house | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/House] lighting and can be toggled on and off by [link mechanisms | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mechanisms].
