Silica

Silica is a block type found in a Desert converted by the Riven, or the Eutrophic Sea. It is the Riven-equivalent to the sand blocks.

Notes

- Any other type of sand becomes Silica when exposed to Riven Rain. Silica gets further broken down into Silicon when exposed to amebic gel, the Riven's water type.

Trivia

- Silica is a chemical compound of one silicon atom and two oxygen atoms. The reason it is prevalent with the Riven is because it is major portion of sand's composition and that Alkahest is notorious for breaking down anything that isn't a pure element.
