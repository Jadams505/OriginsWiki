The Air Tank is an [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that is craftable using early Hardmode materials. It is another accessory that provides extra breath and can be paired with other diving equipment to get up to 4 minutes of breath. Additionally, it can be combined with itself to make the [link Air Pack] accessory.
