Personal Laser Blade

[statblock Personal_Laser_Blade]

The Personal Laser Blade is a melee weapon craftable using materials dropped by the mechanical bosses. It is a longer-range, broadsword that deals high upfront damage. Its slashes have a very large area of effect and can hit through tiles as well as illuminate the area.
