The Personal Laser Blade is a melee weapon craftable using materials dropped by the mechanical bosses. It is a longer-range, broadsword type weapon that deals high upfront damage. Its slashes have a very large area of effect and can hit through tiles as well as illuminate the area. They have a cooldown after everytime its used that lasts around 7 seconds. This weapon requires careful use.
