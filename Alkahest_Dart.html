Alkahest Dart

[statblock Alkahest_Dart]

The Alkahest Dart is a type of [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link dart | | Darts.html] fired by [link dart launchers]. Alkahest darts release clouds of alkahest that remain in place for a short moment and inflict [link Torn] to enemies with a severity of 35%. Enemies directly struck by the dart will instead be inflicted with a Torn of 60%.
