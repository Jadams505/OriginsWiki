Donor Wristband

[statblock Donor_Wristband]

The Donor Wristband is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining the effects of Charm of Myths and [link Plasma Phial]. When equipped, it increases the player's life regeneration rate by 2 and decreases the duration of Potion Sickness and other debuffs by 37.5%.
