Donor Wristband

[statblock Donor_Wristband]

The Donor Wristband is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining the effects of [link Charm of Myths | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Charm_of_Myths] and [link Plasma Phial]. When equipped, it increases the player's [link life regeneration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Health_regeneration] rate by 2 and decreases the duration of [link Potion Sickness | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Potion_Sickness] and other [link debuffs | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Debuff] by 37.5%.
