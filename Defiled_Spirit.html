[statblock Defiled_Spirit]

Defiled Spirits are consumable [link thrown | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Throwing_weapons] [link explosives | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] dropped by [link Defiled Wisps] that spawn after killing Defiled enemies in [link Expert Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Expert_Mode] or from just Defiled enemies themselves when on [link Classic Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Classic_Mode]. It is also sold by the [link Demolitionist | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Demolitionist] [link NPC | | NPCs.html] for [price 2|0] after selling him 50 [link Peat Moss] once the [link Defiled Amalgamation] has been defeated. Defiled spirits can additionally be used as [link ammunition] for the [link Snowball Cannon | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Snowball_Cannon], giving them greater velocity. Defiled spirits explode on impact and have a decent blast radius.
