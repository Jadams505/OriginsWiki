Defiled Spirit

[statblock Defiled_Spirit]

Defiled Spirits are consumable thrown [link explosives | $default | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] dropped by [link Defiled Wisps] that spawn after killing Defiled enemies in [link Expert Mode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Expert_Mode] or from just Defiled enemies themselves when on [link Classic Mode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Classic_Mode]. Defiled spirits can additionally be used as [link ammunition] for the Snowball Cannon, giving them greater velocity. Defiled spirits explode on impact and have a decent blast radius.
