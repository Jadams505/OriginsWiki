Bleeding Obsidian Shard

[statblock Bleeding_Obsidian_Shard]

Bleeding Obsidian Shards are a [link material | | Materials.html] that are harvested in amounts of 4-7 from mining [link Bleeding Obsidian]. It is primarily used to craft Bleeding Obsidian [link blocks], which are then used to craft many Dusk-themed items. It is also used to craft [link Eitrite Bars | | Eitrite_Bar.html].
