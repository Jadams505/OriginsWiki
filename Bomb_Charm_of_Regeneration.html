Bomb Charm of Regeneration

[statblock Bomb_Charm_of_Regeneration]

The Bomb Charm of Regeneration is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining the [link Band of Regeneration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Band_of_Regeneration] and [link Bomb Charm]. While equipped, all [link self-damage | | Self_Damage.html] the player recieves is reduced by 15% and [link life regeneration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Health_regeneration] is increased by 1 [link health | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Health] every second. Additionally when the player receives any self-damage while this is equipped, they will regenerate 20% of the source's damage back for a short while after.
