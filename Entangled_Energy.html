Entangled Energy

[statblock Entangled_Energy]

Entangled Energy is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is obtained from the [link Treasure Bag] dropped by the [link Fiberglass Weaver] in [link Expert Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Expert_Mode]. While equipped, Fiberglass weapons will gain a flat increase to their damage based on the player's health and the ability to [link lifesteal | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Lifesteal]. The rate at which the damage of Fiberglass weapons is increased can be expressed by "(Defense / 2) + Weapon damage".
