Cruel Angel Wings

Cruel Angel Wings are an Hardmode, post-Plantera accessory crafted using Angel Wings, Ectoplasm and Handy Helper. When equipped, the player will be able to temporarily fly while holding the Jump key like any other wings. Additionally, the player will have any incoming projectiles and enemies knocked back that are within a 4.4-tile (or 71-unit) radius of the player while in flight or falling. While Handy Helper is equipped, the cooldown of deflections will be reduced by 50%.

Cruel Angel wings have a flight time of 2.17 seconds and a max horizontal speed of 38.
