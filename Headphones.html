Headphones

[statblock Headphones]

The Headphones are a Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] crafted using mech components dropped by the mechanical bosses. While equipped, tagging an enemy with a whip increases the critical strike chance of any weapon that attacks that enemy nexy by 25%. This effect applies to any teammates as well and can be stacked by multiple players.
