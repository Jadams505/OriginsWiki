Eyndum Cores

Eyndum cores are optional modifications the player can equip into a dedicated core slot (provided by [link Eyndum Armor]) to augment the abilities of the armor set. They provide impressive boosts relating to their specific attribute and cannot be reforged unlike conventional accessories.
