Nova Cascade

[statblock Nova_Cascade]

The Nova Cascade is a Hardmode, post-Lunatic Cultist handcannon crafted with Nova Fragments. It behaves like other handcannons except that its slugs explode everytime they pierce an enemy for up to a maximum of 7 enemies.

Its best modifier is Guileless.
