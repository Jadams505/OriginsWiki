Ruby Reticle

[statblock Ruby_Reticle]

The Ruby Reticle is a Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] crafted with Cursed Flames, Rubies and [link Carburite]. It increases the player's critical strike chance by 15% of the current held weapon's base damage.
