Defiled Seeds

Defiled Seeds are a seed type that can be planted on Dirt and continue to spread the [link Defiled Wastelands] in the world. They are dropped from the Eye of Cthulhu and sold by the Dryad for 5 Silver during a Blood Moon in a Defiled world. Defiled seeds are useful for those who are using [link Mimic Armor] or seek to farm Defiled materials.
