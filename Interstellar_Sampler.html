Interstellar Sampler

[statblock Interstellar_Sampler]

The Interstellar Sampler is a post-[link Moon Lord | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Moon_Lord] [link crafting station | | Crafting_Stations.html] that is crafted at a [link Crystal Ball | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Crystal_Ball] nearby [link Shimmer | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Shimmer]. It is a direct upgrade to the [link Ancient Manipulator | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Ancient_Manipulator] and is the final basic crafting station from Origins. It crafts a wide variety of endgame items using materials found in [link the Cosmos] dimension.
