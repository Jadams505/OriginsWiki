Interstellar Sampler

The Interstellar Sampler is a post-Moon Lord crafting station that is crafted at a Crystal Ball nearby Shimmer. It is a direct upgrade to the Ancient Manipulator and is the final basic crafting station from Origins. It crafts a wide variety of endgame items using materials found in the Cosmos dimension.
