Grenade Tube

[statblock Grenade_Tube]

The Grenade Tube is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] created with [link Illegal Gun Parts | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Illegal_Gun_Parts], the [link Missile Armcannon] and [link Silicon]. When equipped, the player has a chance to randomly fire any grenade in their inventory while shooting a ranged weapon.
