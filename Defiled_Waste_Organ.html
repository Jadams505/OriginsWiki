Defiled Waste Organ

The Defiled Waste Organ is a Hardmode crafting station used to craft furniture based on the Defiled-themed Cancer Block. The Defiled Waste Organ can be purchased from the Steampunker for [price 10|0|0] but only appears for sale in worlds that are generated with the Defiled.
