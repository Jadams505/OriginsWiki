Self-Destruct

[statblock Self_Destruct]

Self-Destruct is a Hardmode explosive weapon crafted using mech components dropped by the mechanical bosses. When used, the player will charge up for 1.3 seconds before exploding themselves, dealing impressive damage in a 22-tile radius. All self-damage reduction greater than or equal to 80% will be ignored for a maximum self-inflicted damage of 125 and a minimum of 26. Additionally, a glowing red button will appear on the player's chest similar to Eyndum Cores while this is selected in the hotbar.

The best modifier is Guileless.
