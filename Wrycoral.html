Wrycoral

[statblock Wrycoral]

Wrycoral is a [link herb | | Herbs.html] grown exclusively on [link Spug Flesh] [link blocks] on the ceilings of the [link Riven Hive]'s caves. It is the Riven-equivalent to [link Deathweed | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Deathweed]. Wrycoral is currently unable to be grown from its own [link seed | | Seeds.html].
