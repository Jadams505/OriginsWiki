Dyes

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla dyes, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Dyes].

Dyes are items that can be placed in the "dye slots" of a player's [link inventory | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Inventory] to alter the colors and/or textures of equipped [link armor], [link vanity items | | Vanity.html] and [link accessories]. The [link Dye Trader | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Dye_Trader] [link NPC | | NPCs.html] sells two dyes as well as the [link Dye Vat | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Dye_Vat]. The other dyes must be crafted using the Dye Vat or obtained through trading in [link Strange Plants | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Strange_Plant] to the Dye Trader.
