Dyes

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla dyes, click here.

Dyes are items that can be placed in the "dye slots" of a player's inventory to alter the colors and/or textures of equipped armor, vanity items and accessories. The Dye Trader NPC sells two dyes as well as the Dye Vat. The other dyes must be crafted using the Dye Vat or obtained through trading in Strange Plants to the Dye Trader.
