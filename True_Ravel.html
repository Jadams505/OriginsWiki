True Ravel

[statblock True_Ravel]

True Ravel is a Hardmode, post-Plantera accessory that is crafted using all of Ravel's upgrades. True Ravel turns the player into a small sphere, upon double-tapping the Down button, that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. On top of the standard ravel statistics, True Ravel also grants the following:

Anytime:
- The ability to dash
While ravelled:
- Increased movement speed and acceleration
- Generate a kinetic wave that damages and knocks back enemies at top speed
- +18 seconds of breath
- Grants the ability to swim
- Emit an aura of light
- Grants usage of any pickaxe or drill in the player's inventory
- Grants usage of equipped hook
- -400 aggro
- Grants the ability to attach to walls and ceilings using movement keys (see Spider Ravel)
- Grants prolonged flight and immunity to fall damage
