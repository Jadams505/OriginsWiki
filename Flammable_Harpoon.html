The Flammable Harpoon is a type of [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link harpoon | | Harpoon_Variants.html] used as [link ammunition] for [link harpoon guns]. The flammable harpoon inflicts [link On Fire! | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/On_Fire!] anywhere from 4.5 to 6 seconds upon striking enemies and when being reeled in.
