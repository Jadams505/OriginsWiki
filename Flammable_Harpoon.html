Flammable Harpoon

The Flammable Harpoon is a type of pre-Hardmode harpoon used as ammunition for harpoon guns. The flammable harpoon inflicts On Fire! anywhere from 4.5 to 6 seconds upon striking enemies and when being reeled in.
