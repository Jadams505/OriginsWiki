Sootgrass

[statblock Sootgrass]

Sootgrass is a block type found around the Ashen Factory. It is the Ashen-equivalent to Grass, and can be converted to other variants with any thrown powder. Unlike the other types of grass, no foliage will grow on Sootgrass nor is it placeable/spread able through its own seed type in which it lacks.
