Minions

If you are looking for the official page on minions, click here.

Minions are mobile characters summoned by the player to fight for them. They are primarily used by summoners as a primary source of damage in combat. Some armor sets are able to summon minions of their own, but they are mainly summoned through weapons. Certain minions act as conventional NPCs and have stats of their own such as health, which are generally summoned by Artifacts (separate from other summoning weapons).

Minions require mana and minion slots to be summoned of which only one minion can be summoned at a time generally. However, there are some items that can reduce the mana cost and increase the minion slots a player has in order to summon more and less costly. Different types of minions can be deployed at the same time, so long as the player has enough minion slots.
