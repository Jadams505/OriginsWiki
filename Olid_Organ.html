Olid Organ

[statblock Olid_Organ]

The Olid Organ is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining a Putrid Scent with a [link Decaying Scale]. While equipped, the player has 5% increased damage and [link critical strike | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Critical_hit] chances and have decreased aggro from enemies. Additionally, all attacks from the player inflict [link Toxic Shock].
