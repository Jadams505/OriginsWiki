The Olid Organ is an [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] crafted from combining the [link Decaying Scale] and Putrid Scent, combining their effects. It allows the player to inflict both
'[link Toxic Shock]' and '[link Solvent]' similarly to Decaying Scale except the [link stun] length of Toxic Shock is twice as long and the critical damage of Solvent is increased to 75%. Olid Organ also has the same combat-based stats of Putrid Scent but boosted to 8% as well.
