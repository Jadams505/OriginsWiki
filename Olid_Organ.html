Olid Organ

[statblock Olid_Organ]

The Olid Organ is a Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] combining a Putrid Scent with a [link Decaying Scale]. While equipped, the player has 5% increased damage and critical strike chances and have decreased aggro from enemies. Additionally, all attacks from the player inflict [link Toxic Shock].
