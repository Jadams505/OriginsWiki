The Nematoclaw is a Acid-themed melee weapon that resembles the Fetid Baghnakhs albiet with a different damage profile. It is a very rare drop from the [link Angel of Death] (1/400), which is only found deeper into the [link Brine Pool]. It inflicts Acid Venom, Poisoned and [link Toxic Shock].
