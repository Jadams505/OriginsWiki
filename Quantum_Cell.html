Qauntum Cell

[statblock Quantum_Cell]

The Quantum Cell is a boss-summoning [link consumable | | Consumables.html] item with an unplanned obtain method. Upon use at a [link Cubekon Altar], it will power up [link Quantux] for the player to fight. If the player is not inside the [link Cubekon Temple] while Quantux is powered up, it will deactivate and continue healing towards its max health.
