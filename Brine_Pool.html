Brine Pool

"No brew I know rivals the Brine. Maybe I should steal some later." - The [link Witch Doctor | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Witch_Doctor]

The Brine Pool is an aquatic [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link biome | | Biomes.html] that features a diversity of enemies fitting in with its extremophiliac theme. The Brine Pool always spawns within a [link Jungle | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Jungle] biome, but not always with a clear entrance from the top. It can be spotted from the presence of [link Dolomite] and an opening on its top so that the player can enter. Within its shell, the Brine Pool is structured with open cells full of [link water | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Water] that the player will have to swim through in order to explore deeper within.

While the player is inflicted with [link Toxic Shock] while submerged in water nearby the Brine Pool or any other source, they will be unable to replenish their [link breath | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Breath_meter] until no longer recieving Toxic Shock and out of water. Furthermore, Dolomite is impervious to any [link explosives | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html]. Because of these factors, proper protection against Toxic Shock and preparation for water travel is advised before navigating the Brine Pool.

Generation

The Brine Pool generates as a large oval-like shape with smaller cells within. Each of the cells connect with an adjacent cell aside from a few which may remain isolated from the rest. Some cells also connect to the top entrance, which is usually 12-20 tiles wide. [link Mud | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mud_Block]-grown [link Peat Moss] encompass the inner regions of each cell, and [link Eitrite Ore] will generate in veins within the Dolomite shell. Additionally, [link Brineglow] vines will generate from the ceiling of each cell and illuminate a small area around them. Some veins of [link Mildew] will appear within Dolomite as well, and spread along it serving as a miniature [link world evil | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Evil_biomes] for the Brine Pool. It can be cleansed using [link Purification Powder | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Thrown_Powder]. Lastly, [link Geyser Cores | | Geyser_Core.html] will generate in Dolomite extensions within some cells to emit hydrothermal waste.

The [link Fiberglass Undergrowth] also always spawns directly underneath the Brine Pool.

Trivia

- The Brine Pool was originally planned to be a large drop off not too dissimilar to its current entrance and have a brine cloud overlay over the player's screen, which would then inflict Toxic Shock. It also had a separate section off to the right that would lead to a temple-like area called "The Ruins", which has been removed for another mod idea later down the line.
