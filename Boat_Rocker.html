Boat Rocker

[statblock Boat_Rocker]

The Boat Rocker is a Hardmode [link harpoon gun | $default | Harpoon_Guns.html] with a 1/5 (20%) chance of being dropped by the [link Lost Diver]. While a harpoon is deployed, the player can use the Interact button to swing the deployed harpoon and the chains to act like a whip. The swinging attack can only be stopped once the harpoon projectile collides with a tile or when the chain breaks from hitting an enemy.

Its best [link Modifier | $default | Modifiers.html] is Godly or Demonic.

Tips

- The length the player chooses before swinging the line changes the speed at which it will rotate. Smaller lengths attack faster while greater lengths attack
slower but have a greater range.
- Boat Rocker's alternate swing attack is most effective while the player is airborne and away from ground.
