Razorwire

[statblock Razorwire]

Razorwire is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] improving the effects of [link Return To Sender] by combining it with a Shackle. Razorwire handles hordes of enemies well by splitting 67% of the damage the player recieves to 3 of the closest enemies. It also passively decreases [link enemy aggression | $default | Aggor.html] reinforcing its role as a defensive accessory.
