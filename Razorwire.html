Razorwire

Razorwire is a pre-Hardmode accessory improving the effects of Return To Sender by combining it with a Shackle. Razorwire handles hordes of enemies well by splitting 67% of the damage the player recieves to 3 of the closest enemies. It also passively decreases enemy aggression by 125, reinforcing its role as a defensive accessory.
