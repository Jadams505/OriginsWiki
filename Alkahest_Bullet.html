Alkahest Bullet

The Alkahest Bullet is a type of Hardmode bullet fired by guns. The bullets carry their namesake Alkahest component inside of a glass tip, shattering on impact to release alkahest on the target. Alkahest bullets inflict Torn with a 25% severity on impact.
