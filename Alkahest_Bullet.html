Alkahest Bullet

[statblock Alkahest_Bullet]

The Alkahest Bullet is a type of Hardmode bullet fired by guns. The bullets carry their namesake [link Alkahest] component inside of a glass tip, shattering on impact to release alkahest on the target. Alkahest bullets inflict [link Torn] on impact.
