Sofas

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla sofas, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Sofas].

[statblock Sofas]

Sofas are vanity [link furniture] items that fulfill a [link house | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/House]'s [link comfort item | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/House#Comfort_items] requirement. Players can sit in sofas by using the Open/Activate button on them and can receive a boost in [link health regeneration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Health_regeneration], similar to [link beds] and [link Sofas]. Players can choose to sit on the left, middle or right positions, and thus up to 3 players can sit on a sofa at a time.
