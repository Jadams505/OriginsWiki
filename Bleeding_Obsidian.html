Bleeding Obsidian

[statblock Bleeding_Obsidian]

Bleeding Obsidian is a type of [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link ore | | Ores.html] [link block | | Blocks.html] found in [link the Dusk]. It is the one of three ores that do not need to be turned into [link bars] in order to utilize as a crafting [link material | | Materials.html]. Bleeding Obsidian requires at least 190% [link pickaxe power | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pickaxe_power] to mine, and yields the player 4-7 [link Bleeding Obsidian Shards | | Bleeding_Obsidian_Shard.html] when destroyed.

Tips

- The most efficent way to obtain Bleeding Obsidian is to convert regular Obsidian into it at an altar with a Soul of Night. An [link obsidian generator | | https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=265529643] can be
created to generate limitless amounts of Obsidian so long as the player has enough Souls of Night in their inventory.
