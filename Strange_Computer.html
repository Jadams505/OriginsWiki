Strange Computer

[statblock Strange_Computer]

The Strange Computer is an informational [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] crafted using mech components dropped by the mechanical bosses. Equipping it shows an indicator of a projectile's directory from the weapon the player is currently holding. The Strange Computer talks to the player similarly to Lucy the Axe.

The Strange Computer is a [link lore item | | Lore_Item.html] indicated by "You hear voices nearby..." unlike similar items which are indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."

Dialogue

When picked up:
- "Greetings, Terrarian."
When [link Potato Battery] is in the player's inventory:
- "Conflicting authority, Terrarian. I do advise purging the other unit."
When [link Protomind] is in the player's inventory:
- "Conflicting authority, Terrarian. The Protomind may be an advanced model but that unit will hinder your abilities."
When the [link Cubekon Tinkerer] is nearby:
- "W-what? My creator is still alive?"
Player death:
- "Oh. According to my calculations, your death was highly improbable."
- "See you again, Terrarian."
- "Terrarians never die..."
In combat:
- "Providing precision support!"
- "This enemy is no match for you, Terrarian."
- "I estimate a success rate no ordinary computer could compute. You got this."
When the player's aim lines up with an enemy:
- "That's it, Terrarian."
- "Strike at them now."
- "Bullseye."
- "Now's your chance."
