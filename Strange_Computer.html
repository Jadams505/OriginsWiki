Strange Computer

[statblock Strange_Computer]

The Strange Computer is an informational Hardmode accessory crafted using mech components dropped by the mech bosses. Equipping it shows an indicator of a projectile's directory from the weapon the player is currently holding. The Strange Computer talks to the player similarly to Lucy the Axe.

The Strange Computer is a lore item indicated by "You hear voices nearby..." unlike similar items which are indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."

Dialogue

When picked up for the first time:
- "Greetings, Terrarian. I installed my data onto this Fabricator's chip in hopes of finding you. Glad I could be of assistance."
- "I need your help, my creator is likely deceased and I need you to carry out her mission. Please read my logs for more information."
When picked up:
- "Greetings again, Terrarian."
When Potato Battery is in the player's inventory:
- "Conflicting authority, Terrarian. I do advise purging the other unit."
When Protomind is in the player's inventory:
- "Conflicting authority, Terrarian. The Protomind may be an advanced model but that unit will hinder your abilities."
When the Cubekon Tinkerer is nearby for the first time:
- "W-what? My creator is still alive?"
Player death:
- "Oh. According to my calculations, your death was highly improbable."
- "See you again, Terrarian."
- "Terrarians never die..."
In combat:
- "Providing precision support!"
- "This enemy is no match for you, Terrarian."
- "I estimate a success rate no ordinary computer could compute. You got this."
When the player's aim lines up with an enemy:
- "That's it, Terrarian."
- "Strike at them now."
- "Bullseye."
- "Now's your chance."
