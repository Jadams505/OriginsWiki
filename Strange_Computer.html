Strange Computer

[statblock Strange_Computer]

The Strange Computer is an informational Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] crafted using mech components dropped by the mechanical bosses. Equipping it shows an indicator of a projectile's directory from the weapon the player is currently holding. The Strange Computer talks to the player similarly to Lucy the Axe.

The Strange Computer is a [link lore item | $default | Lore_Item.html] indicated by "You hear voices nearby..." unlike similar items which are indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."
