Strange Computer

[statblock Strange_Computer]

The Strange Computer is an informational [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] crafted using mech components dropped by the [link mech bosses | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mechanical_bosses]. Equipping it shows an indicator of a projectile's directory from the weapon the player is currently holding. The Strange Computer talks to the player similarly to [link Lucy the Axe | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Lucy_the_Axe].

The Strange Computer is a [link lore item | | Lore_Item.html] indicated by "You hear voices nearby..." unlike similar items which are indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."

Dialogue

When picked up for the first time: - "Greetings, Terrarian. I installed my data onto this Fabricator's chip in hopes of finding you. Glad I could be of assistance." - "I need your help, my creator is likely deceased and I need you to carry out her mission. Please read my logs for more information." When picked up: - "Greetings again, Terrarian." When [link Potato Battery] is in the player's [link inventory | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Inventory]: - "Conflicting authority, Terrarian. I do advise purging the other unit." When [link Protomind] is in the player's inventory: - "Conflicting authority, Terrarian. The Protomind may be an advanced model but that unit will hinder your abilities." When the [link Cubekon Tinkerer] is nearby for the first time: - "W-what? My creator is still alive?" Player death: - "Oh. According to my calculations, your death was highly improbable." - "See you again, Terrarian." - "Terrarians never die..." In combat: - "Providing precision support!" - "This enemy is no match for you, Terrarian." - "I estimate a success rate no ordinary computer could compute. You got this." When the player's aim lines up with an enemy: - "That's it, Terrarian." - "Strike at them now." - "Bullseye." - "Now's your chance."
