Armor

Armor provides the player with protection in the form of damage reduction. Armor is absolutely vital when progressing and determines
the playstyle that will be prevalent in the game. Origins adds armor sets of its own in order to fit the new [link Demolitionist Weapons] in and
add a new variety of playstyles for existing classes.
