Scavenger Bag

[statblock Scavenger_Bag]

The Scavenger Bag is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] post-[link Plantera | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Plantera] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining the effects of the Papyrus Scarab and [link Keepsake Remains]. While equipped, the player's armor penetration is increased by 5, max number of minions by 1, summon damage by 15% and the knockback of minions by 2. Additionally, all of the player's attacks inflict [link Torn].
