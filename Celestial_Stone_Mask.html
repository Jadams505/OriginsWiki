Celestial Stone Mask

[statblock Celestial_Mask]

The Celestial Stone Mask is a [link Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] post-[link Golem | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Golem] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] created by combining Celestial Stone and the [link Stone Mask]. Equipping it provides major boosts to the player's stats at the cost of their movement speed.

Stat boosts:
- +15% melee speed
- +10% damage (all types)
- +4% [link critical strike | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Critical_hit] chance
- +4 HP/s life regeneration
- +8 defense
- +25% mining speed
- +1 minion knockback

Stat reduction:
- -10% movement speed
- -33% jump speed
