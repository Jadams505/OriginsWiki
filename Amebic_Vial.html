Amebic Vial

[statblock Amebic_Vial]

The Amebic Vial is a Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that is obtained from the Treasure Bag dropped by the [link World Cracker] in Expert Mode. It cast amebic tentacles to protect the player from incoming projectiles by grabbing and throwing them away from the player. There is a unique visual effect on the player that can be turned off while equipped on the player.

The tentacles have a range of 4.4 tiles (71 units) at which they will protect the player and have a cooldown of 1.67 seconds before they are deployed again.
