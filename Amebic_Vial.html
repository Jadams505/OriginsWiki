Amebic Vial

[statblock Amebic_Vial]

The Amebic Vial is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is obtained from the [link Treasure Bag | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Treasure_Bag] dropped by the [link World Cracker] in [link Expert Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Expert_Mode]. It generates amebic tentacles to protect the player from incoming projectiles by grabbing and throwing them away from the player. There is a unique visual effect on the player that can be turned off while equipped on the player, this same effect can also be shown when equipped to the player's vanity accessory slot.

The tentacles have a range of 4.4 tiles (71 units) at which they will protect the player and have a cooldown of 1.67 seconds before they can be deployed again.
