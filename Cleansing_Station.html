Cleansing Station

[statblock Cleansing_Station]

The Cleansing Station is a [link furniture] item that is sold by the [link Dryad | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Dryad] [link NPC | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/NPC] after completing her 'Woodsprite Steps' [link quest | | Quests.html]. When used holding down the Interact key, it will rapidly deplete all [link Assimilation] the player has accrued. When holding a [link Mojo Flask] and interacting with the Cleansing Station, it will also refill it back to the 5 charges it started with. It is one of the two only sources of [link Assimilation management | | Assimilation_Strategies.html].
