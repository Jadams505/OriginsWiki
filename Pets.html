Pets

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla pets, click here.

Pets are creatures that follow the player around. They are completely invincible and have no set duration. When summoned, they will give the player a buff with the same name as the pet. A pet will follow the player until they die, summon a different pet, leave the world or cancel the associated buff. The equipped pet can be seen on the player selection menu alongside the player.

Most pets are purely cosmetic and do not consume any minion slots. Some pets known as Light Pets can emit light and take up their own dedicated slots alongside regular pets.
