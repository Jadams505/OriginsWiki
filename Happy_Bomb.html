Happy Bomb

[statblock Happy_Bomb]

The Happy Bomb is an [link explosive | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] that is guaranteed to drop in stacks of 69 from [link Clowns | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Clown]. The Happy Bomb has no difference from regular bombs other than an increase in its damage and the laughing sound effect it makes after slaying an enemy.
