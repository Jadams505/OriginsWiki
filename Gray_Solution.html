It will convert certain [link blocks] and [link walls] to their [link Defiled | | Defiled_Wastelands.html] counterparts, and is sold by the [link Steampunker | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Steampunker] during a [link Blood Moon | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Blood_Moon] or [link Solar Eclipse | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Solar_Eclipse] in a Defiled world.
