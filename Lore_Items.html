Lore items

Lore items are items that come with supporting information in the form of a story or compositional note. Lore items are usually indicated by the "You hear whispers nearby..." or similar phrases in their tooltip where, if the player has acquired the Journal, they can use the Inspect key (in Origins' configuration menu) to unlock its journal entry. The lore of Origins as a whole offers nothing to the gameplay and only serves to enrich the world and/or make it seem more lively.
