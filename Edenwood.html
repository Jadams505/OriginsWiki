Edenwood

Edenwood is a type of wood block and material dropped by Eden Trees found at the Dawn. Edenwood behaves the same as regular wood, but adopts a cherry-red coloration paired with eyes. It can only be obtained in Hardmode due to being solely accessible by the Dawn. Eden Trees can be planted anew by planting Acorns on Edengrass.
