Locket Necklace

[statblock Locket_Necklace]

The Locket Necklace is a Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] crafted using a Panic Necklace and [link Comb]. When equipped, the player will have a 20% increase in damage and a 50% increase in movement speed after receiving damage.
