Locket Necklace

[statblock Locket_Necklace]

The Locket Necklace is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] crafted using a [link Panic Necklace | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Panic_Necklace] and [link Comb]. When equipped, the player will have a 20% increase in damage and a 50% increase in movement speed after receiving damage.
