Defiled Fissure

The Defiled Fissure is a grounded, geyser-like background object found in the Defiled Wastelands. A world with the Defiled features several Defiled Fissures, and destroying three of them (in a single world) summons the Defiled Amalgamation for the player to fight. This can be done using any hammer or explosive. Defiled Fissures emit a faint, white light. They are the Defiled counterpart to the Corruption's Shadow Orbs or the Crimson's Crimson Hearts.

Consequences of destruction

- Each Defiled Fissure drops items when destroyed. The first will always drop the Kruncher accompanied with 100 Musket Balls, of which satisfies the criteria for the Arms Dealer to move in.
- Destroying at least one Fissure fulfills one of the two criteria for a Goblin Army to attack (having at least 200 health is the other requirement).
- The third Defiled Fissure destroyed summons the aforementioned Defiled Amalgamation, which spawns after a short moment to give the player time to reach the surface before it spawns. The Defiled Amalgamation is re-summoned with every third Fissure destroyed.
- Each Fissure gives a separate 50% chance of immediately causing a meteorite to fall when destroyed between midnight (12:00 AM) and dawn (4:30 AM). Destroying any number of Fissures between dawn and midnight will only allow one 50% chance of a meteorite falling the following midnight.
- Once a Defiled Fissure is destroyed, the Traveling Merchant has a chance to sell the Revolver.

Messages

The following status messages appear upon destruction of the Gel Blisters:

- First fissure: "Your body suddenly aches everywhere..."
- Second fissure: "Rattling emanates from the walls..."
- Third fissure: "The skies echo a threatening call..."

Sometime after the Defiled Amalgamation spawns:

- "Defiled Amalgamation has awoken!"
