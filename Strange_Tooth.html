Strange Tooth

[statblock Strange_Tooth]

The Strange Tooth is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is dropped by the [link Eye of Cthulhu | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Eye_of_Cthulhu] in [link Master Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Master_Mode]. While equipped, the player will generate miniature Servants of Cthulhu to fight for them based on how many [link minion slots | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Summon_weapons#Minions] they have without actually taking up any slots. The Servants will kamikaze into enemies and have a chance to drop [link Hearts | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Heart] whenever the player is low on health. The Servants will slowly replenish over time.
