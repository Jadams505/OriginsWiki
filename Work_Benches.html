Work Benches

[statblock Work_Benches]

Work Benches are a type of [link crafting station | | Crafting_Stations.html] required to craft many essential early-game items. They are also one possible way to fulfill a [link house | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/House]'s [link flat surface item | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Flat_surface_items] requirement. The Work Bench is the first crafting station the player can make in the game, and one of the first items overall.
