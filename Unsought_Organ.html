Unsought Organ

[statblock Unsought_Organ]

The Unsought Organ is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] crafted using an [link Olid Organ] and [link Razorwire]. When equipped, half of all damage the player recieves is replicated across three enemies within 15 tiles from the player whilst also being inflicted with [link Toxic Shock]. Additionally, damage and [link critical strike | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Critical_hit] chance are increased by 5%, all attacks inflict [link Toxic Shock] and enemy [link aggression | | Aggro.html] is reduced by 275.
