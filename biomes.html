Biomes

- Biomes are an integral part to the gameplay of Terraria. They will impact not only the pace of your game but the pathway to how you will progress.
Terraria Origins implements some of its own biomes to add variety and have a means to better explain the lore behind the world of Terraria. These
additions include natural generation and artificial structures alike. See some of these biomes below:

Cubekon Temple

- The temple of a highly-advanced alien species entombed far into the Cavern Layer of Terraria. It can be usually located adjacent to Marble Caves
and is comprised of Batholith, a durable and ancient rocky material. Foreign entities should be wary of the dangers that remain within.
