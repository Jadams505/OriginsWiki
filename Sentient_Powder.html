Sentient Powder

[statblock Sentient_Powder]

Sentient Powder is a [link consumable | | Consumables.html] item that can be crafted using [link Acetabularia] or purchased from the [link Dryad | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Dryad] for [price 1|0] each during a [link Blood Moon | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Blood_Moon] in a Riven world. It is able to convert blocks and walls, including those of its [link world evil | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Evil_biomes] counterparts and [link Hallowed | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/The_Hallow] ones, into their Riven form. If thrown at a [link Bunny | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bunny], [link Goldfish | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Goldfish] or [link Penguin | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Penguin], they will turn into their respective Riven variants: the [link Barnacled Hare], [link Bottomfeeder], and [link Riven Penguin] respectively.
