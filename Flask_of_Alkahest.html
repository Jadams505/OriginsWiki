Flask of Alkahest

[statblock Salt_Flask]

The Flask of Alkahest is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link flask | | Flasks.html] that can be crafted using [link Bottled Water | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bottled_Water] and [link Alkahest]. When consumed, it allows all [link melee weapons] and [link whips] to inflict the [link Torn] debuff with a severity of 20% for 5 seconds. This effect will last for 20 minutes, but can be canceled anytime by right-clicking the icon. Melee weapons that fire [link projectiles | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Projectiles] will also inflict the debuff on enemies hit by the projectiles (except for the [link Flairon | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Flairon]'s bubbles).

The Flask of Alkahest cannot be used in conjunction with other flasks, and using it will remove any existing flask effects. Like all other flasks, the effect will not be canceled when the player [link dies | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Death].

Tips

- The Flask of Alkahest will make melee weapons and whips glow when they are used. In combination with [link yoyos], it can be a good lighting source in dark areas.
