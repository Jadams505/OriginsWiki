Flask of Alkahest

The Flask of Alkahest is a Hardmode flask that can be crafted using Bottled Water and Alkahest. When consumed, it allows all melee weapons and whips to inflict the Torn debuff with a severity of 20% for 5 seconds. This effect will last for 20 minutes, but can be canceled anytime by right-clicking the icon. Melee weapons that fire projectiles will also inflict the debuff on enemies hit by the projectiles (except for the Flairon's bubbles).

The Flask of Alkahest cannot be used in conjunction with other flasks, and using it will remove any existing flask effects. Like all other flasks, the effect will not be canceled when the player dies.

Tips

- The Flask of Alkahest will make melee weapons and whips glow when they are used. In combination with yoyos, it can be a good lighting source in dark areas.
