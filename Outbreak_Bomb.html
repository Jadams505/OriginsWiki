Outbreak Bomb

The Outbreak Bomb is an explosive crafted using Demonite Ore and Bombs. They are also sold by the Demolitionist NPC for [price 6|0] after selling him 110 Peat Moss once the Eater of Worlds has been defeated. Upon detonation, the bomb will inflict Poisoned to any enemies in its blast radius. If an enemy dies to this weapon while being poisoned, they will explode and inflict Poisoned to other nearby enemies.
