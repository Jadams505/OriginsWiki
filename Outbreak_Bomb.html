Outbreak Bomb

[statblock Outbreak_Bomb]

The Outbreak Bomb is an [link explosive | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] crafted using [link Demonite Ore | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Demonite_Ore] and [link Bombs | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bomb]. Upon detonation, the bomb will inflict [link Poisoned | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Poisoned] to any enemies in its blast radius. If an enemy dies to this weapon while being poisoned, they will explode and inflict Poisoned to other nearby enemies.
