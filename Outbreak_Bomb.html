Outbreak Bomb

[statblock Outbreak_Bomb]

The Outbreak Bomb is an [link explosive | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] crafted using [link Demonite Ore | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Demonite_Ore] and [link Bombs | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bomb]. They are also sold by the [link Demolitionist | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Demolitionist] [link NPC | | NPCs.html] for [price 6|0] after selling him 110 [link Peat Moss] once the [link Eater of Worlds | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Eater_of_Worlds] has been defeated. Upon detonation, the bomb will inflict [link Poisoned | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Poisoned] to any enemies in its blast radius. If an enemy dies to this weapon while being poisoned, they will explode and inflict Poisoned to other nearby enemies.
