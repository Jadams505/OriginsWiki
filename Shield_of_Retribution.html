Shield of Retribution

[statblock Retribution_Shield]

The Shield of Retribution is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] shield [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] combining the Obsidian Shield with [link Razorwire]. The shield provides an immunity to knockback and the Burning debuff. Additionally, it splits 67% of the damage the player recieves to 3 of the nearest enemies and makes decreases enemy aggression.
