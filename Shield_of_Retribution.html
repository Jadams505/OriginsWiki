Shield of Retribution

The Shield of Retribution is a pre-Hardmode shield accessory combining the Obsidian Shield with Razorwire. The shield provides an immunity to knockback and the Burning debuff. Additionally, it splits half of the damage the player recieves to 3 of the nearest enemies and decreases enemy aggression by 125.
