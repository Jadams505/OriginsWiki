Shield of Retribution

[statblock Shield_of_Retribution]

The Shield of Retribution is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link shield | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Shields] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining the [link Obsidian Shield | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Obsidian_Shield] with [link Razorwire]. The shield provides an immunity to [link knockback | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Knockback] and the [link Burning | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Burning] debuff. Additionally, it splits half of the damage the player recieves to 3 of the nearest enemies and decreases [link enemy aggression | | Aggro.html] by 125.
