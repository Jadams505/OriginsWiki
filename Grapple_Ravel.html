Grapple Ravel

[statblock Grapple_Ravel]

Grapple Ravel is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is crafted using [link Ravel] and the [link Super Turbo Reel]. Grapple Ravel turns the player into a small sphere that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Grapple Ravel also allows the player to utilize their equipped [link hook | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hooks].
