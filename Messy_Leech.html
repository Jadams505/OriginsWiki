Messy Leech

[statblock Messy_Leech]

The Messy Leech is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that can be fished for in the Jungle. It counts as a common item and has a 10% chance of being obtained if the requisites are met. While equipped, all attacks from the player inflict [link Bleeding | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bleeding].

Tips

- Obtaining the Messy Leech early is a good investment for [link Defiled | | Defiled_Wastelands.html] worlds as the Bleeding debuff prevents Defiled enemies from regenerating after stealing mana from the player. This effect also works on the [link Defiled Amalgamation] and could make its fight easier.
