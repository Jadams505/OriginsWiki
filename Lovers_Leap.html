Lover's Leap

[statblock Lovers_Leap]

Lover's Leap is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining [link Spectre Boots | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Spectre_Boots] with a [link Locket Necklace]. It allows the player to run fast while having a 20% increase in damage and 50% increase in movement speed after taking damage. It also allows the player to dash by double-tapping the horizontal movement keys and to temporarily propel themselves vertically after a jump by holding the jump key.

The dash provided by Lover's Leap can damage an enemy the player comes in contact with and knock them back.
