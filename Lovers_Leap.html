Lover's Leap

[statblock Lovers_Leap]

Lover's Leap is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] combining Spectre Boots with a [link Locket Necklace]. When equipped, it allows the player to run fast while have 20% increase in damage and 50% increase in movement speed after taking damage. It also allows the player to dash by double-tapping the horizontal movement keys and to temporarily propel themselves vertically after a jump.

The dash provided by Lover's Leap can damage an enemy the player comes in contact with and knock them back.
