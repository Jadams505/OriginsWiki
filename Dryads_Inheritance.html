Dryad's Inheritance

The Dryad's Inheritance is a Hardmode, Expert Mode-exclusive, post-Plantera accessory combining the Spore Sac and the Last Descendant. While equipped, this increases the player's damage, movement speed, and length of invincibility after taking damage. Additionally, it casts an aura around the player that deals constant damage and the Poisoned debuff to any enemies within. The aura deals even more damage against world evil enemies.
