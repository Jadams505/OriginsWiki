Dryad's Inheritance

[statblock Dryads_Inheritance]

Dryad's Inheritance is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] post-[link Plantera | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Plantera] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining the Spore Sac and the [link Last Descendant]. While equipped, this increases the player's damage, movement speed, and length of invincibility after taking damage. Additionally, it casts an aura around the player that deals constant damage and the Poisoned debuff to any enemies within.
