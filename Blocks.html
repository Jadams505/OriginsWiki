Blocks

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla blocks, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Blocks].

Blocks are the primary components of every Terraria world. They make up nearly all of the game's terrain, with the preponderance
of a particular block type in an area determining its [link biome]. Nearly all blocks are collectible for use by players, and are mined with
[link pickaxes], [link drills], or [link explosives | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html]. All block types can be stacked up to 9999 in a single [link inventory | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Inventory] slot.

Soils

Soils are the most accessible type of block in the game, and they make up the majority of the world. Many of them grow plants naturally. Soils are the only blocks
that can be converted by the [link world evils | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Evil_biomes] and [link Hallow | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/The_Hallow]. All soils are destroyed by [link explosives | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html].

[link Ores]

Ores are found underground (with exception to some), certain types only in particular biomes. They are used in the crafting of [link bars], which in turn are used to craft
[link weapons], [link armor], [link furniture], and more. Many are used to make bricks or other blocks as well.
