Blocks

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla blocks, click here.

Blocks are the primary components of every Terraria world. They make up nearly all of the game's terrain, with the preponderance of a particular block type in an area determining its [link biome]. Nearly all blocks are collectible for use by players, and are mined with [link pickaxes], [link drills], or [link explosives | $default | Demolitionist_Weapons.html]. All block types can be stacked up to 9999 in a single [link inventory | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Inventory] slot.
