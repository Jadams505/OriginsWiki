Blocks

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla blocks, click here.

Blocks are the primary components of every Terraria world. They make up nearly all of the game's terrain, with the preponderance of a particular block type in an area determining its biome. Nearly all blocks are collectible for use by players, and are mined with pickaxes, drills, or explosives. All block types can be stacked up to 9999 in a single inventory slot.

Soils

Soils are the most accessible type of block in the game, and they make up the majority of the world. Many of them grow plants naturally. Soils are the only blocks that can be converted by the world evils and Hallow. All soils are destroyed by explosives.

Ores

Ores are found underground (with exception to some), certain types only in particular biomes. They are used in the crafting of bars, which in turn are used to craft weapons, armor, furniture, and more. Many are used to make bricks or other blocks as well.
