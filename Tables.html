If you are looking for the official page of vanilla tables, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Tables].

[statblock Tables]

Tables are a type of [link furniture] item which can be placed on the ground, commonly used in conjunction with [link chairs]. They fulfill a [link house | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/House]'s [link flat surface item | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Flat_surface_items] requirement. They can also form a minor crafting station when combined with a chair.
