Sniper's Mark is a [link Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] combining the Ranger Emblem and [link Ruby Reticle]. While equipped, ranged damage is increased by 15% and the player's [link critical strike | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Critical_hit] chance is increased by 15% of the current held weapon's base damage.
