Sniper's Mark

[statblock Snipers_Mark]

Sniper's Mark is a Hardmode accessory combining the Ranger Emblem and Ruby Reticle. While equipped, ranged damage is increased by 15% and the player's critical strike chance is increased by 15% of the current held weapon's base damage.
