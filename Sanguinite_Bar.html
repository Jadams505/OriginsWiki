Sanguinite Bar

[statblock Sanguinite_Bar]

Sanguinite Bars are pre-Hardmode bars crafted using Sanguinite Ore at a Furnace. They are used to craft mid-tier pre-hardmode equipment and weapons. Sanguinite bars are considered an evil bar.
