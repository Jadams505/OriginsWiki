Sanguinite Bar

[statblock Sanguinite_Bar]

Sanguinite Bars are [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link bars] crafted using [link Sanguinite Ore] at a [link Furnace | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Furnace]. They are used to craft mid-tier pre-hardmode equipment and weapons. Sanguinite bars are considered an [link evil bar | | Recipes.html].
