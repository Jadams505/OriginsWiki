Blue Bovine

[statblock Blue_Bovine]

Blue Bovine is a [link food] item that is sold by the [link Merchant | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Merchant] [link NPC | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/NPCs] after completing his 'To the Skies!' [link quest | | Quests.html]. When consumed, it provides the Bovine Flight [link buff | | Buffs.html], which gives the player a temporary set of [link wings]. This effect will last for 4 minutes, but can be canceled anytime by right-clicking the icon.

Like most wings, the soda wings allow the player to slow their fall by holding down the Jump key and will not take any [link fall damage | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Fall_damage]. Its flight speed and power are overall stronger than [link Fledgling Wings | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Wings], but it is rather expensive to obtain Blue Bovine as a drawback.

Trivia

- Blue Bovine is a reference to the [link Red Bull | | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Bull] energy drink and its slogan "Red Bull Gives You Wings".
