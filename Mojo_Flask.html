Mojo Flask

[statblock Mojo_Flask]

The Mojo Flask is a [link consumable | | Consumables.html], potion-like item sold by the [link Dryad | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Dryad] [link NPC | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/NPCs] at all times. When consumed, it provides the Purifying [link buff | | Buffs.html], which cleanses 20% of all [link Assimilation] types for each of its 5 uses. The Mojo Flask will go on cooldown for 5 seconds after each use through the [link Mojo Sickness] debuff.

Unlike most consumables, the Mojo Flask will not be removed from the player's inventory once it reaches all of its uses. Instead it will remain empty until the player refills it by right-clicking a [link Cleansing Station] while this is being held.
