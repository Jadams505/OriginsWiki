Accessories

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla accessories, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Accessories].

Accessories are equippable items that are put into their respective accessory slots. They provide movement-based, offensive, defensive,
or other miscellaneous benefits when equipped, but some also function by just being in the player inventory.
