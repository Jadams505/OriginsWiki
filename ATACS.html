ATACS

ATACS is a Hardmode, post-Golem accessory combining the Eye of the Golem with the Strange Computer. Equipping it shows an indicator of a projectile's directory from the weapon the player is currently holding and grants a +10% critical strike chance. ATACS talks to the player similarly to Lucy the Axe.
