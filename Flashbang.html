They are also sold by the [link Demolitionist | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Demolitionist] [link NPC | | NPCs.html] for [price 75] after selling him 25 [link Peat Moss]. Upon detonation, a flash of light will engulf its entire blast radius that inflicts [link Confused | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Confused] for 3.67 seconds and [link Slow | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Slow] for 5 seconds.
