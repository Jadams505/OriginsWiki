Flashbang

The Flashbang is an explosive that can be crafted using Grenades and Fallen Stars. They are also sold by the Demolitionist NPC for [price 75] after selling him 25 Peat Moss. Upon detonation, a flash of light will engulf its entire blast radius that inflicts Confused for 3.67 seconds and Slow for 5 seconds.
