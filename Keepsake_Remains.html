Keepsake Remains

[statblock Keepsake_Remains]

Keepsake Remains are a pre-Hardmode accessory combining a Shark Tooth Necklace with a Symbiote Skull. All attacks from the player inflict Torn and the player's armor penetration is increased by 5 while this is equipped.
