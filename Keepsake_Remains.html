Keepsake Remains

[statblock Keepsake_Remains]

Keepsake Remains are a Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] combining a Shark Tooth Necklace with a [link Symbiote Skull]. All attacks from the player inflict [link Torn] and the player's armor penetration is increased by 5 while this is equipped.
