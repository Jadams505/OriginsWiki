Keepsake Remains

[statblock Keepsake_Remains]

Keepsake Remains are a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining a [link Shark Tooth Necklace | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Shark_Tooth_Necklace] with a [link Symbiote Skull]. All attacks from the player inflict [link Torn] and the player's [link armor penetration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Armor_penetration] is increased by 5 while this is equipped.
