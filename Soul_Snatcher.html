Soul Snatcher

The Soul Snatcher is a pre-Hardmode melee weapon that is crafted using Demonite Bars and Shadow Scales. It is a thrown spear, where, upon latching to an enemy or surface, has a unique effect involving the Shadefire debuff. While latched to an enemy, it inflicts Shadowflame for 3 seconds instead. While latched to a surface, it releases 5 shadowflame walls that inflict Shadefire for 6 seconds to enemies that cross them. After its latching duration has expired, it turns into a pick-up for the player to retrieve.

Its best modifier is Legendary or Beckoning. Beckoning allows the snatcher to return to the player on its own.
