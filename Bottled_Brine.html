Bottled Brine

[statblock Bottled_Brine]

Bottled Brine is a Hardmode material that is brewed using Bottles, Stingers and Magic Brine Droppers. It cannot be directly obtained like water and must be crafted. It is used to make most Brine-themed items and for some brewing.
