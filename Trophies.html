Trophies

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla trophies, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Trophies].

[statblock Trophies]

Trophies are decorative [link furniture] items which usually have a 1/10 (10%) chance to be dropped by most [link bosses]. They are solely cosmetic and do not provide any bonuses.
