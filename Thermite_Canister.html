Thermite Canister

[statblock Thermite_Canister]

The Thermite Canister is a type of [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link canister | | Canisters.html] used as [link ammunition] for [link launchers | | Launchers.html] and the [link Proximity Mine Launcher | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Proximity_Mine_Launcher]. Upon landing on a tile or enemy, the Thermite Canister will explode into napalm that deals damage over time to any enemies that walk over it.
