If you are looking for the official page of vanilla boomerangs, click here.

Boomerangs are a type of melee weapon that are thrown outwards when used and return to the player after a short amount time. They have a much greater range than most other types of melee weapons but are not considered ranged due to not requiring any ammunition.

The best modifier for all boomerangs is Godly. Stackable boomerangs cannot receive any modifiers.
