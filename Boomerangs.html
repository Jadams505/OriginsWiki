Guns

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla boomerangs, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Boomerangs].

Boomerangs are a type of [link melee weapon | | Melee_Weapons.html] that are thrown outwards when used and return to the player after a short amount time. They have a much greater range than most other types of melee weapons but are not considered [link ranged | | Ranged_Weapons.html] due to not requiring any [link ammunition].

The best [link modifier | | Modifiers.html] for all boomerangs is Godly. Stackable boomerangs cannot receive any modifiers.
