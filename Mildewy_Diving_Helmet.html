Mildewy Diving Helmet

The Mildewy Diving Helmet is a Hardmode accessory that is dropped by the Lost Diver in Master Mode. While this is equipped, the player will be able to transform into a mildew carrion for 25 seconds after double-tapping the Down button, which provides the player with 50% more defense and damage. While transformed, the player will not be able to fly or benefit from any movement accessories other than Super Turbo Reel and its upgrades, which will increase the pulling strength of the carrion-form's claws. Once the duration of the transformation has elapsed, the player will go on a 25-second cooldown before the ability can be used again.
