Harpoon Gun

[statblock Harpoon_Gun]

If you were looking for the article with a list of all harpoon guns added to Origins, [link click here | $default | Harpoon_Guns.html].

The Harpoon Gun is a Pre-Hardmode [link weapon | $default | Weapons.html] that has a 1/200 (0.5%) chance of being dropped by most enemies during a Goblin Army. It is functionally the same as the vanilla Harpoon it replaces except that it now requires [link harpoons] for [link ammunition]. The harpoon gun and weapons like it have a small chance of a line and harpoon being lost when colliding with a tile or enemy.

Its best [link Modifier | $default | Modifiers.html] is Godly or Demonic.
