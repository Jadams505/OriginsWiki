Cryosten Armor is an armor set that includes the Cryosten Helmet, Cryosten Breastplate, and Cryosten Greaves. The armor set focuses on the overall heartiness of the player by providing a boost to max life, life regeneration, and heart pick-ups. The greaves additionally provide a slight boost in mobility which is stronger when on ice.

Crafting a full set of Cryosten armor requires 135 ice, 24 shiverthorn and 3 Life Crystals in total.
