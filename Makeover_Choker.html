Makeover Choker

[statblock Makeover_Choker]

The Makeover Choker is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining [link Cross Necklace | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Cross_necklace] and [link Mysterious Spray]. Equipping it increases the length of [link invincibility | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Invincibility_frame] the player has after taking damage and inversely increases their [link life regeneration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Health_regeneration] as their current [link life | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Health] decreases. Like all of [link Mysterious Spray]'s variants, the Makeover Choker applies a unique visual effect on the player depending on their current health.

Notes

- Makeover Choker's regeneration rate is inversely logarithmic to the player's current health.
- Makeover Choker has a 1-second ramp up period after taking damage.

Tips

- It is still possible to die with [link Mysterious Spray] and its variants, by recieving quick bursts of damage similar to what it takes to slay a regenerating [link Defiled | | Defiled_Wastelands.html] enemy.

Trivia

- Although unintentional, it could be assumed the trace amounts of [link Black Bile] present in Mysterious Spray transformed the Cross Necklace into an edgier version of itself.
