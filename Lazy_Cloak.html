Lazy Cloak

[statblock Lazy_Cloak]

The Lazy Cloak is a Hardmode accessory that is dropped by Severus in Master Mode. While equipped, the Lazy Cloak will go after any enemies tagged by the player and inflict the Ichor debuff. There is a unique visual effect on the player that can be turned off while equipped on the player. Additionally, the cloak will not be visibly worn by the player while it is chasing an enemy.
