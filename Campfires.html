Campfires

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla campfires, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Campfires].

[statblock Campfires]

Campfires are functional [link furniture] items that emit light and provide the Cozy Fire [link health regeneration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Health_regeneration] [link buff | | Buffs.html] to players nearby them. A Campfire can be turned off by pressing the Open/Activate on the object or by sending it a [link wire | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Wire] signal, and can be toggled back on again the same way.
