Wilting Rose

[statblock Wilting_Rose]

The Wilting Rose is a [link herb | | Herbs.html] grown on [link Defiled Grass], [link Defiled Jungle Grass] and [link Defiled Stone] [link blocks] found around the [link Defiled Wastelands]. It is the Defiled-equivalent to [link Deathweed | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Deathweed]. Wilting Roses are currently unable to be grown from their own [link seed | | Seeds.html].
