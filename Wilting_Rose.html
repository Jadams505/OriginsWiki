Wilting Rose

The Wilting Rose is a herb grown on Defiled Grass, Defiled Jungle Grass and Defiled Stone blocks found around the Defiled Wastelands. It is the Defiled-equivalent to Deathweed. Wilting Roses are currently unable to be grown from their own seed.
