Petrified Tree

[statblock Petrified_Tree]

Petrified Trees are a variant of [link trees | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Trees] grown on [link Defiled Grass] and [link Defiled Jungle Grass] [link blocks] found at the [link Defiled Wastelands]. Petrified Trees drop [link Endowood] as a dedicated wood item upon destruction and are chopped down using [link axes] or [link chainsaws]. Additionally, Defiled Trees have a 143/2000 (7.015%) chance to drop a [link Bileberry] or [link Prickly Pear] in normal conditions. During the [link Halloween | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Halloween] event, these chances are slightly reduced to a 34073/500000 (6.8146%). [link Petrified Prickly Pears | | Petrified_Prickly_Pear.html] also have a 3/100 (3%) chance to drop from Petrified Tress on top of all of that.
