[statblock Fabricator]

The Fabricator is a post-[link Mech | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mechanical_bosses] [link crafting station | | Crafting_Stations.html] that is sold by the [link Mechanic | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mechanic] [link NPC | | NPCs.html] after completing her 'Mile Wide, Tile Deep' [link quest | | Quests.html]. It is used to craft a lot of useful, technology-themed items post-Mech or later.
