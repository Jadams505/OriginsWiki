Cursed Harpoon

[statblock Cursed_Harpoon]

The Cursed Harpoon is a type of Hardmode [link harpoon | $default | Harpoon_Variants.html] used as [link ammunition] for [link harpoon guns]. The cursed harpoon inflicts Cursed Inferno upon striking enemies and when it is reeled in. Firing high and then reeling in a cursed harpoon is an effective way to damage multiple enemies in a row.
