Nova Monolith

The Nova Monolith is a functional furniture item crafted using Nova Fragments at an Ancient Manipulator. When placed, it can be toggled with the Open/Activate button or from wire like other mechanisms. When activated, the screen and background effects for the Nova Pillar and its preceding lunar event. Like the other monoliths, it can be equipped as a vanity accessory to enable the effect globally for the player.
