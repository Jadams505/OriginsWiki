Cubekon Altar

[statblock Cubekon_Altar]

The Cubekon Altar is found naturally placed in the final chamber of the [link Cubekon Temple], and is used with a [link Quantum Cell] to summon the [link Quantux] [link boss | | Bosses.html]. Each summoning consumes one Lihzahrd Power Cell from the player's [link inventory | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Inventory]. The Cubekon Altar requires a [link pickaxe power | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pickaxe_power] of 250% or greater in order to mine it.
