Music Boxes

Music Boxes are a Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that can be worn by the player to record background music and play it after being placed. The Terraria Origins mod adds music boxes of its own for every theme composed in the [link music mod | $default | Origins_Music_Mod.html] plus extras composed at the [link developer's | $default | Developers.html] leisure. When the Origins Music mod is not enabled, all of the music boxes instead play vanilla equivalents.
