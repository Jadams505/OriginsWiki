Music Boxes

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla music boxes, click here.

Music Boxes are a Hardmode accessory that can be worn by the player to record background music and play it after being placed. The Terraria Origins mod adds music boxes of its own for every theme composed in the music mod plus extras composed at the developer's leisure. When the Origins Music mod is not enabled, all of the music boxes instead play vanilla equivalents.
