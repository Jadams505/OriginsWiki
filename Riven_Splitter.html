Riven Splitter

[statblock Riven_Splitter]

The Riven Splitter is a Pre-Hardmode [link harpoon gun | $default | Harpoon_Guns.html]. It is one of the possible drops from destroying a [link Riven Lesion] or [link Gel Blister]. It is normally obtained alongside 99 [link Harpoons | $default | Harpoon.html] to be used from the harpoon gun.

Its best [link modifier | $default | Modifiers.html] is Godly or Demonic.
