Link Grenade

[statblock Link_Grenade]

The Link Grenade is an explosive that can be crafted using Grenades and Sanguinite Ore. They are also sold by the Demolitionist NPC for [price 3|0] after selling him 85 Peat Moss. Link Grenades have a 20 second fuse time and will only detonate if another explosive detonates nearby or after the fuse time has elapsed.
