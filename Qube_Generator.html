Qube Generator

[statblock Qube_Generator]

The Qube Generator is a giant and indestructible, functional [link furniture] item that is placed in its dedicated room of the [link Cubekon Temple]. It is the sole, infinite source of [link Qubes | | Qube.html], an item necessary for some end-game equipment. The Qube Generator places a Qube in its center every five seconds if there is room.

Notes

- The Qube Generator is actually two different structures placed parallel to each other such that a [link Qube] can be properly placed without being impeded by the Generator.

Tips

- Proper protection is recommended for any meaningful usage of the Qube Generator due to the time demand.
