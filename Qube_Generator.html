Qube Generator

The Qube Generator is a giant and indestructible, functional furniture item that is placed in its dedicated room of the Cubekon Temple. It is the sole, infinite source of Qubes, an item necessary for some end-game equipment. The Qube Generator places a Qube in its center every five seconds if there is room. Additonally, Qubes will not be generated until Moon Lord has been defeated.

Notes

- The Qube Generator is actually two different structures placed parallel to each other such that a Qube can be properly placed without being impeded by the Generator.

Tips

- Proper protection is recommended for any meaningful usage of the Qube Generator due to the time demand.
