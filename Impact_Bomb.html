Impact Bomb

[statblock Impact_Bomb]

The Impact Bomb is an [link explosive | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] that can be crafted using [link Bombs | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bomb] and [link Peat Moss] or purchased from the [link Demolitionist | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Demolitionist] [link NPC | | NPCs.html] after selling 35 Peat Moss to him. The Impact Bomb has an increased [link velocity | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Velocity] from regular bombs and detonates after colliding with a tile or enemy, or after 3 seconds.
