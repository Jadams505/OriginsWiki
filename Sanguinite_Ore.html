Sanguinite Ore

Sanguinite Ore is a type of pre-Hardmode ore block found in small veins Underground or in larger quantities from the Eye of Cthulhu and Scrapper. It is the Ashen-equivalent to the Corruption's Demonite Ore or the Crimson's Crimtane Ore. Sanguinite Ore requires at least 55% pickaxe power to mine, and is exclusive to worlds with the Ashen present.

Sanguinite Ore emits a faint orange light in the dark.
