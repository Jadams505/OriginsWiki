Peat Moss is a pre-Hardmode material that is used to craft some early game [link Demolitionist Weapons]. It is harvested from [link Peat] blocks found in the Jungle biome,
especially on the surface of the [link Brine Pool]. It can be grown on any Mud tile next to a water source whilst in a Jungle biome. It is also crafted into
[link Explosive Powder] for advanced crafting.
