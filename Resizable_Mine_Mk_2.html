Resizable Mine Mk. 2

[statblock Resizable_Mine_Two]

The Resizable Mine Mk. 2 is a type of Pre-Hardmode [link mine | $default | Mines.html] used as [link ammunition] for [link mine launchers | $default | Mine_Launchers.html] and the Proximity Mine Launcher. Unlike other ammo types, most of the damage from mine launchers comes from the mines themselves.
