Harpoon

[statblock Harpoon]

Not to be confused with the vanilla Harpoon item, which is replaced by this in Origins.

The Harpoon is a type of pre-Hardmode ammunition used for harpoon guns. It can be shot and reeled back into the gun, often serving as a highly-reusable resource. This item is considered the regular variant among various harpoon types.
