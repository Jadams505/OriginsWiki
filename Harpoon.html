Harpoon

[statblock Harpoon]

Not to be confused with the vanilla Harpoon item, which is replaced by this in Origins.

The Harpoon is a type of Pre-Hardmode [link ammunition] used for [link harpoon guns]. It can be shot and reeled back into the gun, often serving as a highly-reusable resource. This item is considered the regular [link variant | $default | Harpoon_Variants.html] among various harpoon types.
