Mysterious Spray

[statblock Mysterious_Spray]

Mysterious Spray is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is obtained from the Treasure Bag dropped by the [link Defiled Amalgamation] in [link Master Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Master_Mode]. It inversely increases the player's life regeneration as their current life decreases. Mysterious Spray applies a unique visual effect on the player depending on their current health.

Notes

- Mysterious Spray's regeneration rate is inversely logarithmic to the player's current health.

Tips

- It is still possible to die from [link Mysterious Spray] and its variants by recieving quick bursts of damage similar to what it takes to slay a regenerating [link Defiled | | Defiled_Wastelands.html] enemy.

Trivia

- Mysterious Spray was once an [link Expert Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Expert_Mode] exclusive item rather than its current Master-exclusivity. It was moved to Master after the [link developers] of Origins playtested it in Master Mode and felt it was more balanced that way (at the time Mysterious Spray was impossible to get in any difficulty that wasnt Expert).
