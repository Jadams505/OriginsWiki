Alkaline Canister

[statblock Alkaline_Canister]

The Alkaline Canister is a type of [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link canister | | Canisters.html] used as [link ammunition] for [link launchers | | Launchers.html] and the [link Proximity Mine Launcher | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Proximity_Mine_Launcher]. Upon landing on a tile or enemy, the Alkaline Canister will explode into brine droplets that each have a small chance to inflict [link Toxic Shock].
