Surveysprout

[statblock Surveysprout]

Surveysprout is a [link herb | | Herbs.html] grown on [link Sootgrass] and [link Compacted Scrap] [link blocks] found around the [link Ashen Factory]. It is the Ashen-equivalent to [link Deathweed | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Deathweed]. Surveysprouts are currently unable to be grown from their own [link seed | | Seeds.html].
