Internal Combustion

[statblock Internal_Combustion]

The Internal Combustion is an [link explosive | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] that can be [link fished | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Fishing] for in the [link Ashen Factory]. It is currently unobtainable through conventional means, however, and can only be obtained through [link Shimmer | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Shimmer]. When thrown, it will home to nearby enemies and stick to them and only explode after 5 seconds or when another enemy is nearby the stuck enemy. It will drop after detonation and can be infinitely reused.
