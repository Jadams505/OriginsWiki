Left Click

[statblock Left_Click]

The Left Click is a Hardmode [link harpoon gun | $default | Harpoon_Guns.html] that has a 1/99 (1.01%) chance of being received by the [link Defiled Loot Box] dropped by enemies during the [link Defiled Invasion] event. When using regular [link harpoons | $default | Harpoon.html], a random mouse-pointer projectile has a chance of being fired by this weapon. The loading icon inflicts [link Rasterized] for 2 seconds, the select icon inflicts Ichor, the drag icon pulls enemies toward the player, and the x-icon guarantees a critical hit.

Its best [link modifier | $default | Modifiers.html] is Godly or Demonic.
