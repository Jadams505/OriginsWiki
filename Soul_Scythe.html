Soul Scythe

[statblock Soul_Scythe]

The Soul Scythe is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that has a 1/2 (50%) chance of being dropped by the Treasure Bag dropped by the Eater of Worlds in [link Expert Mode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Expert_Mode]. When equipped, ghastly blades will be launched at nearby enemies. This item shares its loot pool with the Worm Scarf.

Trivia

- The Soul Scythe is a nod to the removed item from the vanilla game with the same name.
