Powerflower

[statblock Powerflower]

Powerflower is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining a Mana Flower with a [link Dim Starlight]. The Powerflower offers an ample supply of mana for mana-hungry builds by releasing stars from critical hits, reducing mana consumption by 8% and automatically using Mana Potions when needed. Like the [link Dim Starlight] and its other upgrades, the Powerflower emits light.
