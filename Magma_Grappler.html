Magma Grappler

[statblock Magma_Grappler]

The Magma Grappler is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link harpoon gun | | Harpoon_Guns.html] crafted with [link Hellstone Bars | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hellstone_Bar] and a [link Harpoon Gun]. Enemies struck by harpoons fired by this weapon are inflicted with [link On Fire! | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/On_Fire!] anywhere from 6 to 8 seconds.

Its best [link modifier | | Modifiers.html] is Godly or Demonic.
