Magma Grappler

The Magma Grappler is a pre-Hardmode harpoon gun crafted with Hellstone Bars and a Harpoon Gun. Enemies struck by harpoons fired by this weapon are inflicted with On Fire! anywhere from 6 to 8 seconds.

Its best modifier is Godly or Demonic.
