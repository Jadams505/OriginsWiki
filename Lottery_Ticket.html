Lottery Ticket

[statblock Lottery_Ticket]

The Lottery Ticket is a [link consumable | | Consumables.html] item that is sold by the [link Merchant | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Merchant] [link NPC | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/NPCs] after completing his 'Stray of Luck' [link quest | | Quests.html]. While in the player's [link inventory | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Inventory] it will increase double the drop rate of rare items from any enemies nearby the player. It will disappear from the player's inventory when it causes an item to drop.
