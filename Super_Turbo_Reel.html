Super Turbo Reel

[statblock Super_Turbo_Reel]

The Super Turbo Reel is a pre-Hardmode accessory combining Magiluminescence and the Turbo Reel. While equipped, the player has 15% increased acceleration and a 75% increase in deceleration, which stacks with the bonus of the Hermes Boots and their variants. Additionally, the returning speed of shot Harpoons is increased by 2.
