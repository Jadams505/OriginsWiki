Super Turbo Reel

[statblock Super_Turbo_Reel]

The Super Turbo Reel is a Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] combining Magiluminescence and the [link Turbo Reel]. While equipped, the player has 15% increased acceleration and a 75% increase in deceleratio, which stacks with the bonus of the Hermes Boots and their variants. Additionally, the returning speed of shot [link Harpoons | $default | Harpoon_Guns.html] is increased by 2.
