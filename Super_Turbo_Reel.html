Super Turbo Reel

[statblock Super_Turbo_Reel]

The Super Turbo Reel is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining [link Magiluminescence | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Magiluminescence] and the [link Turbo Reel]. While equipped, the player has 15% increased acceleration and a 75% increase in deceleration, which stacks with the bonus of the [link Hermes Boots | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hermes_Boots] and their variants. Additionally, the returning speed of shot [link Harpoons | | Harpoon_Guns.html] is increased by 2.
