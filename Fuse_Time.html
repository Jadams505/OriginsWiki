Comb

[statblock Comb]

The Comb is a rare Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that is guaranteed to drop from The Groom. Equipping it grants a 3% boost to all damage types. It is the offensive-equivalent to the Shackle.
