Chew Toy

[statblock Chew_Toy]

The Chew Toy is a developer pet item that is currently only given to the player upon [link world | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/World] generation when to any players named "Chee". It summons a Chee Toy to accompany the player, which appears as a worm-like alien creature. The Chee Toy also speaks to the player within certain contexts.
