Potato Battery

[statblock Potato_Battery]

The Potato Battery is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] crafted using mech components dropped by the mechanical bosses. Equipping the Potato Battery causes all magic projectiles to home towards enemies. The Potato Battery talks to the player similarly to Lucy the Axe.

Dialogue

When picked up:
- "Oh. Hi."
When any bird is nearby:
- "I think I hear the bird! Pick me up!"
- "Agh! Bird! Bird! Kill it! It's evil!"
Player death:
- "[clap clap clap]"
- "[clap clap]"
- "Uh oh."
- "You really do have brain damage, don't you?"
In combat:
- "BURN HIS HOUSE DOWN!"
- "Hey! Moron!"
- "It's hard to see, what's that in your hand? Knowing you it's a deep fryer."
When Moon Lord appears:
- "Well. This is the part where he kills us."
- "Oh no..."
