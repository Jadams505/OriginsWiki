Potato Battery

[statblock Potato_Battery]

The Potato Battery is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] crafted using mech components dropped by the [link mechanical bosses | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mechanical_bosses]. Equipping the Potato Battery causes all magic projectiles to home towards enemies. The Potato Battery talks to the player similarly to [link Lucy the Axe | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Lucy_the_Axe].

Dialogue

When picked up: - "Oh. Hi." When any [link bird | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Birds] is nearby: - "I think I hear the bird! Pick me up!" - "Agh! Bird! Bird! Kill it! It's evil!" Player death: - "[clap clap clap]" - "[clap clap]" - "Uh oh." - "You really do have brain damage, don't you?" In combat: - "BURN HIS HOUSE DOWN!" - "Hey! Moron!" - "It's hard to see, what's that in your hand? Knowing you it's a deep fryer." When [link Moon Lord | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Moon_Lord] appears: - "Well. This is the part where he kills us." - "Oh no..."
