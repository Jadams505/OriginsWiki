Boomerang Magnet

[statblock Boomerang_Magnet]

The Boomerang Magnet is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] combining a Treasure Magnet and the [link Turbo Reel]. The Boomerang Magnet increases the return speed of boomerangs and [link harpoon guns | $default | Harpoon_Guns.html] while also increasing the pick-up range for items.
