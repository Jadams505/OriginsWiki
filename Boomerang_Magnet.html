Boomerang Magnet

[statblock Boomerang_Magnet]

The Boomerang Magnet is a pre-Hardmode accessory combining the Treasure Magnet and the Turbo Reel. The Boomerang Magnet increases the return speed of boomerangs and harpoon guns while also increasing the pickup range for items.
