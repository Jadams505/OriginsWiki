Item synergies

Item synergies are generally a combination of [link accessories], [link armor], [link consumables] and [link weapons] that all benefit each other in some kind of way as to promote a unique way of playing the game. Some item synergies are more powerful than others and some offer completely different purposes during gameplay. Below is a list of all notable synergies, their strategies and their methods of achieving.
