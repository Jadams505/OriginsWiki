Riven Cactus

The Riven Cactus is a variant of the [link Cactus | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Cactus] grown on [link Silica] [link blocks] found at a [link Desert | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Desert] converted by the [link Riven | | Riven_Hive.html]. Unlike the vanilla
equivalents, the Riven Cactus drops its very own item, but it is functionally the same as its vanilla counterparts.
