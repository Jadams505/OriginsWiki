Grave Danger

[statblock Grave_Danger]

Grave Danger is a [link Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] shield [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that has a 1/40 (2.5%) chance of being dropped by any enemies that spawn in the Graveyard biome. It provides 5 flat defense at all times and a 25% boost to the player's defense after death. On hardcore difficulty, Grave Danger instead provides the player with a 15% reduction to all damage with the flat 5 defense boost.

Notes

- Using a [link Candlelight] while Grave Danger is equipped causes a unique entity to spawn that will follow the player.
