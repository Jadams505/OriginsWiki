Grave Danger

[statblock Grave_Danger]

Grave Danger is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link shield | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Shields] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that has a 1/40 (2.5%) chance of being dropped by any enemies that spawn in the [link Graveyard | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Graveyard] biome. It provides 5 flat defense at all times and a 25% boost to the player's defense after [link death | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Death]. On [link Hardcore | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Difficulty#Hardcore] difficulty, Grave Danger instead provides the player with a 15% [link reduction | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Damage_reduction] to all damage with the flat 5 defense boost.

Notes

- Using a [link Candlelight] while Grave Danger is equipped causes a unique entity to spawn that will follow the player.
