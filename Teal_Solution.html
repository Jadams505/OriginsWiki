Teal Solution

[statblock Teal_Solution]

The Teal Solution is a type of [link ammunition] used solely by the Clentaminator and Terraformer. The gray solution will convert certain [link blocks] and [link walls] to their [link Riven | $default | Riven_Hive.html] counterparts, and is sold by the Steampunker during a Blood Moon or Solar Eclipse in a Riven world.
