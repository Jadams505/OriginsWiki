[statblock Teal_Solution]

The Teal Solution is a type of [link ammunition] used solely by the [link Clentaminator | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Clentaminator] and [link Terraformer | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Terraformer]. It will convert certain [link blocks] and [link walls] to their [link Riven | | Riven_Hive.html] counterparts, and is sold by the [link Steampunker | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Steampunker] during a [link Blood Moon | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Blood_Moon] or [link Solar Eclipse | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Solar_Eclipse] in a Riven world.
