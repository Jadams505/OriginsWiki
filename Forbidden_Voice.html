Forbidden Voice

[statblock Forbidden_Voice]

The Forbidden Voice is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] dropped by the Treasure Bag dropped by the Eater of Worlds in [link Master Mode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Master_Mode]. While the [link cursed] version is equipped, the player is silenced, but the mouth will shout at enemies dealing considerable damage and knockback. While the normal version is equipped, just allows the player to spit at enemies inflicting Weak and moderate damage.
