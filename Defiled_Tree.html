Defiled Trees

Defiled Trees are a variant of < a href="https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Trees">trees in Terraria that regularly spawn on the surface of the world. Unlike all other trees, Defiled trees cannot be chopped down or destoryed by lava. They must be destroyed with a pickaxe and require a pickaxe power of at least 65%. They can also be blown up with explosives. Destroying a Defiled tree will provide a lot of [link Defiled Stone] and more acorns to grow more trees. Defiled trees do not bend in the wind and cannot be shaken due to being comprised of Defiled matter and spirits.
