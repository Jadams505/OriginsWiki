Properties and Lore

Undead Chunks

Undead Chunks are actually the strong-enough bond of multiple Defiled Spirits to serve as a crafting component for the Terrarian. They act as a node
that binds together all of the chunks, serving as a kind of glue. The Terrarians creations cannot be commanded by the Defiled without an nervous system to
take hold.
