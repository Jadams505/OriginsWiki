Explosive Harpoon

[statblock Explosive_Harpoon]

The Explosive Harpoon is a type of [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link harpoon | $default | Harpoon_Variants.html] used as [link ammunition] for [link harpoon guns]. The explosive harpoon explodes when the harpoon is broken from the chain, making it more effective with faster firing harpoon guns with lower ammo conservation rates such as the [link Boat Rocker].
