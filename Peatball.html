Peatball

[statblock Peatball]

Peatballs are consumable thrown [link explosives | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] crafted from [link Peat Moss]. They are also sold by the [link Demolitionist | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Demolitionist] [link NPC | | NPCs.html] for [price 3|75] after selling him 15 [link Peat Moss]. Peatballs can additionally be used as [link ammunition] for the Snowball Cannon. Peatballs explode on impact and have a comparatively large blast radius.
