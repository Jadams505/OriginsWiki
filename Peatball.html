Peatball

Peatballs are consumable, thrown explosives crafted from Peat Moss. They are also sold by the Demolitionist NPC for [price 3|75] after selling him 15 Peat Moss. Peatballs can additionally be used as ammunition for the Snowball Cannon. Peatballs explode on impact and have a comparatively large blast radius.
