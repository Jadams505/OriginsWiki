Cages

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla cages, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Cages].

[statblock Cages]

Cages are decorative [link furniture] items that display animated [link critters]. Any critter caught with a [link Bug Net | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bug_Net] or equivalent can be combined with a [link Terrarium | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Terrarium] to craft a cage.
