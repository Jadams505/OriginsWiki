Rope of Sharing

[statblock Rope_Of_Sharing]

The Rope of Sharing is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that has a 1/50 (2%) chance of being dropped by any Zombie. While equipped, the player can choose one other entity to share relevant stats with. Relevant stats depends on the entity the player chooses, which can be another player or NPC.

Relevant stats include:
- Life
- Mana
- Damage multipliers
- Accesory effects
