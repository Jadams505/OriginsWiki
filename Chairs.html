Chairs

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla chairs, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Chairs].

[statblock Chairs]

Chairs are decorative [link furniture] items that fulfill a [link house | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/House]'s [link comfort item | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/House#Comfort_items] requirement. In combination with a [link Work Bench | | Work_Benches.html] or a [link Table | | Tables.html], they also form minor crafting stations. Players can sit in chairs by using the Open/Activate button on them and can receive a boost in [link health regeneration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Health_regeneration] similar to [link beds] and [link sofas]. [link NPCs] can sit on chairs too.
