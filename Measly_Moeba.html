Measly Moeba

The Measly Moeba is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Riven Hive. It sneakily drifts in the glowing water of Riven Hives remaining somewhat translucent and hard to spot. It damages the player if they are submerged and get too close, but it will not move quickly as to conceal itself.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Measly Moeba: "Small and nearly undetectable in its natural habitat of the pools of the Riven Hive, this variant of the Primordial Amoeba swims to facilitate the process of assimilation or digestion."
