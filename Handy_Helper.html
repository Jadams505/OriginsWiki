Handy Helper

[statblock Handy_Helper]

The Handy Helper is a Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] combining the Power Glove with the [link Amebic Vial]. While equipped, gloved amebic tentacles will protect you from projectiles and push away enemies that get too close. Additionally, the Handy Helper doubles melee knockback, increases melee speed by 12%, enables auto-swing on all melee weapons and whips, and increases the size of all melee weapons by 10%. Handy Helper will behave differently depending on the modifiers it has. Similarly to [link Amebic Vial], there is a unique visual effect on the player that can be turned off while equipped on the player.

The tentacles have a range of 4.4 tiles (71 units) at which they will protect the player and have a cooldown of 1.67 seconds before they are deployed again.
