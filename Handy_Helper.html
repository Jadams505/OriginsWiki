Handy Helper

[statblock Handy_Helper]

The Handy Helper is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining the [link Power Glove | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Power_Glove] with the [link Amebic Vial]. While equipped, gloved amebic tentacles will protect the player from projectiles and [link push away | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Knockback] enemies that get too close. Additionally, the Handy Helper doubles melee knockback, increases melee speed by 12%, enables [link auto-swing | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Autoswing] on all [link melee weapons] and [link whips], and increases the size of all melee weapons by 10%. Similarly to [link Amebic Vial], there is a unique visual effect on the player that can be turned off while equipped on the player.

The tentacles have a range of 4.4 tiles (71 units) at which they will protect the player and have a cooldown of 1.67 seconds before they are deployed again.
