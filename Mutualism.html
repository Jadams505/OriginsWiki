Mutualism

"The Dryad is not supportive of this... AT ALL." - [link Mimic Armor] tooltip

The Mutualism mechanic as of now only applies to the Mimic-Defiled Spread gimmick that is vital to the [link Mimic Armor] playstyle. Mutualism is the mechanic that
allows the player to grow with the [link Defiled Wastelands] meaning the spread of the Defiled in the world has a direct boost on the player as well. More about the
Mutualism mechanic can be explained in the [link Mimic Armor] page.
