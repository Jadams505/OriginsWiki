Asylum Whistle

[statblock Asylum_Whistle]

The Asylum Whistle is a Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] found in Locked Gold Chests stowed away in the Dungeon. It allows the player to tag two different enemies at once whilst increasing minion damage by 10%.

The Asylum Whistle is a [link lore item | $default | Lore_Item.html] indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."
