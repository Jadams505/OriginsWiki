Superjump Cape

[statblock Superjump_Cape]

The Superjump Cape is a Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that has a 1/3 (33%) chance of dropping from the Halloween-exclusive Superman Zombie. It gives the player incredible airtime while also providing immunity to fall damage serving as an alternative to wings.

Tips

- To get the most out of the Superjump Cape, jump right after building momentum to cover a large distance.

Trivia

- The Superjump Cape is inspired by the cape worn by the fictional character Super Brainz in the Plants vs Zombies franchise.
