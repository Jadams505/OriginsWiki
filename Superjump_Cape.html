Superjump Cape

[statblock Superjump_Cape]

The Superjump Cape is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that has a 1/3 (33%) chance of dropping from the [link Halloween-exclusive | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Halloween] [link Superman Zombie | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Zombie#Variants]. It gives the player incredible airtime while also providing immunity to fall damage serving as an alternative to [link wings | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Wings].

Tips

- To get the most out of the Superjump Cape, jump right after building momentum to cover a large distance.

Trivia

- The Superjump Cape is inspired by the cape worn by the fictional character [link Super Brainz | | https://plantsvszombies.fandom.com/wiki/Super_Brainz] in the Plants vs Zombies franchise.
