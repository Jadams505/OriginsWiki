Blotopus

[statblock Blotopus]

The Blotopus is a Pre-Hardmode gun that can be fished for in the Crimson with a 1/10 (10%) chance with 100 Fishing Power, up to a maximum 1/4 (25%) chance with 300 Fishing Power. It is the Crimson-equivalent to the Corruption's Purple Clubberfish. It rapidly fires needles out of its mouth that are affected by gravity and inflict Bleeding.

Its best [link modifier | $default | Modifiers.html] is Demonic or Deadly.

Tips

- The Blotopus has a higher overall DPS than the Minishark, however it burns through ammo faster despite its 20% ammo conservation rate.
