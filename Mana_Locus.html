Mana Locus

[statblock Mana_Locus]

The Mana Locus is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode], [link Expert Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Expert_Mode]-exclusive, post-[link Golem | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Golem] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining the [link Celestial Starlight] and [link Focus Crystal]. While equipped, the player's [link life regeneration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Health_regeneration] is boosted depending on damage the player deals, their [link critical strike | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Critical_hit] chance is increased by 15% of the current weapon's base damage, and weapon damage is increased while the player stands still. Additionally, the [link pickup range | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Items#Pickup] for [link Stars | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Star] is increased and are prompted to generate from [link critical hits | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Critical_hit]. It also provides a bright source of light and star-like particle effects around the player as well.
