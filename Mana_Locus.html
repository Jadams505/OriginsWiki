Mana Locus

[statblock Mana_Locus]

The Mana Locus is a Hardmode, Expert Mode-exclusive, post-Golem accessory combining the Celestial Starlight and Focus Crystal. While equipped, the player's life regeneration is boosted depending on damage the player deals, their critical strike chance is increased by 15% of the current weapon's base damage, and weapon damage is increased while the player stands still. Additionally, the pickup range for Stars is increased and are prompted to generate from critical hits. It also provides a bright source of light and star-like particle effects around the player as well.
