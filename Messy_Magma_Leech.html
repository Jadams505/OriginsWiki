Messy Magma Leech

The Messy Magma Leech is a Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] combining a Magma Stone and a [link Messy Leech]. While equipped, all attacks from the player inflict the Bleeding and On Fire! debuffs.
