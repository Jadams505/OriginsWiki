The Developers

This page is dedicated to the developers of the Terraria Origins mod. The developers will each share whatever they would like people to know,
hence the inconsistent formatting.

chee>

- Hello my name on the internet is "chee" and I'm an aspiring freelance video game developer working with the Origins Team to fulfill our dreams together.
I come from a background as a gamer enjoying a lot of different games such as Mechassault (my favorite game of all time). The idea of Origins came up in
my mind back in 2016 and at the time it was just an idea with a couple of early rendition sprites of some things in the mod. I eventually met my best friend
Tyftyer (was known as Terlet Paper at the time) that same year and it eventually came to the topic of how cool Terraria is, which transformed into us
beginning our partnership. For the following three years, we were just planning the mod and let it sit in the background, but around 2020 we started making
progress as we saw the Journey's End update had been coming around and that official TModLoader support would be brought into Steam. We've been very
dedicated to the mod ever since, but I've personally been quite seaonal with my progress (my work ethic involves me taking big breaks because I cram a lot
into a short time span). WIP...
