Suspicious Looking Ice Cream

The Suspicious Looking Ice Cream is a boss-summoning consumable item crafted using Sentient Powder and Bud Barnacle. Upon use, it will summon the World Cracker. If not used in the Riven Hive, it will do nothing and will not be consumed.
