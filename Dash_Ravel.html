Dash Ravel

[statblock Dash_Ravel]

Dash Ravel is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is crafted using a [link Tabi | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Tabi] and a [link Ravel]. Dash Ravel turns the player into a small sphere that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Dash Ravel allows the player to dash anytime while this is equipped, but with a small delay whenever the player is not already ravelled. Dash Ravel deals damage and knocks back enemies whenever it reaches top speed, which can be quickly reached by dashing.
