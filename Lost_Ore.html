Lost Ore is the Defiled counterpart to Demonite Ore and Crimtane Ore. Lost Ore emits a bright white glow when placed. Although it can be found very rarily in the caverns in a Defiled world, only the [link Defiled Amalgamation] and Eye of Cthulhu will drop usable amounts.

Lore

Lost Ore

Lost Ore acts as a catalyst for generating Defiled matter. Lost Ore itself is not inherently connected with the Defiled, but is used as a key resource needed to
maintain its infrastructure. Lost Ore is a natural resource of Terraria that was banished along with the Defiled in the [link Dusk] by a group of Dryads long ago.
