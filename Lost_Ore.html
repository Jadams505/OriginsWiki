Lost Ore

Lost Ore is a type of pre-Hardmode ore block found in small veins Underground or in larger quantities from the Eye of Cthulhu and Defiled Amalgamation. It can also be dropped in small amounts by Defiled Swarmers. It is the Defiled-equivalent to the Corruption's Demonite Ore or the Crimson's Crimtane Ore. Lost Ore requires at least 55% pickaxe power to mine, and is exclusive to worlds with the Defiled present.

Lost Ore emits a faint white light in the dark.
