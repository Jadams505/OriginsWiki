World Cracker

The World Cracker is a pre-Hardmode, Riven-themed worm boss. It is summoned when three Gel Blisters are destroyed in a Riven world, or when a player uses Suspicious Looking Ice Cream anywhere in the Riven Hive or Underground Riven Hive.

The World Cracker has a very simple behavior, however, it has a few quirks that make it challenging in of itself. It has a total of 19 segments in all difficulties including the head and tail, and it all counts as one unlike the Eater of Worlds. It will circle around the player and move to the opposite direction of the player making it unpredictable. While moving around it will shoot an amoeba bubble from any one of its segments that deals high damage. Furthermore, the World Cracker has armor on all of its segments that reduce damage until it is destroyed.

Defeating the World Cracker and destroying its armor are the only ways of obtaining Undead Chunks, and are one of the ways of obtaining usable quantities of Encrusted Ore alongside the Eye of Cthulhu. Defeating the World Cracker for the first time also allows the Tavernkeep NPC to spawn as the Unconcious Man, and allows meteorites to land.

Behavior

As previously stated, the World Cracker's behavior is simple. It behaves like most worms except that it prefers to attack in vertical strikes and moves opposite of the player. If the player doesn't move, it will instead go for a direct strike. It will also shoot an amoeba bubble at the player from any of its segments every .84 to 1.17s.

Difficulty Changes

- It rains in the world while the World Cracker is present.

Notes

- The World Cracker will pursue the player even outside of the Hive.

Tips

- Focus on speed when preparing for the World Cracker as it attacks vertically, allowing you to easy sidestep it with enough acceleration.

Trivia

- The Destroyer is the Hardmode mechanical boss equivalent of the World Cracker alongside the Eater of Worlds.
