Drill Ravel

[statblock Drill_Ravel]

Drill Ravel is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is crafted using a [link Ancient Chisel | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Ancient_Chisel] and a [link Ravel]. Drill Ravel turns the player into a small sphere, upon double-tapping the Down button, that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Drill Ravel also allows the player to utilize any [link pickaxe] or [link drill] in their [link inventory | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Inventory] with a 25% increase to their [link mining speed | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mining_speed].
