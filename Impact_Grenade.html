Impact Grenade

[statblock Impact_Grenade]

The Impact Grenade is an [link explosive | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] that can be crafted using [link Grenades | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Grenade] and [link Peat Moss] or purchased from the [link Demolitionist | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Demolitionist] [link NPC | | NPCs.html] after selling 40 Peat Moss to him. The Impact Grenade has an increased [link velocity | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Velocity] from regular grenades and detonates after colliding with a tile or enemy, or after 3 seconds.
