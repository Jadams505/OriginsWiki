Forest Staff

[statblock Forest_Staff]

The Forest Staff is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link summon weapon | | Summon_Weapon.html] that can be found in living wood chests inside of a [link Living Tree | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Living_Tree]. It summons a Teeny Truffle [link minion | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Summon_weapons#Minions] that attacks by approaching enemies until it can throw tree branches at them. The branches deal considerable damage while also inflicting the [link Bleeding | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bleeding] debuff.

Forest Staff's best [link modifier | | Modifiers.html] is Mythical.

Tips

- The Teeny Truffle's range is limited so the player may need to replace this minion often to maintain consistent damage.
- The Forest Staff is one of the few weapons in Origins that can inflict the [link Bleeding | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bleeding] debuff, which makes it synergize with items like [link Explosive Artery] quite well.

Trivia

- The [link Truffle | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Truffle] have unique interaction dialogue depending on whether or not the Teeny Truffle is present.
