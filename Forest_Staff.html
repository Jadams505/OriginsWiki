Forest Staff

[statblock Forest_Staff]

The Forest Staff is a pre-hardmode summon weapon that can be found in living wood chests inside of a Living Trees. It summons a Teeny Truffle minion that attacks by approaching enemies until it can throw tree branches at them. The branches deal considerable damage while also inflicting the Bleeding debuff.

Forest Staff's best modifiers are Mythical, Ruthless, or Godly. The Teeny Truffle deals good enough damage to greatly benefit from damage bonuses.

Notes

- The Teeny Truffle is a grounded minion and thus is not effective to use when the player is airborne.

Tips

- The Teeny Truffle's range is limited so the player may need to replace this minion often to maintain consistent damage.
- The Forest Staff is one of the few weapons in Origins that can inflict Bleeding, which makes it synergize with items like [link Explosive Artery] quite well.

Trivia

- The Truffle have unique interaction dialogue depending on whether or not the Teeny Truffle is present.
