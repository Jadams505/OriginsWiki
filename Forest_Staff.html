Forest Staff

[statblock Forest_Staff]

The Forest Staff is a pre-Hardmode summon weapon that can be found in living wood chests inside of a Living Tree. It summons a Teeny Truffle minion that attacks by approaching enemies until it can throw tree branches at them. The branches deal considerable damage while also inflicting the Bleeding debuff.

Forest Staff's best modifier is Mythical.

Tips

- The Teeny Truffle's range is limited so the player may need to replace this minion often to maintain consistent damage.
- The Forest Staff is one of the few weapons in Origins that can inflict the Bleeding debuff, which makes it synergize with items like Explosive Artery quite well.

Trivia

- The Truffle have unique interaction dialogue depending on whether or not the Teeny Truffle is present.
