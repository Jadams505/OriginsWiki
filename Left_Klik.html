Left Klik

The Left Klik is a Hardmode harpoon gun that has a 1/99 (1.01%) chance of being received by the Defiled Loot Box dropped by enemies during the Defiled Invasion event. When using regular harpoons, a random mouse-pointer projectile has a chance of being fired by this weapon. The loading icon inflicts Rasterized for 2 seconds, the select icon inflicts Ichor debuff, the drag icon pulls enemies toward the player, and the x-icon guarantees a critical hit.

Its best modifier is Godly or Demonic.
