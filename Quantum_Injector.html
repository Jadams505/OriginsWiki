Quantum Injector

[statblock Quantum_Injector]

The Quantum Injector is a [link consumable | | Consumables.html] item that is crafted a [link Mana Crystal | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mana_Crystal] and [link Qubes | | Qube.html]. When used, it will permanently increase the player's [link mana | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mana] capacity by 40, up to a maximum of 400 total. It takes 5 injections to reach this maximum. It also restores 40 mana when used, but only as long as the character's maximum mana is below 400.
