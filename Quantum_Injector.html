Quantum Injector

[statblock Quantum_Injector]

The Quantum Injector is a consumable item that is crafted a Mana Crystal and Qubes. When used, it will permanently increase the player's mana capacity by 40, up to a maximum of 400 total. It takes 5 injections to reach this maximum. It also restores 40 mana when used, but only as long as the character's maximum mana is below 400.
