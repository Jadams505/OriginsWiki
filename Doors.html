Doors

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla doors, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Doors].

[statblock Doors]

Doors are functional [link furniture] items that prevent [link enemies | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Enemies] from entering an area when closed. Players can open them by pressing the Open/Activate buttons on them. Doors can be placed in any space so long as it is exactly three blocks high, with ceiling and floor [link blocks]. Friendly [link NPCs] will also open and close doors during the day as they wander about.
