Riven Penguin

[statblock Riven_Penguin]

The Riven Penguin is an [link enemy | | Enemies.html] derived from a [link Penguin | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Penguin] that has been corrupted, either by the player using [link Sentient Powder] or by a [link Blood Moon | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Blood_Moon] event on a [link Riven | | Riven_Hive.html] world. The Riven Penguin behaves like its vanilla counterparts: the [link Corrupt Penguin | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Corrupt_Penguin] and [link Vicious Penguin | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Vicious_Penguin].

Notes

- Although they spawn during the Blood Moon event, they cannot drop [link Bloody Tears | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bloody_Tear].

Trivia

- The [link Bestiary | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bestiary] entry for the Riven Penguin: "During a Blood Moon, the Riven Hive is able to overcome its weakness of the cold and spread, forcing normally docile penguins to adapt."
