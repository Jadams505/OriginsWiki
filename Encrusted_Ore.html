Encrusted Ore

[statblock Encrusted_Ore]

Encrusted Ore is a type of pre-Hardmode ore block found in small veins Underground or in larger quantities from the Eye of Cthulhu and World Cracker. It is the Riven-equivalent to the Corruption's Demonite Ore or the Crimson's Crimtane Ore. Encrusted Ore requires at least 55% pickaxe power to mine, and is exclusive to worlds with the Riven present.

Encrusted Ore emits a faint blue light in the dark.
