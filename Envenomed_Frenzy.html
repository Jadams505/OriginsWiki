Envenomed Frenzy is a flail-like melee weapon that is dropped from the [link Mildew Carrion]. It casts out multiple Mildewy vines that whip around in a rapid and aggressive manner towards where it is aimed. It inflicts 'Acid Venom', 'Poisoned', and '[link Toxic Shock]'.
