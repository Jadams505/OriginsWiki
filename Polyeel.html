Polyeel

Polyeel are a type of fish which are only obtainable by Shimmer cycling its equivalents. It is the Ashen-equivalent to the Corruption's Ebonkoi or the Crimson's Hemopiranha. They are used to craft Seafood Dinners and Ambition Potions.
