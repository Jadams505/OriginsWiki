Wings

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla wings, click here.

Wings are accessories that allow the player to fly temporarily by pressing and holding the Jump key. Flight time is reset when the player rests on solid objects, including both standing on blocks and using climbing items like Ropes, hooks and climbing accessories. Certain wing types provide different movement capabilities for the player. After the player has run out of flight time, holding the Jump key allows the player to slowly glide to the ground. Gliding fall speed can be increased by holding the Down key.
