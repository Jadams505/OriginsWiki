Brineglow

[statblock Brineglow]

The Brineglow is a type of vine plant grown on [link Peat Moss] [link blocks] found inside the [link Brine Pool]. They cannot grow and will break off when not submerged, similarly to Peat Moss. Brineglow Vine bulbs give off a soft, blue light.
