The Bird

The Bird is a post-Moon Lord, melee developer weapon that can be crafted using a Baseball Bat and Razorwire for players named "Pandora". The Bird improves upon the Baseball Bat's knockback focused playstyle, but with improved combat performance. Using The Bird normally makes it attack in a horizontal swipe like its Baseball Bat predecessor, but using its alternate use function by using the RMB key launches enemies in the air. Knocking an enemy into a solid surface or another enemy will damage it and the enemy it collides with.

Trivia

- The Bird and "Pandora" are a reference to one of the developer's characters with the same name. The Bird is a carry-over item from the Startropic Adventures game.
