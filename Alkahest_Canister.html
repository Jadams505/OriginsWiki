Alkahest Canister

[statblock Alkahest_Canister]

The Alkahest Canister is a type of [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link canister | | Canisters.html] used as [link ammunition] for [link launchers | | Launchers.html] and the [link Proximity Mine Launcher | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Proximity_Mine_Launcher]. Upon landing on a tile or enemy, the Alkahest Canister will explode into many alkahest droplets, similar to [link Brainade], that each inflict [link Torn] of smaller severities. Enemies are inflicted with greater Torn depending on how many alkahest droplets hit them.
