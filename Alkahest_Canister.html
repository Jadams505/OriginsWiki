Alkahest Canister

[statblock Alkahest_Canister]

The Alkahest Canister is a type of Hardmode canister used as ammunition for launchers and the Proximity Mine Launcher. Upon landing on a tile or enemy, the Alkahest Canister will explode into many alkahest droplets, similar to Brainade, that each inflict Torn of smaller severities. Enemies are inflicted with greater Torn depending on how many alkahest droplets hit them.
