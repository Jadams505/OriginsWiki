Parasitic Influence is an [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that is dropped from a [link Riven Lesion]. Parasitic Influence gives the player a 27% chance to dash and hit an enemy within 9-tile radius. Using a Expert accessory Shield of Cthulhu on an enemy will always prompt the Parasitic Influence to strike that same enemy a second time.

Tips

- Parasitic Influence is very useful with the Expert accessory Shield of Cthulhu, which allows the player to dash at enemies and hurt them in a similar manner. This combo allows the player to double strike the same enemy dealing even more free damage.
