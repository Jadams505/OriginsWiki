Magic Pain Glove

[statblock Magic_Pain_Glove]

The Magic Pain Glove is a Hardmode accessory combining Feral Claws and a Magic Glove. The Glove increases melee attack speed by 12% and generates a random magic projectile while the player's hand is being swung. It also grants auto-use to all melee weapons and whips. Furthermore, it also provides an 8% reduction in mana consumption and allows the player to automatically consume mana-restoring potions in the their inventory.
