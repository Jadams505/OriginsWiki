Bleeding Obsidian Armor

[statblock Bleeding_Obsidian_Armor]

Bleeding Obsidian armor is an [link armor] set consisting of the Bleeding Obsidian Hardee, Bleeding Obsidian Trenchcoat and Bleeding Obsidian Chaps. In order to craft the full set the player will need the full [link Obsidian armor | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Obsidian_armor] set and 9 [link Bleeding Obsidian] [link blocks].

A full set of Bleeding Obsidian armor provides the following benefits:

- +26 [link defense | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Defense]
- +20% [link explosive | $default | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] [link velocity | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Velocity]
- -25% [link self-damage | $default | Self_Damage.html]
- +15% movement speed
- +2% acceleration/deceleration
Set bonus
- 50% chance to duplicate explosive projectiles
