Broken Record

The Broken Record is a boss-summoning consumable item crafted using Urns of Ashes and Biocomponent 10. It does not function like other boss-summoning items since its respective boss, the Scrapper, is currently not implemented. Instead it acts like a grab bag upon its use, dropping one of the following items:

- Ace Shrapnel accompanied by 200 Scrap
- Area Denial
- Neural Network accompanied by 100 Musket Balls
- Seal of Cinders
- Smith's Hammer
