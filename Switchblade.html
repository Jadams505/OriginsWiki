Switchblade

The Switchblade is a pre-Hardmode melee weapon that is crafted using Sanguinite Bars and NE-8. The sword's function can be switched by pressing the Interact button, changing between a shortsword and broadsword function. It deals more damage per second in shortsword form, but has more range in its broadsword form.
