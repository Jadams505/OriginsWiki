Alkahest Arrow

[statblock Alkahest_Arrow]

The Alkahest Arrow is a type of [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link arrow | | Arrows.html] fired by [link bows] or [link repeaters]. The arrows carry their namesake [link Alkahest] component inside of a glass tip, shattering on impact to release alkahest on the target. Alkahest arrows inflict [link Torn] with a 50% severity on impact.
