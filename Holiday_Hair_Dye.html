Holiday Hair Dye

[statblock Holiday_Hair_Dye]

The Holiday Hair Dye is a [link consumable | | Consumables.html] item that is sold by the [link Stylist | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Stylist] [link NPC | | NPCs.html] that is sold at all times. When used, it will apply a custom effect depending on the current holiday.

Notes

- Hair color will also change dynamically on the map/minimap.
- The Holiday Hair Dye does nothing on [link April Fools' Day | | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/April_Fools%27_Day].
