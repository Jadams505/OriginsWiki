Launchers

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla launchers, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Launchers].

Launchers are a type of [link explosive weapon | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] that use [link rockets], [link canisters] or [link Stynger Bolts | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Stynger_Bolt] as [link ammunition]. The ammo used by launchers generally explode on contact with
an [link enemy | | Enemies.html] or solid [link block | | Blocks.html], dealing heavy damage within the area of the explosion. Origins has added new types of ammunition for launchers to use.

The best [link modifier | | Modifiers.html] for Launchers is Guileless.
