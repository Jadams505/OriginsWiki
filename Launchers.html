Launchers

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla launchers, click here.

Launchers are a type of explosive weapon that use rockets, canisters or Stynger Bolts as ammunition. The ammo used by launchers generally explode on contact with an enemy or solid block, dealing heavy damage within the area of the explosion. Origins has added new types of ammunition for launchers to use.

The best modifier for Launchers is Guileless.
