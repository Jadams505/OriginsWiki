Launchers are a type of [link explosive weapon | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] use [link rockets], [link canisters], or Stynger Bolts as [link ammunition]. The ammo used by launchers generally explodes on contact with
an [link enemy | | Enemies.html] or solid [link block | | Blocks.html], dealing heavy damage within the area of the explosion. It may damage the player if not fired carefully. Origins has added new types of
ammunition for launchers to use.

The best [link modifier | | Modifiers.html] for Launchers is Guileless.
