Depth Ravel

[statblock Depth_Ravel]

Depth Ravel is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is crafted using the [link Arctic Diving Gear | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Arctic_Diving_Gear], a [link Flipper | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Flipper] and [link Ravel]. Depth Ravel turns the player into a small sphere that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Depth Ravel allows seamless movement and swimming while submerged, gives the player another 18 seconds of [link breath | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Breath_meter], and causes the player to generate light while ravelled.
