Primordial Soup is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] obtained by smashing a [link Riven Lesion] or [link Gel Blister]. When equipped, the player's [link life regeneration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Health_regeneration] will be increased depending on the current severity of [link Torn] being applied to them.
