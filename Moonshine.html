Moonshine

Moonshines are large plants which can be found growing naturally in the Dusk, and can be broken with a pickaxe or drill, or by coming into contact with water. They cannot be grown from seeds like most other plants, but are instead placed fully-grown, either naturally upon world generation or by the player. They provide the Empowered buff to players, enemies, and NPCs within a rectangular area of 10x12 tiles centered on the Moonshine, which heals for 2% of max life every second, and a 15% increase in enemy spawn rates for players.

Moonshine can be placed on any blocks, unlike Sunflowers.
