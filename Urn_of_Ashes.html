Urn of Ashes

[statblock Ash_Urn]

The Urn of Ashes is a [link consumable | | Consumables.html] item that can be crafted using [link Fungarust] or purchased from the [link Dryad | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Dryad] for [price 1|0] each during a [link Blood Moon | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Blood_Moon] in an Ashen world. Currently, the Urn of Ashes does not behave like other thrown powder and will not spread the [link Ashen | | Ashen_Factory.html] or turn certain enemies into their Ashen counterparts, and is soley used to craft.
