Helium Tank

[statblock Helium_Tank]

The Helium Tank is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining a Whoopie Cushion and an [link Air Tank]. While worn, it provides the player with immunity to [link Suffocation | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Suffocation] and an additional 25 seconds of [link breath | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Breath_meter]. It does nothing different from its original until the player takes damage at which it will make the player character's voice a higher pitch.
