Helium Tank

[statblock Helium_Tank]

The Helium Tank is a Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] combining a Whoopie Cushion and an [link Air Tank]. While worn, it provides the player with immunity to Suffocation and an additional 25 seconds of breath. It does nothing different from its original until the player takes damage at which it will make the player character's voice a higher pitch.
