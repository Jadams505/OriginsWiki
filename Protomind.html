Protomind

[statblock Protomind]

The Protomind is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining a [link Brain of Confusion | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Brain_of_Confusion] with the [link Potato Battery]. When equipped, enemies have a 3/5 (60%) chance of receiving the [link Confused | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Confused] [link debuff | | Debuffs.html] for a minimum of 1.5 seconds. The chance, duration, and area of effect of the debuff are based on the amount of damage taken. Additionally, all magic projectiles home towards enemies.

Furthermore, the player wielding it has a 1/6 (16.67%) chance of dodging an attack, which inflicts the [link Cerebral Mindtrick | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Brain_of_Confusion] debuff on them for 4 seconds. Despite being a debuff, it increases [link critical strike | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Critical_hit] chance and [link summon damage | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Damage#Summon] by 10%. While the debuff is active, the player cannot dodge another attack.
