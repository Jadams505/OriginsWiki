Batholith Grindstone

The Batholith Grindstone is a functional furniture item that serves as a direct upgrade to the Sharpening Station. Placing and pressing Open/Activate on the Batholith Grindstone grants the Vorpal buff, which increases the player's armor penetration while holding a melee weapon by 19. This lasts until player dies or leaves the world, but can be canceled anytime by right-clicking the icon. While the buff is active, the player cannot have the 'Sharpened' buff.
