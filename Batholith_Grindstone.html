Batholith Grindstone

[statblock Batholith_Grindstone]

The Batholith Grindstone is a functional [link furniture] item that serves as a direct upgrade to the [link Sharpening Station | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Sharpening_Station]. Placing and pressing Open/Activate on the Batholith Grindstone grants the Vorpal [link buff | | Buffs.html], which increases the player's [link armor penetration | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Defense#Armor_penetration] while holding a [link melee weapon | | Melee_Weapon.html] by 19. This lasts until player [link dies | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Death] or leaves the world, but can be canceled anytime by right-clicking the icon. While the buff is active, the player cannot have the 'Sharpened' buff.
