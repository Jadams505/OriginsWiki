Haggard Artery

[statblock Haggard_Artery]

Haggard Artery is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining an [link Explosive Artery] with a [link Messy Leech]. While equipped, enemies inflicted with Bleeding within 32 tiles of the player have an 6.67% chance to explode every second. Additionally, all enemies attacked by the player are inflicted Bleeding.
