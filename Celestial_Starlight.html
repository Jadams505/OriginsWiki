Celestial Starlight

[statblock Celestial_Starlight]

The Celestial Starlight is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] crafted using a [link Celestial Magnet | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Celestial_Magnet] and [link Dim Starlight]. Equipping it increases the [link pickup range | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Items#Pickup] for [link Stars | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Star] and causes them to generate from [link critical hits | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Critical_hit]. It also provides a source of light brighter than its original.

Tips

- The Celestial Starlight is very beneficial when the player reforges their equipment and weapons around critical strike chance, so mana stars can fall as often as possible.
- The best weapon to use with the Celestial Starlight is the [link Laser Tag Gun] for its 50% chance for critical hits.
