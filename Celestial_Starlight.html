Celestial Starlight

[statblock Celestial_Starlight]

The Celestial Starlight is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] crafted combining a Celestial Magnet and [link Dim Starlight]. Equipping it increases the pick-up range for stars and causes them to generate from critical hits. It also provides a source of light brighter than its original.
