Defiled Ekko

[statblock Defiled_Ekko]

The Defiled Ekko is an [link enemy | | Enemies.html] found in the [link Defiled Wastelands] all throughout the game after the player has reached 400 max [link life | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Health]. Defiled Ekkoes follow the behaviors of [link Town NPCs | | NPCs.html], except with hostility towards the player and various weapons they could use. Defiled Ekkoes can use their own version of the [link Infusion], [link Dismay] and [link Bone Latcher] that each scale with difficulty. In [link Master Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Master_Mode], Defiled Ekkoes gain the benefits of [link Mysterious Spray] once [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] is reached.

Tips

- The best way to handle Defiled Ekkoes is to build barriers to avoid them if possible, however, if a confrontation is necessary, use [link explosives | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] to make haste of them. Like most Defiled enemies, they are prone to high bursts of damage.

Trivia

- The [link Bestiary | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bestiary] entry for the Defiled Ekko: "An attempt for the Defiled to copy the abilities of the Terrarian, this being serves as a huge threat to anything that is cleasning the Defiled."
