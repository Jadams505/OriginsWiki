The Cybernetic Brain is an [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that is craftable using post-Moonlord materials. The Cybernetic Brain acts like an alternative to the Ankh Shield providing immunity to a lot of debuffs, but comes with the added benefit of increasing mana regeneration.
