Strange Feather

[statblock Strange_Feather]

The Strange Feather is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that has a 1/60 (1.67%) chance of being dropped by a Harpy. Equipping it will turn the player into a [link Half-Harpy | | Strange_Feather.html] when in Space. While in the Half-Harpy form the player has infinite flight and a slow fall while holding an item. The Strange Feather is a really strong alternative to wings as not only does the player have infinite flight, but also a really fast horizontal speed whilst flying.
