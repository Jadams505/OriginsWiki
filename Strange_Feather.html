Strange Feather

The Strange Feather is a Hardmode accessory that has a 1/60 (1.67%) chance of being dropped by a Harpy. Equipping it will turn the player into a Half-Harpy when in Space. While in the Half-Harpy form the player has infinite flight and a slow fall while holding an item. The Strange Feather is a really strong alternative to wings as not only does the player have infinite flight, but also a really fast horizontal speed whilst flying.
