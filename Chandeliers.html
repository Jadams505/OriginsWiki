Chandeliers

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla chandeliers, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Chandeliers].

[statblock Chandeliers]

Chandeliers are a type of [link furniture] and [link light source | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Light_sources] that emits light when hung from the ceiling. Chandeliers take up a 3x3-tile area of wall space and can be turned on and off using [link mechanisms | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Mechanisms].
