Sonar Visor

[statblock Sonar_Visor]

The Sonar Visor is a Hardmode post-Plantera [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] crafted using an Ultrabright Helmet and [link Advanced Imaging]. It provides greater visibility in the dark by highlighting tiles nearby the player. It grants a unique visual effect when worn.
