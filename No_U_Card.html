No U Card

[statblock No_U_Card]

The No U Card is a [link Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] immunity [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] crafted using an Ankh Charm and a [link Return To Sender]. It retaliates against enemies that have struck the player, returning 50% of damage recieved and applies any debuffs that would've applied to the player. Additionally, it grants the player immunity to the following debuffs:
Bleeding
Broken Armor
Confused
Cursed
Darkness
Poisoned
[link Rasterized | §ModImage§/Buffs/Rasterized_Debuff.png]
Silenced
Slow
Stoned
Weak

Trivia

- The No U Card is inspired by the internet joke of rearranging the letters of an Uno card, the No U card essentially behaving like a glorified Reverse card.
