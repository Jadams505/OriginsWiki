The Mass Blade is a post-mechanical melee weapon that is build using a random variety of materials. It is a giant and powerful clubbing weapon that requires the plater to charge up before attacking with it. The velocity at which the attack dashes and how far the player will go depends on the time spent charging the attack. During the dash, the player character will club any nearby enemies.
