Gooey Water

Gooey Water is a consumable weapon that can be crafted using Bottled Water, Riven Seeds and Silica. It spreads a small area of Riven when thrown and deals slight damage to any enemies it lands on. It and its equivalents count as generic damage and only receive bonuses from any generic boosts.
