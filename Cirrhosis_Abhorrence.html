Cirrhosis' Abhorrence

The Cirrhosis' Abhorrence is a pre-Hardmode accessory combining a Lousy Liver with a Messy Magma Leech. While equipped, 5 of the closest enemies to the player will have be constantly afflicted by the Bleeding, On Fire! and Rasterized.
