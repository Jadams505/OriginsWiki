Cirrhosis' Abhorrence

[statblock Cirrhosis_Abhorrence]

The Cirrhosis' Abhorrence is a Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] combining a [link Lousy Liver] with a [link Messy Magma Leech]. While equipped, 5 of the closest enemies to the player will have their stats reduced while being constantly afflicted by the Bleeding and On Fire! debuffs.
