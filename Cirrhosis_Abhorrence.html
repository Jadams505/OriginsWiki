Cirrhosis' Abhorrence

[statblock Cirrhosis_Abhorrence]

The Cirrhosis' Abhorrence is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] combining a [link Lousy Liver] with a [link Messy Magma Leech]. While equipped, 5 of the closest enemies to the player will have be constantly afflicted by the Bleeding, On Fire! and [link Rasterized].
