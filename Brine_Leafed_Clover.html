Brine-Leafed Clover

[statblock Brine_Leafed_Clover_4]

The Brine-Leafed Clover is a [link Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] found on [link Peat Moss] in the [link Brine Pool]. While equipped, it has a chance to improve the player's odds of obtaining rarer drops from enemies. The clover's sprite will change as leaves are removed whenever it has caused a rare drop to fall.
