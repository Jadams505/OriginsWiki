The Bomb Handling Device is an [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] found in Gold Chests in the Dungeon. The BHD increases the throwing velocity of [link Demolitionist Weapons] by 50% making it easier to reach further-away enemies due to the gravity most of these weapons experience.

Notes

- As well as being nicknamed the "Bomb Yeeter", it also adopts the acronym "BHD" for simplicity.
