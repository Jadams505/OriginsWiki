The Carpalfish is an aqautic enemy that can be found deeper into the [link Brine Pool]. Carpalfish will swim around slowly similar to a Goldfish until the player is nearby. When this is the case, it will quickly charge towards the player's general direction and not stop until it collides with a tile. Afterwards, it will continue its previous behavior.

Notes

- Carpalfish do not flop around on land.

- Unlike any other aquatic enemy, Carpalfish still charge at the player when outside of water. They can even track the player through walls.

Tips

- Carpalfish will attack from further away if the player has a light source nearby them, stay in the dark.

Trivia

- Carpal means "bone". So, Carpalfish can also be known as "Bonefish".
