Carpalfish

[statblock Carpalfish]

The Carpalfish is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link enemy | | Enemies.html] that spawns in the [link Brine Pool] at any time in the game. It will swim like most fish and charge at the player at a fast speed after building-up the attack. It will charge at the player even if separated by a wall as long as the player is submerged in [link water | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Water].
