ACME Crate

[statblock ACME_Crate]

The ACME Crate is a Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] created using a [link Magic Tripwire] and a [link Nitro Crate]. Equipping the crate increases [link explosive | $default | Demolitionist_Weapons.hmtl] blast radii by 120% and causes them to explode when an enemy is within proximity of the blast.

Notes

- Explosives will not detonate while this is equipped only if there is no enemy nearby it as well.

Tips

- The ACME Crate is very dangerous for the player to use due to the greatly increased blast radius it provides for explosives, increasing the likelihood of the player receiving [link self-damage | $default | Self_Damage.html]. Due to this factor, it is advised players have some form of self-damage resistance if they lack the evasiveness to avoid their own explosives.

Trivia

- The ACME Crate is inspired by the fictional entity in Road Runner/Wile E. Coyote cartoon known as the ACME Corporation. Their products tend to have an explosive factor and a likelihood to pre-maturely detonate, hence the name for this item.
