ACME Crate

[statblock ACME_Crate]

The ACME Crate is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] created using [link Magic Tripwire] and a [link Nitro Crate]. Equipping the crate increases [link explosive | | Demolitionist_Weapons.hmtl] blast radii by 60% and causes them to explode when an enemy is within proximity of the blast.

Tips

- The ACME Crate is very dangerous for the player to use due to the greatly increased blast radius it provides for explosives, increasing the likelihood of the player receiving [link self-damage | | Self_Damage.html]. Due to this factor, it is adviseable players have some form of self-damage resistance if they lack the evasiveness to avoid their own explosives.

Trivia

- The ACME Crate is inspired by the fictional entity in Road Runner/Wile E. Coyote cartoon known as the [link ACME Corporation | | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Acme_Corporation]. Their products tend to have an explosive factor and a likelihood to pre-maturely detonate, hence the name for this item.
