It fires faster than the S.D.M.G. and [link Chain Gun | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Chain_Gun] dealing higher damage whilst also having 70% chance [link not to consume ammo | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Ammo_conservation]. Additionally, [link bullets] fired by this weapon inflict the [link Wet | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Wet] debuff.

Its best [link modifier | | Modifiers.html] is Unreal.
