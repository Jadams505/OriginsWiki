L.W.M.U.G.

The L.W.M.U.G. is a Hardmode post-Moon Lord gun that is an upgrade of the S.D.M.G.. It fires faster than the S.D.M.G. and Chain Gun dealing higher damage whilst also having 70% chance not to consume ammo. Additionally, bullets fired by this weapon inflict the Wet debuff.

Its best modifier is Unreal.
