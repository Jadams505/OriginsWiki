L.W.M.U.G.

[statblock LWMUG]

The L.W.M.U.G. is a Hardmode post-Moon Lord [link gun | $default | Guns.html] that is an upgrade of the S.D.M.G.. It fires faster than the S.D.M.G. and Chain Gun dealing higher damage whilst also having 70% chance not to consume ammo. Additionally, [link bullets] fired by this weapon inflict the Wet debuff.

Its best [link modifier | $default | Modifiers.html] is Unreal.
