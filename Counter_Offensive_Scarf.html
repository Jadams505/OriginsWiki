Counter-offensive Scarf

[statblock Counter_Offensive_Scarf]

The Counter-offensive Scarf is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode], [link Expert Mode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Expert_Mode]-exclusive [link accessory | | Accessories.html] combining the [link Worm Scarf | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Worm_Scarf] and [link Comb]. While equipped, the player with have up to +8% damage when enemies get close and up to 15% [link damage reduction | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Damage_reduction] when enemies are far away from the player.
