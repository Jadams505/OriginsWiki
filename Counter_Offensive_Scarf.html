Counter-offensive Scarf

[statblock Counter_Offensive_Scarf]

The Counter-offensive Scarf is a pre-Hardmode, Expert Mode-exclusive accessory combining the Worm Scarf and Comb. While equipped, the player with have up to +8% damage when enemies get close and up to 15% damage reduction when enemies are far away from the player.
