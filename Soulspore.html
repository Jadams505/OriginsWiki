Soulspore

[statblock Soulspore]

The Soulspore is a plant grown on Defiled Grass or Defiled Jungle Grass blocks found in the Defiled Wastelands. It is the Defiled-equivalent to the Corruption's Vile Mushroom and the Crimson's Vicious Mushroom.
