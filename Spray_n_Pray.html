Spray 'n Pray

[statblock Spray_n_Pray]

The Spray 'n Pray is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link gun | | Guns.html] sold by the [link Painter | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Painter] [link NPC | | NPCs.html] after selling him 25 [link Paintings | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Paintings] found in the world. it uses [link Paints | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Paints] as a source of [link ammunition] and fires a colored stream of paint depending on the color of paint used from the player's inventory. It fires rapidly and has a 60% chance [link not to consume ammo | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Ammo_conservation].

The Spray 'n Pray also doubles as an effective painting tool to color tiles due to its range and speed. Furthermore, it has the ability to paint enemies the selected color too.

Its best [link modifier | | Modifiers.html] is Unreal.
