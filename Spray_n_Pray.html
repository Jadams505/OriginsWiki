Spray 'n Pray

[statblock Spray_n_Pray]

The Spray 'n Pray is a Hardmode [link gun | $default | Guns.html] sold by the Painter after selling him 25 Paintings found in the world. it uses Paints as a source of [link ammunition] and fires a colored stream of paint depending on the color of paint used from the player's inventory. It fires rapidly and has a 60% chance not to consume ammo.

The Spray 'n Pray also doubles as an effective painting tool to color tiles due to its range and speed. Furthermore, it has the ability to paint enemies the selected color too.

Its best [link modifier | $default | Modifiers.html] is Unreal.
