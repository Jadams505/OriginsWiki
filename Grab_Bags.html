Grab bags

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla grab bags, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Grab_bags].

Grab bags are [link consumable | | Consumables.html] items that contain loot chosen at random when they are opened. They can be opened from within a player's inventory by pressing the Open/Activate button on their inventory icons.
