Resizable Mine Mk. 5

[statblock Resizable_Mine_Five]

The Resizable Mine Mk. 5 is a type of Hardmode mine used as [link ammunition] for [link mine launchers | $default | Mine_Launchers.html] and the Proximity Mine Launcher. Unlike other ammo types, most of the damage from mine launchers comes from the mines themselves.
