Resizable Mine Mk. 5

The Resizable Mine Mk. 5 is a type of Hardmode, post-Moon Lord canister used as ammunition for launchers and the Proximity Mine Launcher. All resizable mines remain in place until their the duration of their fuse-time or when touched by an enemy.
