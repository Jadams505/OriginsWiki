Stone Mask

[statblock Stone_Mask]

The Stone Mask is a [link pre-Hardmode | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that can be found anywhere on the surface of the world as a tile. Only one Stone Mask spawns in a world unless in some world seeds. It increases the player's defense by 8, but decreases the player's movement speed by 10% and their jump speed by 33%.

The Stone Mask is a [link lore item | $default | Lore_Item.html] indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."
