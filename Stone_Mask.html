Stone Mask

[statblock Stone_Mask]

The Stone Mask is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that can be found anywhere on the [link surface | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Layers#Surface] of the world as a tile. Only one Stone Mask spawns in a world unless in some [link world seeds | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Secret_world_seeds]. It increases the player's defense by 8, but decreases the player's movement speed by 10% and their jump speed by 33%.

The Stone Mask is a [link lore item | | Lore_Item.html] indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."
