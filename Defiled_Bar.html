Defiled Bars are craftable from [link Lost Ore] and dropped by the [link Defiled Amalgamation]. They are used to crafted a lot of Defiled-themed content and are the Defiled equivalent of the Demonite Bar, Crimtane Bar, and [link Infested Bar].

Properties and Lore

Defiled Bar

Defiled Bars are not a metal material, they are just spare Defiled chunks the Terrarian can use for crafting Defiled equipment.
