Toxic Shock

Toxic Shock is a debuff that is applied to a player whenever they enter the water at the Brine Pool with protection. Toxic Shock can also be applied by enemies and items found in the Brine Pool as well. When an afflicted NPC or player has Toxic Shock they will lose 8 health, have a 1/6 (16.7%) chance to be stunned, have their defense reduced by 20%, and recieve 30% more damage from critical hits for every second it is applied. Visually, afflicted NPCs and players will have dark green splotches scroll across their sprites.
