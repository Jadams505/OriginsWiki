Toxic Shock

[statblock Toxic_Shock]

Toxic Shock is a [link debuff | | Debuffs.html] that is applied to a player whenever they enter the [link water | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Water] at the [link Brine Pool] with protection. Toxic Shock can also be applied by [link enemies] and items found in the Brine Pool as well. When an afflicted NPC or player has Toxic Shock they will lose 8 health, have a 1/6 (16.7%) chance to be [link stunned | | Stun.html] and have their [link defense | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Defense] reduced by 20% for every second it is applied. Visually, afflicted NPCs and players will have dark green splotches scroll across their sprites.
