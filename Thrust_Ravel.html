Thrust Ravel

[statblock Thrust_Ravel]

Thrust Ravel is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode], post-[link Plantera | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Plantera] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is crafted using the [link Hoverboard | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hoverboard] and [link Ravel]. Thrust Ravel turns the player into a small sphere that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Thrust Ravel also allows the player to fly at a decent speed and for a longer duration than typical [link wings].
