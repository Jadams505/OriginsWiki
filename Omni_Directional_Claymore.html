Omni-directional Claymore

The Omni-directional Claymore is a post-Mech, mine-like explosive crafted using mech components dropped by the mech bosses. When placed the claymore emits an 8-tile long laser beam that can be redirected that will cause the mine to explode when an enemy crosses it.
