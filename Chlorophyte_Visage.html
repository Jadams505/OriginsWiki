Chlorophyte Visage

[statblock Chlorophyte_Visage]

The Chlorophyte Visage is a helmet [link armor] piece to serve as the [link Demolitionist | $default | Demolitionist_Weapons.html]-equivalent for [link Chlorophyte armor | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Chlorophyte_armor]. Equipping this as part of a full set does not change the set bonus provided and only offers basic combat stats.

The Chlorophyte Visage provides the following benefits:

- +5 [link defense | $default | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Defense]
- +35% explosive blast radius
- -25% explosive [link fuse-time | $default | Fuse_Time.html]
