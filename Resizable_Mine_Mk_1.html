Resizable Mine Mk. 1

The Resizable Mine Mk. 1 is a type of pre-Hardmode canister used as ammunition for launchers and the Proximity Mine Launcher. All resizable mines remain in place until their the duration of their fuse-time or when touched by an enemy.
