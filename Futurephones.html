Futurephones

Futurephones are an Hardmode, post-Mechanical accessory combining Headphones and Sniper's Mark. While equipped, all attacks (other than from a minion) will be able to tag enemies. Additionally, generic damage is increased by 5% and generic critical strike chances by 10% against tagged enemies.

Crafting

Recipes

{ stations:[ `Tinkerer's Workshop` ], items:[ { result:'Futurephones', ingredients:[ 'Headphones', `Sniper's Mark` ] } ] }
Notes

- Futurephone's bonuses are multiplicative and applied after every other boost the player has in stats since it applies to enemies that are tagged.
