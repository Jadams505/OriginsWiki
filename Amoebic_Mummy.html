Amoebic Mummy

The Amoebic Mummy is a Hardmode enemy that spawns in Deserts overrun by the Riven. The Amoebic Mummy approaches the player and behaves like its other counterparts.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Amoebic Mummy: "It was only a matter of time before the Riven got to the body. It now wanders aimlessly in the Rivenated deserts in search of new hosts."
