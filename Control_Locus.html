Control Locus

[statblock Control_Locus]

The Control Locus is a Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] that combines the [link Stack of Shraps] and [link Weakpoint Analyzer]. While equipped, all ranged and [link explosive | $default | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] projectiles are duplicated 7 times and gain a 20% increased ammo conservation rate. Creating the Control Locus will require a world with access to either the Shimmer or both the Crimson and [link Ashen | $default | Ashen_Factory.html].
