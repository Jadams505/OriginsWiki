Control Locus

The Control Locus is a pre-Hardmode, Master Mode-exclusive accessory that combines the Stack of Shraps and Weakpoint Analyzer. While equipped, all ranged projectiles are duplicated 5 times and all explosive projectiles are only duplicated twice, and gain a 20% increased ammo conservation rate.
