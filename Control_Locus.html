Control Locus

[statblock Control_Locus]

The Control Locus is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that combines the [link Stack of Shraps] and [link Weakpoint Analyzer]. While equipped, all ranged and [link explosive | | Demolitionist_Weapons.html] projectiles are duplicated 7 times and gain a 20% increased [link ammo conservation | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Ammo_conservation] rate.
