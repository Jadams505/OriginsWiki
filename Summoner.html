Summoner Class

The Summoner Class is one of the few class avaiable in the game whose characteristics are dependent on having a summonable minion to
fight alongside. Summoners usually need to acquire a separate summoning item in order to have minions, but not always. Origins attempts to
give the Summoner class a lot of attention to help it become more viable alongside the other classes.

Minions

Minions are creatures summoned by Summoners to fight for them. Minions can be stackable depending on the minion slots available to the player.
