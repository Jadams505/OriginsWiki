Ninja Ravel

[statblock Ninja_Ravel]

Ninja Ravel is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that combines [link Spider Ravel] and [link Stealth Ravel]. Ninja Ravel turns the player into a small sphere that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Ninja Ravel allows the player to latch onto walls and ceilings by pressing the corresponding key whereas Move Left is used to remain attached to a wall directly left of the player or Up is used to remain attached to a ceiling the player can touch. On top of that the player's [link aggro] is reduced by 400 while ravelled.
