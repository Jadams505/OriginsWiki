Magic Glove

The Magic Glove is a Hardmode accessory combining a Mana Cloak and a Gun Glove. Swinging while this is equipped will create a random magic projectile from the player's swinging-hand. It also provides an 8% reduction in mana consumption and allows the player to automatically consume mana-restoring potions in the their inventory.
