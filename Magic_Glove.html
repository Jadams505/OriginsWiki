Magic Glove

[statblock Magic_Glove]

The Magic Glove is a Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] combining a Mana Cloak and a [link Gun Glove]. Swinging while this is equipped will create a random magic projectile from the player's swinging-hand.

Possible projectiles spawned:
- Amber Bolt
- Bee Gun
- Demon Scythe
- Magic Missile
- Orange Zapinator
- Water Bolt
- Wand of Sparking

Tips

- Like the [link Gun Glove], the Magic Glove benefits from melee speed boosts.
