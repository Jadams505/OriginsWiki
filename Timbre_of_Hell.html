Timbre of Hell

[statblock Timbre_Hell]

Timbre of Hell is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that has a 1/48 (2.08%) chance of being dropped by a Demon. While equipped and when the player is afflicted with the On Fire! debuff, all damage from player-inflicted debuffs is multiplied by 3 besides the On Fire! debuff, which instead has its damage multiplied by 6.

Timbre of Hell is a [link lore item | | Lore_Item.html] indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."
