Trap Charm

[statblock Trap_Charm]

The Trap Charm is a Pre-Hardmode [link accessory | $default | Accessories.html] found in Underground Deadman's Chests. It protects the player by halving all damage recieved from traps and granting immunity to the Poisoned debuff applied by traps.
