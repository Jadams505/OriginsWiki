Candelabras

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla candelabras, click [link here | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Candelabras].

[statblock Candelabras]

Candelabras are a type of [link furniture] and [link light source | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Light_sources] that emits light when placed on a flat surface such as [link blocks], [link platforms] and flat furniture items. They are not destroyed by [link water | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Water].
