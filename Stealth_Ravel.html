Stealth Ravel

[statblock Stealth_Ravel]

Stealth Ravel is a [link Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that is crafted using [link Putrid Scent | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Putrid_Scent] and [link Ravel]. Stealth Ravel turns the player into a small sphere that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. The player's [link aggro] is reduced by 400 while ravelled and this is equipped.
