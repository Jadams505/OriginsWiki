Traffic Cone

[statblock Traffic_Cone]

The Traffic Cone is a small [link furniture] item that is dropped by [link Conehead Zombies | | Conehead_Zombie.html]. It can also be crafted using [link Rubber] at a [link Heavy Work Bench | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Heavy_Work_Bench]. When placed, any players or [link enemies] within a 30-tile radius of the cone will be constantly inflicted with the [link Slow | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Slow] debuff.
