Explosive Artery

[statblock Explosive_Artery]

The Explosive Artery is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that has a 1/87 (1.15%) chance of being dropped by a [link Blood Crawler | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Blood_Crawler] or a [link Face Monster | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Face_Monster]. While equipped, enemies inflicted with [link Bleeding | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Bleeding] within 32 tiles of the player have an 8% chance to explode every second.
