Explosive Artery

[statblock Explosive_Artery]

The Explosive Artery is a [link pre-Hardmode | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Pre-Hardmode] [link accessory | | Accessories.html] that has a 1/87 (1.15%) chance of being dropped by a Blood Crawler or a Face Monster. While equipped, enemies inflicted with Bleeding within 32 tiles of the player have an 8% chance to explode every second.
